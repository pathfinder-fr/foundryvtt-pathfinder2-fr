The squirming swill retains some residue from the magical broth it was stewed in. This liquid can be used as an oil or potion of the swill's level or lower, typically a @Compendium[pf2e.equipment-srd.2RuepCemJhrpKKao]{Healing Potion (Minor)},@Compendium[pf2e.equipment-srd.ukTlC4G83aVQEg7u]{Nectar of Purification}, or @Compendium[pf2e.equipment-srd.j2CHumvbjmlLQX2i]{Oil of Potency}.
