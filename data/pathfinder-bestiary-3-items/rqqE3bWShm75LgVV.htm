Parent Name: Nosferatu Malefactor Name: Dominate Nom: Domincation État: libre ------ Description (en) ------

The nosferatu can cast @Compendium[pf2e.spells-srd.OsOhx3TGIZ7AhD0P]{Dominate} at will as a divine innate spell. Casting it requires staring into the target's eyes, giving the spell the visual trait. The save DC is 29, and a creature that succeeds is temporarily immune to that nosferatu's Dominate for 24 hours.

Fully destroying the nosferatu ends the domination, but merely reducing the nosferatu to 0 HP is insufficient to break the spell.
