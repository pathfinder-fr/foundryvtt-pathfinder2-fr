Parent Name: Water Wisp Name: Drench Nom: Tremper État: libre ------ Description (en) ------

The wisp puts out all fires in a single 5-foot square.

It extinguishes non-magical fire of that size or smaller automatically and attempts to counteract magical fires (counteract modifier [[/r 1d20+6]]).
