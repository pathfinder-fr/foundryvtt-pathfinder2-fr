Name: Effect: Major Battle Curse Nom: Effet : Malédiction majeure du combat État: libre ------ Description (en) ------

Your body proves capable of truly heroic feats, but doing so interferes with your mental focus.

You take a -2 status penalty to AC and saving throws, and are @Compendium[pf2e.conditionitems.e1XGnhKNSQIm5IXg]{Stupefied 2}.

You gain a +6 status bonus to weapon and unarmed damage rolls, and a +1 status bonus to weapon and unarmed attack rolls.

You also gain fast healing equal to your level while in a non-trivial combat encounter.

Note: You need to apply the stupefied condition and fast healing manually. The remaining effects are applied automatically.
