Name: Telepathic Demand Nom: Ordre télépathique État: officielle ------ Data ------ RangeEN: planetary RangeFR: planétaire TargetEN: 1 creature you've telepathically contacted before TargetFR: 1 créature que vous avez déjà contacté télépathiquement auparavant ------ Description (en) ------

You send the target a message of 25 words or fewer, and it can respond immediately with its own message of 25 words or fewer. Your message is insidious and has the effect of @Compendium[pf2e.spells-srd.qwlh6aDgi86U3Q7H]{Suggestion}, with the message substituting for the spoken suggestion. On a successful save, the target is temporarily immune for 1 day, and on a critical success, the target is temporarily immune for 1 month. You can target a creature only if you have previously been in telepathic contact with it before, such as via the telepathy spell.
