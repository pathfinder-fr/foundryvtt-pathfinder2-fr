Name: Tortoise and the Hare Nom: La tortue et le lièvre État: libre ------ Data ------ RangeEN: 30 feet RangeFR: 9 m TargetEN: 1 foe, and either yourself or 1 ally TargetFR: 1 ennemi et soit vous, soit 1 allié ------ Description (en) ------

You prove that slow and steady doesn't always win the race by turning a foe into a proverbial tortoise and yourself or an ally into a hare, siphoning some of the foe's speed and granting it to the other target. The effects depend on the foe's Fortitude save.

Critical Success The targets are unaffected.

Success The foe is @Compendium[pf2e.conditionitems.xYTAsEpcJE1Ccni3]{Slowed 1} for 1 round, and the other target is unaffected.

Failure The foe is slowed 1 for 3 rounds and the other target is @Compendium[pf2e.conditionitems.nlCjDvLMf2EkV2dl]{Quickened} for 1 round. It can use the extra action to Step, Stride, or Strike.

Critical Failure The foe is slowed 1 for 3 minutes and the other target is quickened for 1 minute. It can use the extra action to Step, Stride, or Strike.
