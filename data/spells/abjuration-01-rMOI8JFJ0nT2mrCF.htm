Name: Phase Familiar Nom: Déphasage de familier État: libre ------ Data ------ RangeEN: 60 feet RangeFR: 18 m TargetEN: your familiar TargetFR: votre familier ------ Description (en) ------

Trigger Your familiar would take damage.

Effect You draw upon your patron's power to momentarily shift your familiar from its solid, physical form into an ephemeral version of itself shaped of mist. Your familiar gains resistance 5 to all damage and is immune to precision damage. These apply only against the triggering damage.

Heightened (+1) Increase the resistance by 2.
