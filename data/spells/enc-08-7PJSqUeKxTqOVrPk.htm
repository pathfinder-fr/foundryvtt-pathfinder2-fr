Name: Power Word Stun Nom: Mot de pouvoir étourdissant État: officielle ------ Description (en) ------

You stun the target with an arcane word of power. Once targeted, the target is then temporarily immune for 10 minutes. The effect of the spell depends on the target's level. 13th or Lower The target is @Compendium[pf2e.conditionitems.dfCMdR4wnpbYNTix]{Stunned} for 1d6 rounds. 14th-15th The target is Stunned for 1 round. 16th or Higher The target is Stunned 1.

Heightened (+1) The levels at which each outcome applies increase by 2.
