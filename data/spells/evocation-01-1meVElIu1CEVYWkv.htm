Name: Noxious Vapors Nom: Vapeurs nocives État: libre ------ Description (en) ------

You emit a cloud of toxic smoke that temporarily obscures you from sight. Each creature except you in the area when you @Compendium[pf2e.actionspf2e.aBQ8ajvEBByv45yz]{Cast the Spell} takes [[/r {1d6}[poison]]]{1d6 poison damage} (basic Fortitude save). A creature that critically fails the saving throw also becomes @Compendium[pf2e.conditionitems.fesd1n5eVhpCSS18]{Sickened 1}. All creatures in the area become @Compendium[pf2e.conditionitems.DmAIPqOBomZ7H95W]{Concealed}, and all creatures outside the smoke become Concealed to creatures within it. This smoke can be dispersed by a strong wind.

Heightened (+1) The damage increases by [[/r {1d6}[poison]]]{1d6 poison}.
