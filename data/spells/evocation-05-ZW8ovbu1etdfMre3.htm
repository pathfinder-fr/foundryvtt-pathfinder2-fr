Name: Acid Storm Nom: Tempête d'acide État: libre ------ Data ------ AreasizeEN: 20-foot-radius burst AreasizeFR: explosion de 6 mètres de rayon RangeEN: 120 feet RangeFR: 36 mètres ------ Description (en) ------

You evoke a storm of acid rain that pelts the area for the spell's duration. A creature that begins its turn in the area takes 3d8 acid damage.

Heightened (+2) The damage increases by 1d8.
