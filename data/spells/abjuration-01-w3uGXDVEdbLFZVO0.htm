Name: Angelic Halo Nom: Halo angélique État: officielle ------ Data ------ AreasizeEN: 15-foot emanation AreasizeFR: émanation de 4,5 mètres ------ Description (en) ------

You gain an angelic halo with an aura that increases allies' healing from the @Compendium[pf2e.spells-srd.rfZpqmj0AIIdkVIs]{Heal} spell. Allies in your halo's emanation who are healed by a heal spell gain a status bonus to Hit Points regained equal to double the heal spell's level.
