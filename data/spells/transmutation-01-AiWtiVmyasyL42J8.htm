Name: Crushing Ground Nom: Sol écrasant État: libre ------ Data ------ RangeEN: 60 feet RangeFR: 18 m TargetEN: 1 creature TargetFR: 1 créature ------ Description (en) ------

You tear open the ground then slam it shut. The target creature takes 2d6 bludgeoning damage with a Reflex save.

Critical Success The target is unaffected.

Success The target takes half damage.

Failure The target takes full damage, is @Compendium[pf2e.conditionitems.AJh5ex99aV6VTggg]{Flat-Footed}, and takes a -10-foot circumstance penalty to Speed.

Critical Failure The target takes double damage and is flat-footed and @Compendium[pf2e.conditionitems.eIcWbB5o3pP6OIMe]{Immobilized}. It can attempt to Escape against your spell DC. If it doesn't Escape, the target takes an additional 2d6 bludgeoning damage when the spell ends.

Heightened (+1) Increase the initial damage and additional damage by 2d6.
