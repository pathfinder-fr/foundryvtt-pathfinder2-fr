Success The foe is unaffected.

Failure The foe is @Compendium[pf2e.conditionitems.TBSHQspnbcqxsmjL]{Frightened 1}.

Critical Failure The foe is Frightened 2.

Heightened (5th) Foes in the area are Frightened 1 on a success, Frightened 2 on a failure, and Frightened 3 and @Compendium[pf2e.conditionitems.sDPxOjQ9kx2RZE8D]{Fleeing} for 1 round on a critical failure. They are still unaffected on a critical success.
