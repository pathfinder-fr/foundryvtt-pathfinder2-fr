Name: You're Mine Nom: Tu es à moi État: officielle ------ Description (en) ------

You manipulate the target's emotions, potentially allowing you to control it for a brief instant. The target must attempt a Will save.

Critical Success The target is unaffected.

Success The target is @Compendium[pf2e.conditionspf2e.dfCMdR4wnpbYNTix]{Stunned} 1.

Failure On the target's next turn, it's @Compendium[pf2e.conditionspf2e.dfCMdR4wnpbYNTix]{Stunned} 1 and you partially control it, causing it to take a single action of your choice. If it has actions left, it can act normally.

Critical Failure The target is @Compendium[pf2e.conditionspf2e.9qGBRpbX9NEwtAAr]{Controlled} for 1 round.

Heightened (7th) On a failure, the target is @Compendium[pf2e.conditionspf2e.9qGBRpbX9NEwtAAr]{Controlled} for 1 round. On a critical failure, the target is @Compendium[pf2e.conditionspf2e.9qGBRpbX9NEwtAAr]{Controlled} for up to 1 minute; it receives a new Will save at the end of each of its turns, and on a success, the spell ends.
