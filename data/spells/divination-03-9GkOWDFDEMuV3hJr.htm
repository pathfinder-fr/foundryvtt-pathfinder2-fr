Name: Familiar's Face Nom: Visage du familier État: libre ------ Data ------ RangeEN: 1 mile RangeFR: 1,5 km TargetEN: 1 willing creature that is your animal companion or familiar TargetFR: 1 créature consentante qui est votre compagnon animal ou votre familier ------ Description (en) ------

The target becomes a scrying sensor, allowing you to see through its eyes, smell what it smells, and similarly use its other senses. If you can cast a spell with the revelation trait that affects your senses, such as @Compendium[pf2e.spells-srd.jwK43yKsHTkJQvQ9]{See Invisibility}, while this spell is active, you gain the benefit of the spell through the target's senses instead of your own. You can also speak through the target, with a voice much like yours, though it takes on some of the timbre and character of the target's growls or squawks. You can use @Compendium[pf2e.actionspf2e.q9nbyIF0PEBqMtYe]{Command an Animal} on the target as part of @Compendium[pf2e.actionspf2e.3f5DMFu8fPiqHpRg]{Sustaining this Spell}. You don't need line of sight or line of effect to your target when you @Compendium[pf2e.actionspf2e.aBQ8ajvEBByv45yz]{Cast this Spell}.
