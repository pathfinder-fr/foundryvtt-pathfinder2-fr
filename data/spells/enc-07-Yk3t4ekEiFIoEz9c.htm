Name: Power Word Blind Nom: État: aucune ------ Description (en) ------

You utter an arcane word of power that can make the target @JournalEntry[Blinded] upon hearing it. Once targeted, the target is then temporarily immune for 10 minutes. The effect of the spell depends on the target's level.

11th or Lower The target is @JournalEntry[Blinded] permanently.

12th–13th The target is @JournalEntry[Blinded] for 1d4 minutes.

14th or Higher The target is @JournalEntry[Dazzled] for 1 minute.

Heightened (+1) The levels at which each outcome applies increase by 2.
