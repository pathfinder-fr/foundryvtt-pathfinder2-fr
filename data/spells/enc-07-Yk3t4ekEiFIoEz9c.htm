12th–13th The target is @Compendium[pf2e.conditionspf2e.XgEqL1kFApUbl5Z2]{Blinded} for 1d4 minutes.

14th or Higher The target is @Compendium[pf2e.conditionspf2e.TkIyaNPgTZFBCCuh]{Dazzled} for 1 minute.
