Name: Feeblemind Nom: Débilité État: officielle ------ Description (en) ------

You drastically reduce the target's mental faculties. The target must attempt a Will save.

Critical Success The target is unaffected.

Success The target is @Compendium[pf2e.conditionspf2e.e1XGnhKNSQIm5IXg]{Stupefied} 2 for 1 round.

Failure The target is @Compendium[pf2e.conditionspf2e.e1XGnhKNSQIm5IXg]{Stupefied} 4 with an unlimited duration.

Critical Failure The target’s intellect is permanently reduced below that of an animal, and it treats its Charisma, Intelligence, and Wisdom modifiers as –5. It loses all class abilities that require mental faculties, including all spellcasting. If the target is a PC, they become an NPC under the GM’s control.

------ Description (fr) ------

Vous réduisez drastiquement les facultés mentales de la cible. La cible doit faire un jet de Volonté.

Succès critique La cible n’est pas affectée.

Succès La créature est @Compendium[pf2e.conditionspf2e.e1XGnhKNSQIm5IXg]{stupéfiée} 2 pendant 1 round.
