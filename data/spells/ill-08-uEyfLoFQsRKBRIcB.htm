A field of cascading, ever-changing colors manifests in the air. Creatures are @JournalEntry[Dazzled] while inside the pattern, as are those within 20 feet of the pattern's area. A creature must attempt a Will save if it is inside the pattern when you cast it, enters the pattern, ends its turn within the pattern, or uses a Seek or Interact action on the pattern. A creature currently affected by the pattern doesn't need to attempt new saves.

Success The creature is unaffected.

Failure The creature is @JournalEntry[Confused] for 1d4 rounds.

Critical Failure The creature is @JournalEntry[Stunned] for 1d4 rounds. After the @JournalEntry[Stunned] condition ends, the creature is @JournalEntry[Confused] for the remaining duration of the spell.
