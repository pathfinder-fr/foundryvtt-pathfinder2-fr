Name: Foresight Nom: Prémonition État: officielle ------ Data ------ RangeEN: touch RangeFR: contact TargetEN: 1 creature TargetFR: 1 créature ------ Description (en) ------

You gain a sixth sense that warns you of danger that might befall the target of the spell. If you choose a creature other than yourself as the target, you create a psychic link through which you can inform the target of danger. This link is a mental effect. Due to the amount of information this spell requires you to process, you can't have more than one foresight spell in effect at a time. Casting foresight again ends the previous foresight. While foresight is in effect, the target gains a +2 status bonus to initiative rolls and isn't @Compendium[pf2e.conditionitems.AJh5ex99aV6VTggg]{Flat-Footed} against @Compendium[pf2e.conditionitems.VRSef5y1LmL2Hkjf]{Undetected} creatures or when flanked. In addition, you gain the following reaction: @Compendium[pf2e.actionspf2e.VOEWhPQfN3lvHivK]{Foresight}

------ Description (fr) ------

Vous obtenez un sixième sens qui vous avertit de tout danger susceptible de s’abattre sur la cible du sort.

En raison du nombre d’informations que ce sort vous demande de traiter, vous ne pouvez avoir qu’un seul sort de Prémonition actif à la fois. Si vous lancez le sort de nouveau, la précédente incantation se termine.
