Name: Soul Siphon Nom: Siphon d'âme État: libre ------ Data ------ RangeEN: 30 feet RangeFR: 9 mètres TargetEN: 1 living creature TargetFR: 1 créature vivante ------ Description (en) ------

You siphon off a portion of the target's soul to bolster your own vitality. The target must attempt a Fortitude save or take 1d4 negative damage. You gain temporary Hit Points equal to the total amount of HP the target loses, including the HP lost from becoming @Compendium[pf2e.conditionitems.4D2KBtexWXa6oUMR]{Drained}.

Critical Success The creature is unaffected.

Success The creature takes half damage.

Failure The creature takes full damage and becomes @Compendium[pf2e.conditionitems.4D2KBtexWXa6oUMR]{Drained 1}.

Critical Failure The creature takes double damage and becomes @Compendium[pf2e.conditionitems.4D2KBtexWXa6oUMR]{Drained 2}.

Heightened (+1) Increase the damage by 1d4.
