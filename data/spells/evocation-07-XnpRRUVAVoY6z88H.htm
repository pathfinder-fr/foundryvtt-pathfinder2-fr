You draw upon surrounding magical energy to empower a ley line, enhancing both its positive and negative effects.

The duration of an empowered ley line's benefits increases: when you successfully @Compendium[pf2e.actionspf2e.gxtq81VAhpmNvEgA]{Tap Ley Line} that's empowered, you gain its benefits until the end of your next turn on a success (1 minute on a critical success).
