You inflict despair on creatures in the area. The effects for each creature are determined by its Will save.

Critical Success The creature is unaffected.

Success For 1 round, the creature can't use reactions and must attempt another save at the start of its turn; on a failure, it is @Compendium[pf2e.conditionitems.xYTAsEpcJE1Ccni3]{Slowed} 1 for that turn as it sobs uncontrollably.
