Name: Deceiver's Cloak Nom: Cape de tromperie État: libre ------ Description (en) ------

You wrap yourself in a cloak of illusion, appearing as another creature of the same body shape with roughly similar height and weight as yourself. This has the effects of a 3rd-level @Compendium[pf2e.spells-srd.i35dpZFI7jZcRoBo]{Illusory Disguise}.

Heightened (6th) You can appear as any creature of the same size, even with a completely different body shape.
