Name: Touch of Corruption Nom: État: aucune ------ Description (en) ------

The antipaladin infuses the target with negative energy. If the target is living, this deals 3d6 negative damage (basic Fortitude save); on a failed saving throw, the target also takes a –2 status penalty to AC for 1 round.

If the target is a willing undead creature, the target regains 18 Hit Points, and if it is one of the antipaladin's allies, it also gains a +2 status bonus to damage rolls for 1 round.
