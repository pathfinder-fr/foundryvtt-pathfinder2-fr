Name: Glitterdust Nom: Poussière scintillante État: libre ------ Data ------ AreasizeEN: 10-foot burst AreasizeFR: explosion de 3 m RangeEN: 120 feet RangeFR: 36 m ------ Description (en) ------

Creatures in the area are outlined by glittering dust. Each creature must attempt a Reflex save. If a creature has its invisibility negated by this spell, it is @Compendium[pf2e.conditionitems.DmAIPqOBomZ7H95W]{Concealed} instead of @Compendium[pf2e.conditionitems.zJxUflt9np0q4yML]{Invisible}. This applies both if the creature was already Invisible and if it benefits from new invisibility effects before the end of the invisibility negation effect from this spell.

Critical Success The target is unaffected.

Success The target's invisibility is negated for 2 rounds.

Failure The target is @Compendium[pf2e.conditionitems.TkIyaNPgTZFBCCuh]{Dazzled} for 1 minute and its invisibility is negated for 1 minute.

Critical Failure The target is @Compendium[pf2e.conditionitems.XgEqL1kFApUbl5Z2]{Blinded} for 1 round and Dazzled for 10 minutes. Its invisibility is negated for 10 minutes.
