Name: Dominate Nom: Domination État: officielle ------ Data ------ RangeEN: 30 feet RangeFR: 9 m TargetEN: 1 creature TargetFR: 1 créature ------ Description (en) ------

You take command of the target, forcing it to obey your orders. If you issue an obviously self-destructive order, the target doesn't act until you issue a new order. The effect depends on its Will save.

Critical Success The target is unaffected.

Success The target is @Compendium[pf2e.conditionitems.dfCMdR4wnpbYNTix]{Stunned 1} as it fights off your commands.

Failure You control the target. It gains the @Compendium[pf2e.conditionitems.9qGBRpbX9NEwtAAr]{Controlled} condition, but it can attempt a Will save at the end of each of its turns. On a success, the spell ends.

Critical Failure As a failure, but the target receives a new save only if you give it a new order that is against its nature, such as killing its allies.

Heightened (10th) The duration is unlimited.
