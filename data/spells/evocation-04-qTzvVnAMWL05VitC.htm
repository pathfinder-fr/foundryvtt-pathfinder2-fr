Name: Painful Vibrations Nom: Vibrations douloureuses État: libre ------ Data ------ RangeEN: 100 feet RangeFR: 30 m TargetEN: 1 living creature TargetFR: 1 créature vivante ------ Description (en) ------

You send powerful sound waves through an opponent's body, vibrating its bones and internal organs painfully. The creature takes 8d6 sonic damage and must attempt a Fortitude save.

Critical Success The target is unaffected.

Success The target takes half damage.

Failure The target takes full damage, is @Compendium[pf2e.conditionitems.fesd1n5eVhpCSS18]{Sickened 1}, and is @Compendium[pf2e.conditionitems.9PR9y0bi4JPKnHPR]{Deafened} for 1 round.

Critical Failure The target takes double damage, is @Compendium[pf2e.conditionitems.fesd1n5eVhpCSS18]{Sickened 2}, and is deafened for 1 minute.

Heightened (+1) The damage increases by 2d6.
