Name: Gravitational Pull Nom: Force gravitationnelle État: libre ------ Data ------ RangeEN: 30 feet RangeFR: 9 m TargetEN: 1 creature TargetFR: 1 créature ------ Description (en) ------

By suddenly altering gravity, you pull the target toward you. The target is pulled 10 feet closer to you unless it succeeds at a Fortitude save. On a critical failure, it's also knocked @Compendium[pf2e.conditionitems.j91X7x0XSomq8d60]{Prone}. The effects of this spell change depending on the number of actions you spend when you Cast this Spell.

1 (somatic) The spell targets one creature.

2 (somatic, verbal) The spell targets one creature and pulls the target 20 feet instead of 10.

3 (material, somatic, verbal) The spell targets up to 5 creatures.
