Name: Rapid Adaptation Nom: Adaptation rapide État: libre ------ Data ------ RangeEN: touch rangeFR: contact TargetEN: your companion or eidolon TargetFR: votre compagnon ou eidolon ------ Description (en) ------

You flood the target with energy from the terrain around it, compressing centuries of evolution into a single moment. The target gains one of the following natural adaptations based on the surrounding environment.

  • Aquatic The target gains a swim Speed equal to its land Speed. If it already had a swim Speed, it gains a +10-foot status bonus to its swim Speed. @Compendium[pf2e.spell-effects.fGK6zJ7mWz9D5QYo]{Spell Effect: Rapid Adaptation (Aquatic Base Swim Speed)} or @Compendium[pf2e.spell-effects.itmiGioGNuVvt4QE]{Spell Effect: Rapid Adaptation (Aquatic Speed Bonus)}
  • Arctic The target isn't affected by severe or extreme cold, and when it rolls a success on an Acrobatics check to Balance on ice and snow, it gets a critical success instead. @Compendium[pf2e.spell-effects.iiV80Kexj6vPmzqU]{Spell Effect: Rapid Adaptation (Arctic)}
  • Desert The target isn't affected by severe or extreme heat, and when it rolls a success on an Acrobatics check to Balance on sand, it gets a critical success instead. @Compendium[pf2e.spell-effects.qo7DoF11Xl9gqmFc]{Spell Effect: Rapid Adaptation (Desert)}
  • Forest The target gains scent as an imprecise sense with a range of 30 feet. @Compendium[pf2e.spell-effects.jtMo6qS47hPx6EbR]{Spell Effect: Rapid Adaptation (Forest)}
  • Mountain The target isn't @Compendium[pf2e.conditionitems.AJh5ex99aV6VTggg]{Flat-Footed} when Climbing, and when it rolls a success on an Acrobatics check to Balance on rubble, it gets a critical success instead. @Compendium[pf2e.spell-effects.sDN9b4bjCGH2nQnG]{Spell Effect: Rapid Adaptation (Mountain)}
  • Plains The target gains a +10-foot status bonus to its land Speed. @Compendium[pf2e.spell-effects.AM49w68oKykc2fHI]{Spell Effect: Rapid Adaptation (Plains)}
  • Underground The target gains darkvision. @Compendium[pf2e.spell-effects.0q2716S34XL1y9Hh]{Spell Effect: Rapid Adaptation (Underground)}
