Name: Hellfire Plume Nom: Volute de feu infernal État: officielle ------ Data ------ AreasizeEN: 10-foot radius, 60-foot-tall cylinder AreasizeFR: rayon de 3 mètres, cylindre de 15 mètres de hauteur RangeEN: 60 feet RangeFR: 18 mètres ------ Description (en) ------

You call forth a plume of hellfire that erupts from below, dealing 4d6 fire damage and 4d6 evil damage.

Heightened (+1) The fire damage increases by 1d6, and the evil damage increases by 1d6.
