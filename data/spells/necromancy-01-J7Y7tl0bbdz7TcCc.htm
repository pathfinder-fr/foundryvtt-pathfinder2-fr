Name: Ray of Enfeeblement Nom: Rayon affaiblissant État: officielle ------ Data ------ RangeEN: 30 feet RangeFR: 9 mètres TargetEN: 1 creature TargetFR: 1 créature ------ Description (en) ------

A ray that saps a foe's strength flashes from your hand. Attempt a ranged spell attack against the target. If you succeed, that creature attempts a Fortitude save to determine the spell's effect. If your attack roll is a critical success, use the outcome for one degree of success worse than the result of its save.

Critical Success The target is unaffected.

Success The target becomes @Compendium[pf2e.conditionitems.MIRkyAjyBeXivMa7]{Enfeebled 1}.

Failure The target becomes @Compendium[pf2e.conditionitems.MIRkyAjyBeXivMa7]{Enfeebled 2}.

Critical Failure The target becomes @Compendium[pf2e.conditionitems.MIRkyAjyBeXivMa7]{Enfeebled 3}.
