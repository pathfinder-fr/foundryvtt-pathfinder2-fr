You afflict the target with the curse of the roiling, unforgiving sea. The target must attempt a Will save.

Critical Success The target is unaffected.

Success The target becomes @Compendium[pf2e.conditionspf2e.fesd1n5eVhpCSS18]{Sickened} 1. Reducing its @Compendium[pf2e.conditionspf2e.fesd1n5eVhpCSS18]{Sickened} condition to 0 ends the curse.

Failure The target becomes @Compendium[pf2e.conditionspf2e.fesd1n5eVhpCSS18]{Sickened} 1 and can't reduce its @Compendium[pf2e.conditionspf2e.fesd1n5eVhpCSS18]{Sickened} condition below 1 while the curse remains. The curse can be lifted by remove curse or similar magic. Whenever the target is @Compendium[pf2e.conditionspf2e.fesd1n5eVhpCSS18]{Sickened} and on the water at least a mile from shore, it is also @Compendium[pf2e.conditionspf2e.xYTAsEpcJE1Ccni3]{Slowed} 1.

Critical Failure As failure, but the target becomes @Compendium[pf2e.conditionspf2e.fesd1n5eVhpCSS18]{Sickened} 2.
