Name: Sweet Dream Nom: Doux rêve État: changé État d'origine: officielle ------ Description (en) ------

With soothing song or tales, you lull the target into an enchanting dream. When you cast the spell, the target falls @Compendium[pf2e.conditionitems.fBnFDH2MTzgFijKf]{Unconscious} if it wasn't already. While Unconscious, it experiences a dream of your choice.

If it sleeps for at least 1 minute, it gains the benefit of the dream for the remainder of the spell's duration.

  • @Compendium[pf2e.spell-effects.v051JKN0Dj3ve5cF]{Dream of Insight} +1 status bonus to Intelligence-based skill checks
  • @Compendium[pf2e.spell-effects.MJSoRFfEdM4j5mNG]{Dream of Glamour} +1 status bonus to Charisma-based skill checks
  • @Compendium[pf2e.spell-effects.sE2txm68yZSFMV3v]{Dream of Voyaging} +5-foot status bonus to Speed

If you Cast this Spell again, the effects of any previous sweet dream you cast end.
