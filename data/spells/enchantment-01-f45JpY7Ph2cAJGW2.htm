Name: Evil Eye Nom: Mauvais œil État: libre ------ Data ------ RangeEN: 30 feet RangeFR: 9 m TargetEN: 1 creature TargetFR: 1 créature ------ Description (en) ------

You fix your eye on the target, imposing a malevolent hex. The target becomes frightened based on the results of its Will save. This condition value can't be reduced below 1 while the spell is active and you can see the target. Regardless of the outcome, the target is then temporarily immune for 1 minute.

Success The target is unaffected.

Critical Failure The target is @Compendium[pf2e.conditionitems.TBSHQspnbcqxsmjL]{Frightened 2}.
