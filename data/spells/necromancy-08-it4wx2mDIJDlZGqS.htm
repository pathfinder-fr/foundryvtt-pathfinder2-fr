You call forth a divine cataclysm from your deity, destroying living and undead creatures in the area alike. You can't cast this spell if you don't have a deity. This spell gains the trait corresponding to the alignment damage dealt.

Heightened (+1) The damage increases by 1d6 negative damage, [[/r 1d6]] alignment damage, and 1d6 additional negative and positive damage for a true neutral deity.
