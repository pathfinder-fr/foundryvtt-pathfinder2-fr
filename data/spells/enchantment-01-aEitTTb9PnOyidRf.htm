Name: Needle of Vengeance Nom: Aiguille de la vengeance État: libre ------ Data ------ RangeEN: 30 feet RangeFR: 9 mètres TargetEN: 1 enemy TargetFR: 1 ennemi ------ Description (en) ------

A long, jagged needle jabs into the target foe's psyche whenever it tries to attack a specifically forbidden creature. Name yourself or one of your allies. The target takes [[/r {2}[mental]]]{2 mental damage} any time it uses a hostile action against the named creature.

Heightened (+1) Increase the damage by [[/r {2}[mental]]]{2 mental}.
