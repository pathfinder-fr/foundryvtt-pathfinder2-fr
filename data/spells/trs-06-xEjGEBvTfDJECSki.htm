You take the form of your ancestral spirits, becoming @Compendium[pf2e.conditionitems.zJxUflt9np0q4yML]{Invisible} and quasi‑corporeal. You can't move through solid objects, but you can float through tiny gaps. You can't @Compendium[pf2e.actionspf2e.aBQ8ajvEBByv45yz]{Cast Spells} (but you can @Compendium[pf2e.actionspf2e.3f5DMFu8fPiqHpRg]{Sustain a Spell}), activate items, or use actions that have the attack or manipulate trait.
