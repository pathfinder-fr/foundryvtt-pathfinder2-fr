Name: Admonishing Ray Nom: Rayon d'admonestation État: libre ------ Data ------ RangeEN: 60 feet RangeFR: 18 mètres TargetEN: 1 creature TargetFR: 1 créature ------ Description (en) ------

A ray of energy bludgeons your target into submission without causing lasting harm. When you cast this spell, you choose whether the ray feels like a strong punch or slap. Make a spell attack roll. The ray deals 2d6 bludgeoning damage.

Critical Success The target takes double damage.

Success The target takes full damage.

Heightened (+1) The damage increases by 2d6.

------ Description (fr) ------

Un rayon d'énergie frappe votre cible pour le forcer à se soumettre sans lui causer de dégâts durables.
