Name: Mask of Terror Nom: Masque terrifiant État: officielle ------ Data ------ RangeEN: 30 feet RangeFR: 9 mètres TargetEN: 1 creature TargetFR: 1 créature ------ Description (en) ------

The target appears to be a gruesome and terrifying creature. The effect is unique to each observer, so a human viewing the target might see a demon with bloody fangs, but a demon observing the target might see a glowing angelic visage. When any creature attempts a hostile action against the target, the creature must attempt a Will save. It is then temporarily immune until the end of its next turn.

Success The creature is unaffected.

Failure The creature becomes @Compendium[pf2e.conditionitems.TBSHQspnbcqxsmjL]{Frightened 2} before using its action.

Critical Failure The creature becomes @Compendium[pf2e.conditionitems.TBSHQspnbcqxsmjL]{Frightened 2}, and its action fails and is wasted.

Heightened (8th) You can target up to 5 creatures. If a creature uses a hostile action or reaction that affects multiple targets simultaneously, it needs to attempt only one save against mask of terror.

Une créature doit faire un jet de Volonté lorsqu’elle tente une action hostile contre la cible. Elle est ensuite temporairement immunisée jusqu’à la fin de son prochain tour.

Succès La créature n’est pas affectée.

Échec La créature est @Compendium[pf2e.conditionitems.TBSHQspnbcqxsmjL]{Effrayée} 2 avant d’effectuer son action.
