Name: Medusa's Wrath Nom: Courroux de la méduse État: libre ------ Description (en) ------

You make an attack filled with a medusa's petrifying power. Make an unarmed Strike with the following additional effects.

Critical Success The target is @Compendium[pf2e.conditionitems.xYTAsEpcJE1Ccni3]{Slowed 2} and must attempt a Fortitude save at the end of each of its turns; this ongoing save has the incapacitation trait.

On a failed save, the Slowed condition increases by 1 (2 on a critical failure). A successful save reduces the Slowed condition by 1. When a creature is unable to act due to the Slowed condition from medusa's wrath, it is @Compendium[pf2e.conditionitems.dTwPJuKgBQCMxixg]{Petrified} permanently. The spell ends if the creature is Petrified or the Slowed condition is removed.

Success As critical success, but the target is initially @Compendium[pf2e.conditionitems.xYTAsEpcJE1Ccni3]{Slowed 1}.
