Name: Stone to Flesh Nom: Pierre en chair État: officielle ------ Description (en) ------

Manipulating the fundamental particles of matter, you convert stone into flesh and blood. You restore a @Compendium[pf2e.conditionspf2e.dTwPJuKgBQCMxixg]{Petrified} creature to its normal state or transform a stone object into a mass of inert flesh (without stone's Hardness) in roughly the same shape.
