Name: Thunderburst Nom: Explosion tempêtueuse État: libre ------ Description (en) ------

You create a powerful blast of air and a loud peal of thunder, dealing [[/r 2d6 #bludgeoning]] bludgeoning damage and [[/r 2d6 #sonic]] sonic damage. Each creature in the area must attempt a Fortitude save.

Critical Success The creature is unaffected.

Success The creature takes half damage.

Failure The creature takes full damage and is @Compendium[pf2e.conditionitems.9PR9y0bi4JPKnHPR]{Deafened} for 1 minute.

Critical Failure The creature takes double damage and is
Deafened for 1 hour.

Heightened (+2) Increase each type of damage by [[/r 2d6]] and the area by 5 feet.
