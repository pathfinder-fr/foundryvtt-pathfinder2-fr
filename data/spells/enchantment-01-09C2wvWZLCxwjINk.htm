You speak the true name of a creature to more surely affect it with your magic. Until the end of the current turn, the target is @Compendium[pf2e.conditionitems.AJh5ex99aV6VTggg]{Flat-Footed} against your spells and takes a -2 circumstance penalty to saving throws against your spells.
