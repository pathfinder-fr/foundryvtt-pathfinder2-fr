You pray over a dying creature, drawing its shadow into your service as long as its owner is stooped at death's door.

You summon a @Compendium[pf2e.pathfinder-bestiary.IUzKFRX0uHl1yxkn]{Greater Shadow} in a space adjacent to the dying creature. The greater shadow has its usual stats, with the following modifications:
