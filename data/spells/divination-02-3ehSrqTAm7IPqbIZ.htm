Name: Spirit Sense Nom: Perception des esprits État: libre ------ Description (en) ------

You open your mind to the metaphysical, enabling you to sense nearby spirits. Even if you aren't @Compendium[pf2e.actionspf2e.TiNDYUGlMmxzxBYU]{Searching}, you get a check to find haunts and spirits in the area. You gain a +1 status bonus to the following checks regarding haunts or spirits: Perception checks to @Compendium[pf2e.actionspf2e.BlAOM2X92SI6HMtJ]{Seek}, attempts to @Compendium[pf2e.actionspf2e.B2BpIZFHoF9Kjzpx]{Recall Knowledge}, skill checks to determine the reason for their existence, and skill checks to disable a haunt. You also gain a +1 status bonus to AC and saving throws against haunts and spirits.

Heightened (6th) The spell's duration lasts until the next time you make your daily preparations.
