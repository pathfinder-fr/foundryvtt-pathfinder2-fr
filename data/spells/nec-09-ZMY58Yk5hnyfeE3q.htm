Name: Linnorm Sting Nom: Piqûre de linnorm État: libre ------ Description (en) ------

You replicate the fiery venom of the tor linnorm and inflict an enemy with the dread dragon's debilitating bite. You deal [[/r 2d12 #piercing]] piercing damage to the targeted creature and afflict it with tor linnorm venom. If the target takes piercing damage, they must attempt a Fortitude save.

Critical Success The target is unaffected.

Success The target takes [[/r 8d6 #fire]] fire damage.

Failure The target is afflicted with Tor Linnorm Venom at stage 1.

Critical Failure The target is afflicted with tor linnorm venom at stage 2.
