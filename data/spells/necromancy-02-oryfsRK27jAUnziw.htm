Name: Imp Sting Nom: Dard de diablotin État: libre ------ Data ------ RangeEN: touch RangeFR: contact TargetEN: 1 creature TargetFR: 1 créature ------ Description (en) ------

You reproduce an imp's evil, poisonous sting, stabbing at an enemy and possibly affecting them with the devil's benumbing venom. You deal [[/r {1d4}[piercing]]]{1d4 piercing damage} to the touched creature and afflict the creature with imp venom. If the target takes piercing damage, they also take [[/r {1d6}[evil]]]{1d6 evil damage} and must attempt a Fortitude save.

Critical Success The target is unaffected.

Success The target takes [[/r {1d6}[poison]]]{1d6 poison damage}.

Failure The target is afflicted with Imp Venom at stage 1.

Critical Failure The target is afflicted with imp venom at stage 2.
