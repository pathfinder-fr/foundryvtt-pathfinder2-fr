Name: Unfolding Wind Rush Nom: Ruée du vent déroulé État: changé État d'origine: libre ------ Description (en) ------

You travel on a rushing wind. Move two times: two Strides, two Steps, or one Stride and one Step (in either order). You can Stride or Step into the air during this movement, moving upward at a 45-degree angle at most; if you end your movement in the air, you fall after taking one action or when your turn ends, whichever comes first. A @Compendium[pf2e.spells-srd.it4ZsAi6XgvGcodc]{Wall of Wind} appears in all the spaces you left during this movement, lasting for 1 round.

Heightened (5th) The wall of wind lasts 3 rounds.
