Name: Overstuff Nom: État: aucune ------ Description (en) ------

Huge amounts of food and drink fill the target. It receives a full meal's worth of nourishment and must attempt a Fortitude save. A target @JournalEntry[Sickened] by this spell takes a –10-foot status penalty to its Speed until it's no longer @JournalEntry[Sickened].

Success The target is @JournalEntry[Sickened] 1, but if it spends an action to end the condition, it succeeds automatically.

Failure The target is @JournalEntry[Sickened] 1.

Critical Failure The target is @JournalEntry[Sickened] 2.
