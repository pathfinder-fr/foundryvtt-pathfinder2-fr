Name: Pied Piping Nom: Flûte de Hamelin État: libre ------ Data ------ AreasizeEN: 5-foot emanation AreasizeFR: émanation de 1,5 m ------ Description (en) ------

Your performance enraptures those who hear it, compelling them to follow you about in admiration. Each creature within the emanation must attempt a Will save when you Cast the Spell or the first time they enter the area, after which they become temporarily immune for 1 day.

Once per turn, you can spend a single action, which has the concentrate trait, to increase the emanation's radius by 5 feet. You can @Compendium[pf2e.actionspf2e.AjLSHZSWQ90exdLo]{Dismiss} the spell.

Critical Success The creature is unaffected.

Success The creature is @Compendium[pf2e.conditionitems.AdPVz7rbaVSRxHFg]{Fascinated} with you.

Failure The creature uses all its actions to move toward you and compliment your performance. This effect ends if a hostile action is used against the affected creature.

Critical Failure The target gains the minion trait and is @Compendium[pf2e.conditionitems.9qGBRpbX9NEwtAAr]{Controlled} by you. This effect ends if a hostile action is used against the affected creature, or if you direct the creature to use any action that causes it harm.
