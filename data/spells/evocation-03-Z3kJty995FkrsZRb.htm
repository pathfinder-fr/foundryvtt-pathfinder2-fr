You ignite a creature in lasting flames. The fire deals 4d8 fire damage and [[/r {2d6}[persistent,fire]]] @Compendium[pf2e.conditionitems.lDVqvLKA6eF3Df60]{Persistent Fire Damage} to the creature, which must attempt a Fortitude save.

Critical Success The creature is unaffected.

Success The creature takes half damage and takes no persistent damage.

Failure The creature takes full damage, as well as full persistent damage.

Critical Failure The creature takes double damage, as well as double persistent damage.

Heightened (+1) Increase the initial damage by 1d8 and the persistent damage by [[/r {1d6}[fire]]].
