Name: Hypnopompic Terrors Nom: Terreurs hypnopompiques État: libre ------ Data ------ RangeEN: 30 feet RangeFR: 9 mètres TargetEN: up to 10 creatures TargetFR: jusqu'à 10 créatures ------ Description (en) ------

You send a wave of nightmarish visions crashing over your targets, leaving them @Compendium[pf2e.conditionitems.6uEgoh53GbXuHpTF]{Paralyzed} by fear. These visions inflict real wounds on vulnerable targets, dealing 6d12 mental damage. If you target a sleeping creature, it wakes up as it attempts the save but takes one degree of success worse than the result it rolled on its save.

Critical Success The target is unaffected.

Success The target is @Compendium[pf2e.conditionitems.TBSHQspnbcqxsmjL]{Frightened 2} and takes half the mental damage.

Failure The target is frightened 2, is @Compendium[pf2e.conditionitems.6uEgoh53GbXuHpTF]{Paralyzed} for 1 round, and takes the full mental damage.

Critical Failure The target is frightened 2, is paralyzed for 2 rounds, and takes double the mental damage.
