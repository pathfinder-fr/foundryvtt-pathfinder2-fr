You send a wave of nightmarish visions crashing over your targets, leaving them @Compendium[pf2e.conditionitems.6uEgoh53GbXuHpTF]{Paralyzed} by fear. These visions inflict real wounds on vulnerable targets, dealing 6d12 mental damage. Critical Success The target is unaffected.

Success The target is @Compendium[pf2e.conditionitems.TBSHQspnbcqxsmjL]{Frightened 2} and takes half the mental damage.

Failure The target is frightened 2, is @Compendium[pf2e.conditionitems.6uEgoh53GbXuHpTF]{Paralyzed} for 1 round, and takes the full mental damage.

Critical Failure The target is frightened 2, is paralyzed for 2 rounds, and takes double the mental damage.
