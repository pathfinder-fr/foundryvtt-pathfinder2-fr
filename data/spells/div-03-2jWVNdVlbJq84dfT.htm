Name: Battlefield Persistence Nom: État: aucune ------ Description (en) ------

Trigger You  are  about  to  attempt  a  saving throw, but you haven’t rolled yet.

You’re difficult to remove from battle. You gain a +2 status
bonus to the triggering save, and if the triggering save is
against an incapacitation effect, it treats you as if you were
2 levels higher.
