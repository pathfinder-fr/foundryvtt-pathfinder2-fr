Name: Thoughtful Gift Nom: Cadeau attentionné État: libre ------ Data ------ RangeEN: 120 feet RangeFR: 36 m TargetEN: 1 willing creature TargetFR: 1 créature consentante ------ Description (en) ------

You teleport one object of light or negligible Bulk held in your hand to the target. The object appears instantly in the target's hand, if they have a free hand, or at their feet if they don't. The target knows what object you are attempting to send them. If the target is @Compendium[pf2e.conditionitems.fBnFDH2MTzgFijKf]{Unconscious} or refuses to accept your gift, or if the spell would teleport a creature (even if the creature is inside an extradimensional container), the spell fails.

Heightened (3rd) The spell's range increases to 500 feet.

Heightened (5th) As 3rd level, and the object's maximum Bulk increases to 1. You can @Compendium[pf2e.actionspf2e.aBQ8ajvEBByv45yz]{Cast the Spell} with 3 actions instead of 1; doing so increases the range to 1 mile and you don't need line of sight to the target, but you must be extremely familiar with the target.

Intensifié (5e) Comme au niveau 3 et l'encombrement maximal de l'objet passe à 1. Vous pouvez @Compendium[pf2e.actionspf2e.aBQ8ajvEBByv45yz]{Lancer le sort} avec 3 actions au lieu de 1 ; Procéder ainsi augmente la portée à 1,5 km et vous n'avez pas besoin d'une ligne de mire vers la cible, mais vous devez être extrêmement familier de la cible.
