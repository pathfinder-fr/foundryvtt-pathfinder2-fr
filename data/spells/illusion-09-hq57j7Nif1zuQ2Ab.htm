Name: Unspeakable Shadow Nom: Ombre imprononçable État: libre ------ Data ------ RangeEN: 120 feet RangeFR: 36 m TargetEN: 1 creature TargetFR: 1 créature ------ Description (en) ------

You alter a creature's shadow, transforming it into a terrifying monster out to devour the creature. The creature must attempt a Will save. A creature that has the frightened condition from unspeakable shadow must spend at least one of its actions each turn to either attack its shadow (making a Strike ineffectually) or flee from its shadow (using one action to move away as though it had the fleeing condition).

Critical Success The target is unaffected.

Success The target is @Compendium[pf2e.conditionitems.TBSHQspnbcqxsmjL]{Frightened 2}.

Failure The target is @Compendium[pf2e.conditionitems.TBSHQspnbcqxsmjL]{Frightened 3}. It can't reduce its frightened value below 1 for 1 minute.

Critical Failure The target is so afraid, it might instantly die. It must succeed at a Fortitude save or die; this saving throw has the incapacitation trait. If it succeeds at its save, the target is @Compendium[pf2e.conditionitems.TBSHQspnbcqxsmjL]{Frightened 4} and can't reduce its frightened value below 1 for 1 minute.
