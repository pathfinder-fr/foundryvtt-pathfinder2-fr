Name: Puff of Poison Nom: Bouffée de poison État: libre ------ Data ------ RangeEN: 5 feet RangeFR: 1,5 mètres TargetEN: 1 creature TargetFR: 1 créature ------ Description (en) ------

You exhale a shimmering cloud of toxic breath at an enemy's face. The target takes poison damage equal to your spellcasting modifier and [[/r {2}[persistent,poison]]] @Compendium[pf2e.conditionitems.lDVqvLKA6eF3Df60]{Persistent Poison Damage}, depending on its Fortitude save.

Critical Success The creature is unaffected.

Success The target takes half initial and persistent damage.

Failure The target takes full initial and persistent damage.

Critical Failure The target takes double initial and persistent damage.

Heightened (+2) The initial poison damage increases by 1d8 and the persistent poison damage increases by 1.
