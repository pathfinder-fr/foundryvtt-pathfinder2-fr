You harness positive energy to heal your animal companion’s wounds. You restore [[/r 1d10]] Hit Points to your animal companion. The number of actions you spend @Compendium[pf2e.actionspf2e.aBQ8ajvEBByv45yz]{Casting this Spell} determines range and other parameters.

[two-actions] (verbal) The spell has a range of 30 feet and restores an additional 8 Hit Points to the target.

Heightened (+1) The amount of healing increases by [[/r 1d10]], and the additional healing for the 2-action version increases by 8.
