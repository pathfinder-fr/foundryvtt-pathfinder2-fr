Name: Spinning Staff Nom: Bâton tournoyant État: libre ------ Data ------ MaterialEN: You're wielding a staff MaterialFR: Vous maniez un bâton ------ Description (en) ------

You spin your staff with a twirling flourish, attacking two foes and transforming the momentum into magical energy to charge for your next @Compendium[pf2e.actionspf2e.QDW9H8XLIjuW2fE4]{Spellstrike}. Make a melee Strike with your staff against one foe and then a second melee Strike with your staff against a different foe. Your multiple attack penalty applies to both of these attacks as normal.
