Name: Rostland Partisan Nom: Partisan du Rost État: libre ------ Description (en) ------

Prerequisite Region - Broken Lands

You grew up among the southern houses of Brevoy in old Rostland. You're accustomed to arguing from a position of underappreciated worth.

Choose two ability boosts. One must be to Constitution or Charisma, and one is a free ability boost.

You're trained in the Diplomacy skill and the Politics Lore skill. You gain the @Compendium[pf2e.feats-srd.KpFetnUqTiweypZk]{Group Impression} skill feat.
