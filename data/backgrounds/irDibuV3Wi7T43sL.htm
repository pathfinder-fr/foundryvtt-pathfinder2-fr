Name: Child of the Puddles Nom: Marmot des flaques État: libre ------ Description (en) ------

Prerequisite Region - Absalom and Starstone Isle

You grew up in the soggy, squalid quarter of Absalom known as the Puddles. You're at home in tightly packed urban environments.

Choose two ability boosts. One must be to Dexterity or Intelligence, and one is a free ability boost.

You're trained in the Acrobatics skill, and the Absalom Lore skill. You gain the @Compendium[pf2e.feats-srd.CnqMJR8e9jqJR7MM]{Steady Balance} skill feat.
