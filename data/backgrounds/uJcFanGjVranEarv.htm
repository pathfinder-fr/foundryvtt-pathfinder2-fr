Name: Lumber Consortium Laborer Nom: Ouvrier du Consortium du bois État: libre ------ Description (en) ------

Prerequisite Region - Shining Kingdoms

You have suffered as a worker for the unscrupulous Lumber Consortium, laboring under harsh conditions in dangerous wooded regions of Andoran.

Choose two ability boosts. One must be to Strength or Dexterity, and one is a free ability boost.

You're trained in the Athletics skill and the Forest Lore skill. You gain the @Compendium[pf2e.feats-srd.W6Gl9ePmItfDHji0]{Assurance} skill feat with Athletics.
