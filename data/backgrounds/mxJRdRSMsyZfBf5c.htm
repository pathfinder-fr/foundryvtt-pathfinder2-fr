Name: Hermean Heritor Nom: Héritier d'Herméa État: libre ------ Description (en) ------

[Legacy - Age of Ashes]

With the restrictions on Hermean citizenship lifted, you may have fled the city of Promise or have spent some of your childhood in the company of someone who did.

Choose two ability boosts. One must be to Constitution or Wisdom, and one is a free ability boost.

You're trained in the Society skill and the Legal Lore skill. You gain the @Compendium[pf2e.feats-srd.P9HCz0uR6xPHuw72]{Multilingual} skill feat or the @Compendium[pf2e.feats-srd.W6Gl9ePmItfDHji0]{Assurance} skill feat for Society.
