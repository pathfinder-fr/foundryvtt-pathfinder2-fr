Even in a city renowned for its alchemy, you were able to rise above the competition.

Choose two ability boosts. One must be to Dexterity or Intelligence, and one is a free ability boost.

You’re trained in the Crafting skill and the Alchemical Lore skill. You gain the @Compendium[pf2e.feats-srd.RwEZCpQEN90Mkrvm]{Specialty Crafting} skill feat with alchemy.
