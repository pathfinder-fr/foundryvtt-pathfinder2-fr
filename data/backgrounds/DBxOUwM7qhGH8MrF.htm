Name: Courier Nom: Garçon de course État: libre ------ Description (en) ------

In your youth, you earned coin running messages for persons of wealth and influence, darting through crowded cobblestone streets. Your dogged commitment to deliver your message was good training for the life of an adventurer.

Choose two ability boosts. One must be to Dexterity or Intelligence, and one is a free ability boost.

You're trained in the Society skill and the Lore skill for the city in which you were raised. You gain the @Compendium[pf2e.feats-srd.WeQGWvlWdeLeOlCN]{Glean Contents} skill feat.
