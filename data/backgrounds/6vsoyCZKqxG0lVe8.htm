Name: Inlander Nom: Habitant de l'intérieur des terres État: libre ------ Description (en) ------

Prerequisite Region - Absalom and Starstone Isle

You grew up in an untamed region of the Isle of Kortos, and you know how to survive in the wild.

Choose two ability boosts. One must be to Constitution or Wisdom, and one is a free ability boost.

You're trained in the Survival skill and a Lore skill related to the terrain type associated with your home region (such as Hills Lore or Mountains Lore). You gain the @Compendium[pf2e.feats-srd.tGIXuk0XeWmG04CX]{Survey Wildlife} skill feat.
