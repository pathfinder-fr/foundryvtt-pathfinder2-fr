Name: Goblinblood Orphan Nom: Orphelin des guerres du Sang gobelin État: officielle ------ Description (en) ------

Prerequisite Region - Old Cheliax

Your family, whether goblin, hobgoblin, or human, died in the Goblinblood Wars. Though you were marked by these losses, you managed to survive through your own resilience and resourcefulness.

Choose two ability boosts. One must be to Dexterity or Constitution, and one is a free ability boost.

You're trained in the Survival skill and the Goblin Lore skill. You gain the @Compendium[pf2e.feats-srd.W6Gl9ePmItfDHji0]{Assurance} skill feat with Survival.
