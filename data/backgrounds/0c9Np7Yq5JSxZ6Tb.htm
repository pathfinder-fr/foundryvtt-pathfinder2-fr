Name: Alkenstar Tinker Nom: Bricoleur d'Alkenastre État: libre ------ Description (en) ------

Prerequisite Region - Impossible Lands

Your dedication to the scientific inquiry of your native Alkenstar provides great insight into mechanical and chemical innovation.

You're trained in the Crafting skill and the Engineering Lore skill. You gain the @Compendium[pf2e.feats-srd.is3Oz9wt11lNq62K]{Alchemical Crafting} skill feat.
