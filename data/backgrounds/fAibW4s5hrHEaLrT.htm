Name: Child of Westcrown Nom: Enfant de Couronne d'Ouest État: libre ------ Description (en) ------

Whether you come from Westcrown or elsewhere, you hold the values of pre-Thrune Cheliax dear. You disdain the infernal government, but you are proud of your country and do not consider yourself a rebel.

Choose two ability boosts. One must be to Strength or Charisma, and one is a free ability boost.

You’re trained in the Diplomacy skill and the Westcrown Lore skill. You gain the @Compendium[pf2e.feats-srd.KpFetnUqTiweypZk]{Group Impression} skill feat.
