Name: Freed Slave of Absalom Nom: Esclave libéré d'Absalom État: libre ------ Description (en) ------

Prerequisite Region - Absalom and Starstone Isle

As a recently freed slave in Absalom, you belong to a new, close-knit social class at the heart of the city's most important trades.

Choose two ability boosts. One must be to Constitution or Intelligence, and one is a free ability boost.

You're trained in the Society skill and the Absalom Lore skill. You gain the @Compendium[pf2e.feats-srd.X2jGFfLU5qI5XVot]{Streetwise} skill feat.
