Name: Bonuwat Wavetouched Nom: Bonuwat Touché par les vagues État: libre ------ Description (en) ------

You are a child of the Bonuwat people, and the sea’s salt flows through your veins. You’ve taken to sailing and swimming gracefully and with ease, earning you the honorific “wavetouched.”

Choose two ability boosts. One must be to Strength or Wisdom, and one is a free ability boost.

You’re trained in the Athletics skill and the Ocean Lore skill. You gain the @Compendium[pf2e.feats-srd.iOY6YfGBaOvMNAor]{Underwater Marauder} skill feat.
