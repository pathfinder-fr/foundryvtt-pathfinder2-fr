Name: Adaptive Anadi Nom: Anadi adaptée État: libre ------ Description (en) ------

You descend from a line of anadi who worked to perfect their transformation magic, allowing them to integrate into a wider variety of cultures. Choose a common, Medium humanoid ancestry. Your human form is replaced with a form that matches this choice. You also gain the @Compendium[pf2e.feats-srd.ihN8gkHSdPG9Trte]{Adopted Ancestry} feat for your chosen humanoid ancestry.
