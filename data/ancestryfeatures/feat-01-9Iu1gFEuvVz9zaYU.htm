Name: Spined Azarketi Nom: Azarketi épineux État: libre ------ Description (en) ------

Your fins conceal launchable toxic spines. You have a spine ranged unarmed attack with the unarmed trait that deals [[/r 1d4 #poison]] poison damage, has a range increment of 10 feet, and is in the dart weapon group.
