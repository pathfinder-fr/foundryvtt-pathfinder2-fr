Name: Earthly Wilds Kitsune Nom: Kitsune des Étendues sauvages terrestres État: libre ------ Description (en) ------

You are a creature of the material world, with an affinity closer to the wilds than urban society. You gain a jaws unarmed attack that deals [[/r 1d6 #piercing]] piercing damage. Your jaws are in the brawling group and have the finesse and unarmed traits. Your alternate form is a fox, which has the statistics of 1st‑level @Compendium[pf2e.spells-srd.gfPjmG6Fe6D3MFjl]{Pest Form}.
