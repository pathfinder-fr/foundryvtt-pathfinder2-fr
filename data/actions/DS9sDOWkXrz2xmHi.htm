Requirements You are wielding a bow

You @Compendium[pf2e.actionspf2e.aBQ8ajvEBByv45yz]{Cast a Spell} that takes 1 or 2 actions to cast and requires a spell attack roll. The effects of the spell do not occur immediately but are imbued into the bow you're wielding.

Conditions Vous maniez un arc
