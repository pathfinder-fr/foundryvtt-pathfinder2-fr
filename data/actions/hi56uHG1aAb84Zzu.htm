Trigger A creature that you can see uses a mental effect against you

Success The creature is unaffected.

Failure The creature is @Compendium[pf2e.conditionitems.TBSHQspnbcqxsmjL]{Frightened 2}, and you gain a +2 status bonus to your saving throw or other defense against the triggering mental effect.
