Name: Opportune Riposte Nom: Riposte opportune État: libre ------ Description (en) ------

Trigger A foe within your reach critically fails a Strike against you

You take advantage of an opening from your enemy's fumbled attack. You either make a melee Strike against the triggering foe or attempt to @Compendium[pf2e.actionspf2e.Dt6B1slsBy8ipJu9]{Disarm} it of the weapon it used for the Strike.
