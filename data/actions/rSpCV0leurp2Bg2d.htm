Name: Instinctive Obfuscation Nom: Obscurcissement instinctif État: libre ------ Description (en) ------

Prerequisites At least one arcane or occult innate spell gained from a gnome heritage or gnome ancestry feat

Frequency Once per day

Trigger You are attacked by a foe

The magic within you manifests as a natural reaction to threats. You gain the effects of @Compendium[pf2e.spells-srd.j8vIoIEWElvpwkcI]{Mirror Image} but with two images instead of three. The tradition of this action matches the tradition of your gnome ancestry options.
