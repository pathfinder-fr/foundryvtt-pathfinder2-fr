Name: Clue In Nom: Ajouter un indice État: libre ------ Description (en) ------

Frequency once per 10 minutes

Trigger Another creature attempts a check to investigate a lead you're pursuing

You share information with the triggering creature. They gain a circumstance bonus to their check equal to your circumstance bonus to checks investigating your subject from @Compendium[pf2e.actionspf2e.xTK2zsWFyxSJvYbX]{Pursue a Lead}. The GM can add any relevant traits to this reaction depending on the situation, such as auditory and linguistic if you're conveying information verbally.
