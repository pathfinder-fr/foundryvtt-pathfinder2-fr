Name: Explode Nom: Exploser État: changé État d'origine: libre ------ Description (en) ------

You intentionally take your innovation beyond normal safety limits, making it explode and damage nearby creatures without damaging the innovation... hopefully. The explosion deals [[/r max(2, @actor.level)d6[fire]]]{leveled fire damage} with a basic Reflex save to all creatures in a @Template[type:emanation|distance:5] around you (if you're wearing or holding the innovation) or around your innovation (if your innovation is a minion).

At 3rd level, and every level thereafter, increase your explosion's damage by 1d6.

If you have the breakthrough innovation class feature, you can choose either a 5-foot or @Template[type:emanation|distance:10] for the area when you use Explode; if you have the revolutionary innovation class feature, you can choose a 5-foot, 10-foot, or @Template[type:emanation|distance:15].

@Check[type:flat|dc:17|name:Unstable Flat Check|traits:unstable|showDC:all]{Unstable Check}

On a failure, the innovation malfunctions in a spectacular (though harmless) fashion, such as a belch of smoke or shower of sparks, and it becomes incapable of being used for further unstable actions.

On a critical failure, you also take [[{@actor.level}[fire]]]{fire damage equal to your level}.
