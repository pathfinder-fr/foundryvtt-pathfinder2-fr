Name: Barbed Quills Nom: Piquants barbelés État: libre ------ Description (en) ------

Frequency once per day

Trigger You are hit with an unarmed strike or a strike with a non-reach melee weapon.

You break off quills in your attacker's flesh. You deal [[/r {1d8}[piercing]]]{1d8 piercing damage} to the triggering creature (basic Reflex save using your class DC or spell DC, whichever is higher.) On a critical failure, the creature also takes [[/r {1d4}[persistent,bleed]]] @Compendium[pf2e.conditionitems.lDVqvLKA6eF3Df60]{Persistent Bleed Damage} as your quills hook into its flesh. At 3rd level, and every 2 levels thereafter, this damage increases by 1d8 and the persistent piercing damage increases by 1.

[[/r ceil(@actor.level/2)d8 #piercing]]{Levelled} piercing damage

[[/r 1d4+ceil(@actor.level/2)-1 #persistent bleed]]{Levelled} @Compendium[pf2e.conditionitems.lDVqvLKA6eF3Df60]{Persistent Bleed Damage}

Note: +1 bonus from Bristle is not added automatically

Au niveau 3, puis à chaque niveau impair par la suite, ces dégâts augmentent d'1d8 et les dégâts perforants persistants augmentent de 1.
