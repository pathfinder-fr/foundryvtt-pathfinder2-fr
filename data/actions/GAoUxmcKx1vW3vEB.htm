Name: Exhale Poison Nom: Expirer le poison État: changé État d'origine: libre ------ Description (en) ------

Frequency once per hour
Requirements You were within the area of an inhaled poison within the last minute; Iron Lung

You sharply exhale the poison that previously surrounded you in a 15-foot cone. Creatures in the area are affected by the poison; the poison's DC and effects are unchanged.
