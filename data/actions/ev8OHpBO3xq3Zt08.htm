Name: Tail Toxin Nom: Queue à toxine État: libre ------ Description (en) ------

Frequency once per day

Requirements You are wielding a piercing or slashing weapon

You apply your tail's venom to a piercing or slashing weapon. If your next Strike with that weapon before the end of your next turn hits and deals damage, you deal @Compendium[pf2e.conditionitems.lDVqvLKA6eF3Df60]{Persistent Poison Damage} equal to your level to the target.
