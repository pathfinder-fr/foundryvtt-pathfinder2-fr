Name: Cavern Kobold Nom: Kobold cavernicole État: libre ------ Description (en) ------

You hatched into a warren among narrow tunnels and countless kin. Your body is flexible.

When @Compendium[pf2e.actionspf2e.pprgrYQ1QnIDGZiy]{Climbing} rock walls, stalactites, and other natural stone features, you move at half your Speed on a success and at full Speed on a critical success (and you move at full Speed on a success if you have @Compendium[pf2e.feats-srd.P04Hw8E6WAWARKHP]{Quick Climb}). This doesn't affect you if you're using a climb Speed.

If you roll a success on an Acrobatics check to @Compendium[pf2e.actionspf2e.kMcV8e5EZUxa6evt]{Squeeze}, you get a critical success instead.
