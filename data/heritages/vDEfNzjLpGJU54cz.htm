Name: Quillcoat Shisk Nom: Shisk à piquants État: libre ------ Description (en) ------

Your body has adapted a defensive mechanism to break off your quills in an attacker, allowing you to defend yourself against aggressive predators, though it takes a while for the quills to grow back. You gain the @Compendium[pf2e.actionspf2e.oAWNluJaMlaGysXA]{Barbed Quills} reaction.
