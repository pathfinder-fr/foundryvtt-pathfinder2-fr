Name: Thalassic Azarketi Nom: Azarketi thalassique État: libre ------ Description (en) ------

You trace your lineage from azarketis who lived their lives among the great oceans and seas of the world. You know how to use the currents and the primal magic of water to guide your attacks. You gain the @Compendium[pf2e.feats-srd.iOY6YfGBaOvMNAor]{Underwater Marauder} skill feat, and your piercing ranged attacks don't have their range increments halved when fighting underwater targets.

Vous retracez votre lignage des azarketis qui ont vécu leurs vies parmi les grands océans et les mers du monde.
