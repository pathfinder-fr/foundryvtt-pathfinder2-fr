Name: Powerful Alchemy Nom: Alchimie puissante État: libre ------ Description (en) ------

Alchemical items you create on the fly are particularly potent. When you use @Compendium[pf2e.classfeatures.sPtl05wwTpqFI0lL]{Quick Alchemy} to create an infused alchemical item that allows a saving throw, you can change its DC to your class DC.
