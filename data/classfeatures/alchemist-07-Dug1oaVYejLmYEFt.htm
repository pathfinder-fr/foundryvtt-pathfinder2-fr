Name: Perpetual Infusions (Mutagenist) Nom: Infusions perpétuelles (Mutagèniste) État: officielle ------ Description (en) ------

You have learned how to create perpetual alchemical infusions that can provide a near-infinite supply of certain simple items.

You gain the ability to create two 1st-level alchemical items using @Compendium[pf2e.classfeatures.sPtl05wwTpqFI0lL]{Quick Alchemy} without spending a batch of infused reagents. The items you can select depend on your research field and must be in your formula book.

Choose two of the following formulas:

  • @Compendium[pf2e.equipment-srd.IQK9N2mEOyAj3iWU]{Bestial Mutagen (Lesser)},
  • @Compendium[pf2e.equipment-srd.wbr6rkyaVYnDhdgV]{Cognitive Mutagen (Lesser)},
  • @Compendium[pf2e.equipment-srd.xE0EdDrf734l2fQH]{Juggernaut Mutagen (Lesser)},
  • @Compendium[pf2e.equipment-srd.5MKBwpE401uz4kNN]{Quicksilver Mutagen (Lesser)},
  • @Compendium[pf2e.equipment-srd.bOPQDM54W8ZDoULY]{Serene Mutagen (Lesser)}, or
  • @Compendium[pf2e.equipment-srd.YwRAHWW8yUI07sy9]{Silvertongue Mutagen (Lesser)}
