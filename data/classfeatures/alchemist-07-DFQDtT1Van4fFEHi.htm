Name: Perpetual Infusions (Bomber) Nom: Infusions perpétuelles (artificier) État: officielle ------ Description (en) ------

You have learned how to create perpetual alchemical infusions that can provide a near-infinite supply of certain simple items.

You gain the ability to create two 1st-level alchemical items using @Compendium[pf2e.classfeatures.sPtl05wwTpqFI0lL]{Quick Alchemy} without spending a batch of infused reagents. The items you can select depend on your research field and must be in your formula book.

Choose two of the following formulas:

  • @Compendium[pf2e.equipment-srd.M1k5QQc1qQLxzyCK]{Acid Flask (Lesser)},
  • @Compendium[pf2e.equipment-srd.yd3kEK21YknZLlcT]{Alchemist's Fire (Lesser)},
  • @Compendium[pf2e.equipment-srd.AFR01HVd7DcZvkpP]{Bottled Lightning (Lesser)},
  • @Compendium[pf2e.equipment-srd.EpxmUtLpCkE8R6KJ]{Frost Vial (Lesser)},
  • @Compendium[pf2e.equipment-srd.T6Appwwl6nUl56Xj]{Tanglefoot Bag (Lesser)}, or
  • @Compendium[pf2e.equipment-srd.Xnqglykl3Cif8rN9]{Thunderstone (Lesser)}.
