Name: Maestro Nom: État: aucune ------ Description (en) ------

Your muse is a virtuoso, inspiring you to greater heights. If it’s a creature, it might be a performance-loving creature such as a choral angel or lillend azata; if a deity, it might be Shelyn. As a bard with a maestro muse, you are an inspiration to your allies and confident of your musical and oratorical abilities. You gain the @Item[Lingering Composition] feat and add @Item[Soothe] to your spell repertoire.
