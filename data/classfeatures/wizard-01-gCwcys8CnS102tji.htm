As an abjurer, you master the art of protection, strengthening defenses, preventing attacks, and even turning magic against itself. You understand that an ounce of prevention is worth a pound of cure. You add one 1st-level abjuration spell (such as @Compendium[pf2e.spells-srd.TTwOKGqmZeKSyNMH]{Feather Fall}) to your spellbook.
