Name: Arcane School: Necromancy Nom: Nécromancie État: changé ------ Description (en) ------

As a necromancer, you call upon the powers of life and death. While your school is often vilified for its association with raising the undead, you understand that control over life also means control over healing. You add one 1st-level necromancy spell (such as @Compendium[pf2e.spells-srd.k34hDOfIIMAxNL4a]{Grim Tendrils}) to your spellbook. You learn the @Compendium[pf2e.spells-srd.BH3sUerzMb2bWnv1]{Call of the Grave} school spell.

En tant que nécromancien, vous puisez dans le pouvoir de la vie et de la mort.
