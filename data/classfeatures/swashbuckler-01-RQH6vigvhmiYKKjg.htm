You @Compendium[pf2e.actionspf2e.VjxZFuUXrCU94MWR]{Strike} with flair. When you have panache and you Strike with an agile or finesse melee weapon or agile or finesse unarmed attack, you deal [[/r 2 #precision]] additional precision damage. If the strike is part of a finisher, the additional damage is [[/r 2d6 #precision]] precision damage instead.

As your swashbuckler level increases, so does your additional damage for precise strike. Increase the amount of additional damage on a Strike and the number of additional dice on a finisher by one at 5th, 9th, 13th, and 17th levels.
