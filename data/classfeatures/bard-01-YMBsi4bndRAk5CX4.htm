Your muse is a virtuoso, inspiring you to greater heights. If it's a creature, it might be a performance-loving creature such as a choral angel or lillend azata; if a deity, it might be @Compendium[pf2e.deities.gxwmzxUWBOhdgFSN]{Shelyn}.
