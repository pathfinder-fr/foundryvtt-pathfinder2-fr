Name: Exemplary Finisher Nom: État: aucune ------ Description (en) ------

You execute your finishing moves with spectacular flair, adding special effects to your finishers. If a @Compendium[pf2e.actionspf2e.VjxZFuUXrCU94MWR]{Strike} you make as part of a finisher hits a foe, you add one of the following effects to the Strike, depending on your swashbuckler’s style.

  • Battledancer: You can @Compendium[pf2e.actionspf2e.UHpkTuCtyaPqiCAB]{Step} as a free action immediately after the finisher.
  • Braggart: If the foe was temporarily immune to your @Compendium[pf2e.actionspf2e.2u915NdUyQan6uKF]{Demoralize}, their temporary immunity ends.
  • Fencer: The foe is @Compendium[pf2e.conditionspf2e.AJh5ex99aV6VTggg]{Flat-Footed} until your next turn.
  • Gymnast: If the foe is @Compendium[pf2e.conditionspf2e.kWc1fhmv9LBiTuei]{Grabbed}, @Compendium[pf2e.conditionspf2e.VcDeM8A5oI6VqhbM]{Restrained}, or @Compendium[pf2e.conditionspf2e.j91X7x0XSomq8d60]{Prone}, you gain a circumstance bonus to the damage roll equal to twice the number of weapon damage dice.
  • Wit: The foe takes a –2 circumstance penalty to attack rolls against you until the start of your next turn.
