Name: Desecrator [Neutral Evil] Nom: État: aucune ------ Description (en) ------

You always take what pleases you, no matter who it hurts, and you spread the influence of evil across all you touch. You gain the @Compendium[pf2e.actionspf2e.enQieRrITuEQZxx2]{Selfish Shield} champion’s reaction and the @Compendium[pf2e.spells-srd.jFmWSIpJGGebim6y]{Touch of Corruption} devotion spell. After the tenets of evil, add this tenet.

• Subvert or corrupt everything in your path that is pure and good, and sow doubt among those upholding such ideals.

DIVINE SMITE 9TH

Your champion’s reaction improves as follows.

Desecrator: If your Charisma modifier is +2 or greater, your resistance against the triggering damage equals your Charisma modifier + half your level

EXALT 11TH

Your champion’s reaction improves in the following ways when you gain the exalt class feature at 11th level. These affect enemies rather than allies, as they exalt your dark god to bring pain upon your foes instead of uplifting your companions.

Desecrator: When you use Selfish Shield, each enemy within 15 feet of you takes a –1 status penalty to attack rolls against you until the start of your next turn.
