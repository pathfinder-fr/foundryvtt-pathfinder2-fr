Name: Weapon Expertise (Level 11) Nom: Expertise avec les armes (Niveau 11) État: libre ------ Description (en) ------

Oracle: You've dedicated yourself to learning the intricacies of your weapons. Your proficiency ranks for simple weapons and unarmed attacks increase to expert.

Witch: Through sheer experience, you've improved your technique with your weapons. Your proficiency ranks for simple weapons and unarmed attacks increase to expert.
