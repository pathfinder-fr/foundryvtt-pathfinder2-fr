Granted by @Compendium[pf2e.spells-srd.9kOI14Jep97TzGO7]{Life-Giving Form}

You gain resistance 5 to precision damage and weakness 5 to negative damage, and your unarmed Strikes deal an extra [[/r {1d4}[positive]]]{1d4 positive damage}.

A creature harmed by positive damage (such as an undead) that touches you or damages you with an unarmed attack or non-reach melee weapon instead takes [[/r {3d4}[positive]]]{3d4 positive damage}, and it doesn't become temporarily immune.
