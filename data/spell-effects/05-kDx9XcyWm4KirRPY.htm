Name: Spell Effect: Blessing of Defiance (2 Action Will) Nom: Effet Bénédiction du défi (2 actions Volonté) État: libre ------ Description (en) ------

Granted by @Compendium[pf2e.spells-srd.1b55SgYTV65JvmQd]{Blessing of Defiance}

Channeling forth the power to persevere in the face of adversity, you grant the target the means to deny danger and avoid dire harm. The target gets a +4 status bonus to Reflex saving throws for 1 round. The potency and number of targets of this blessing depend on how many actions you spend when Casting the Spell.

The spell targets you or one willing ally within range. The target gains resistance 15 to mental damage.
