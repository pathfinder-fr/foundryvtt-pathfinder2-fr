Frequency Once per round

Requirement A storm cloud created by Breath Weapon is within 500 feet, and the dragon can see the cloud.

Effect The dragon calls down a lightning bolt from a storm cloud created by its Breath Weapon. This creates a vertical line of lightning to the ground that deal [[/r {6d12}[electricity]]]{6d12 electricity damage} to all enemies in its path (@Check[type:reflex|dc:40|basic:true] save).
