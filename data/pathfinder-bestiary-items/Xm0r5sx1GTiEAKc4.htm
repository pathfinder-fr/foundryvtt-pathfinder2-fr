Parent Name: Mu Spore Name: Cough Spores Nom: Tousser des spores État: officielle ------ Description (en) ------

The mu spore releases a cloud of burrowing spores in a @Template[type:cone|distance:400]. The spores deal [[/r {22d6}[piercing]]]{22d6 piercing damage} to all creatures, objects, and wooden structures in the area, but not to plants or fungi (@Check[type:reflex|dc:46|basic:true] save).

The mu spore can't use this ability again for [[/br 1d4 #Recharge Cough Spores]]{1d4 rounds}.
