Parent Name: Astral Deva Name: Messenger's Amnesty Nom: Amnistie du messager État: officielle ------ Description (en) ------

An astral deva with a message to deliver is continually protected by the effect of @Compendium[pf2e.spells-srd.8xRzLhwGL7Dgy3EZ]{Sanctuary} (DC 32). If the deva breaks the @Compendium[pf2e.spells-srd.8xRzLhwGL7Dgy3EZ]{Sanctuary}, the effect returns if the deva ceases hostility for 10 minutes.
