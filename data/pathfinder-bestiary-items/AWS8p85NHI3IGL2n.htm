Parent Name: Vampire Count Name: Dominate Nom: Domination État: officielle ------ Description (en) ------

The vampire can cast @Compendium[pf2e.spells-srd.OsOhx3TGIZ7AhD0P]{Dominate} at will as a divine innate spell. Casting it requires staring into the target's eyes, giving the spell the visual trait. The save DC uses the DC of the vampire's level, and a creature that succeeds is temporarily immune to that vampire's Dominate for 24 hours.

Fully destroying the vampire ends the domination, but merely reducing the vampire to 0 HP is insufficient to break the spell.
