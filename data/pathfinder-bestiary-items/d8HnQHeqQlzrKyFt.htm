Parent Name: Shaitan Name: Shove into Stone Nom: Poussée dans la pierre État: officielle ------ Description (en) ------

When the shaitan Pushes a creature into a stone barrier, the target must succeed at a @Check[type:reflex|dc:22] save or become merged with the barrier (as @Compendium[pf2e.spells-srd.vh1RpbWfqdNC4L3P]{Meld into Stone}). The victim can attempt to Escape (DC 28).
