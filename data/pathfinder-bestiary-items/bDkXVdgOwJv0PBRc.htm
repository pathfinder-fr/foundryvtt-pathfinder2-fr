Upon Striking a creature with its jaws, the grikkitog can extend its barbed teeth, @Compendium[pf2e.conditionitems.eIcWbB5o3pP6OIMe]{Immobilizing} the target unless it succeeds at a @Check[type:reflex|dc:34] save. While immobilized, the victim takes @Localize[PF2E.PersistentDamage.Bleed3d8.success] and the grikkitog feeds upon its flesh.
