Parent Name: Caligni Stalker Name: Death Flame Nom: Flamme mortelle État: officielle ------ Description (en) ------

When the stalker dies, their body combusts in a flash of white-hot flame. All creatures in a @Template[type:emanation|distance:20] take [[/r {5d6}[fire]]]{5d6 fire damage} (@Check[type:reflex|dc:21|basic:true] save).
