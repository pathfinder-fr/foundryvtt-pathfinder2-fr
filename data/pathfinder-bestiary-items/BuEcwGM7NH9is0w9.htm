Parent Name: Cassisian Name: Eye Beams Nom: Rayons oculaires État: officielle ------ Description (en) ------

The cassisian releases beams of heat or cold from its eyes, dealing [[/r {2d6}[cold]]]{2d6 cold damage} or [[/r {2d6}[fire]]]{2d6 fire damage} (@Check[type:reflex|dc:17|basic:true] save) to all creatures in a @Template[type:line|distance:15]{15-foot line}.

It can't use Eye Beams again for [[/br 1d4 #rounds]]{1d4 rounds}.
