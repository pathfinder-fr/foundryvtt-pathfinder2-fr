Parent Name: Dryad Queen Name: Focus Beauty Nom: Focaliser la beauté État: officielle ------ Description (en) ------

The nymph queen focuses her beauty upon a target within her aura. The creature must attempt a @Check[type:will|dc:30] save. On a failure, it is affected as if by the nymph queen's nymph's beauty aura; if the target was already affected by the dryad queen's beauty, the target suffers the effects of a failed save against @Compendium[pf2e.spells-srd.vLA0q0WOK2YPuJs6]{Charm}. A nymph queen can Focus Beauty on a given creature only once per turn.
