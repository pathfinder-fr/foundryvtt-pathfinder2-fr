A group of six jinkins can work together for an hour to imbue an item with a curse at a range of 60 feet. While this process is lengthy, it's also unobtrusive and can be performed while @Compendium[pf2e.actionspf2e.XMcnh4cSI32tljXa]{Hiding}. Jinkins prefer to use this ability on magic items. The curse makes the item unreliable (@Check[type:flat|dc:5] check or waste any action to Interact with or Activate the item), adds a bizarre requirement to use the item, or imparts some other curse of a similar caliber.
