Parent Name: Young Bronze Dragon Name: Breath Weapon Nom: Arme de souffle État: libre ------ Description (en) ------

The bronze dragon breathes in one of two ways.

The dragon can't use Breath Weapon again for [[/br 1d4 #Recharge Breath Weapon]]{1d4 rounds}.

  • Lightning The dragon breathes lightning in a @Template[type:line|distance:60|traits:arcane,electricity,evocation,damaging-effect]{60-foot line} that deals [[/r {6d12}[electricity]]]{6d12 electricity damage} (@Check[type:reflex|dc:28|basic:true] save).
  • Repulsion Gas The dragon breathes a @Template[type:line|distance:60|traits:arcane,abjuration,incapacitation,mental]{60-foot line} of repulsive gas. Each creature in the area must succeed at a @Check[type:will|dc:28] save or become @Compendium[pf2e.conditionitems.sDPxOjQ9kx2RZE8D]{Fleeing} from the dragon for 1 round (or 2 rounds on a critical failure).
