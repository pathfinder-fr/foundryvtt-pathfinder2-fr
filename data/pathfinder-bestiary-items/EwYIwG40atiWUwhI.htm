Parent Name: Young Gold Dragon Name: Breath Weapon Nom: Arme de souffle État: officielle ------ Description (en) ------

The gold dragon breathes in one of two ways.

The dragon can't use Breath Weapon again for [[/br 1d4 #Recharge Breath Weapon]]{1d4 rounds}.

  • Flame The dragon breathes a blast of flame in a @Template[type:cone|distance:30|traits:arcane,evocation,fire,damaging-effect] that deals [[/r {11d6}[fire]]]{11d6 fire damage} (@Check[type:reflex|dc:31|basic:true] save).
  • Weakening Gas The dragon breathes a blast of weakening gas. Each creature within a @Template[type:cone|distance:30|traits:arcane,necromancy] must succeed at a @Check[type:fortitude|dc:31] save or become @Compendium[pf2e.conditionitems.MIRkyAjyBeXivMa7]{Enfeebled 1} for 1 minute (or @Compendium[pf2e.conditionitems.MIRkyAjyBeXivMa7]{Enfeebled 2} on a critical failure).
