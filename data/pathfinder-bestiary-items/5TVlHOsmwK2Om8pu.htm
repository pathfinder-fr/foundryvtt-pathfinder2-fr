Parent Name: Duskwalker Ghost Hunter Name: Hunt Prey Nom: Chasser une proie État: officielle ------ Description (en) ------

The duskwalker designates as its prey a single creature that it can see and hear or that it is Tracking. It gains a +2 circumstance bonus to Perception checks when it @Compendium[pf2e.actionspf2e.BlAOM2X92SI6HMtJ]{Seeks} its prey and a +2 circumstance bonus to Survival checks when it @Compendium[pf2e.actionspf2e.EA5vuSgJfiHH7plD]{Tracks} its prey. It also ignores the penalty for making ranged attacks within its second range increment against its prey.

Lastly, it gains a +2 circumstance bonus to Deception checks, Intimidation checks, and Stealth checks against its prey and to any check to Recall Knowledge about its prey, as well as a +1 circumstance bonus to AC against its prey's attacks. The duskwalker can have only one prey designated at a time.
