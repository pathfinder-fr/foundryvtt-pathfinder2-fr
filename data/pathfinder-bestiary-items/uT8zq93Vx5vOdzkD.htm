The silver dragon breathes in one of two ways.

The dragon can't use Breath Weapon again for [[/br 1d4 #Recharge Breath Weapon]]{1d4 rounds}.

  • Frost The dragon breathes a cloud of frost in a @Template[type:cone|distance:50|traits:arcane,cold,evocation,damaging-effect] that deals [[/r {20d6}[cold]]]{20d6 cold damage} (@Check[type:reflex|dc:42|basic:true] save).
  • Paralyzing Gas The dragon breathes a blast of paralyzing gas. Each creature within a @Template[type:cone|distance:50|traits:arcane,enchantment,incapacitation] must succeed at a @Check[type:fortitude|dc:42] save or be @Compendium[pf2e.conditionitems.6uEgoh53GbXuHpTF]{Paralyzed} for 1 round (or 3 rounds on a critical failure).
