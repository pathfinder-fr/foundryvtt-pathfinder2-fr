Parent Name: Sea Serpent Name: Spine Rake Nom: Extension des pointes dorsales État: officielle ------ Description (en) ------

The sea serpent extends the spines along its back and Swims or Strides. Each creature the serpent is adjacent to at any point during its movement takes [[/r {4d6+8}[slashing]]]{4d6+8 slashing damage} (@Check[type:reflex|dc:32|basic:true] save).
