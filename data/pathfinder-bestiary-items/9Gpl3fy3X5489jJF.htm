The elemental inferno jumps horizontally and vertically with a maximum height and distance each equal to its Speed. Its intense heat is suppressed until the end of the jump. At any point during the jump, flames explode from the elemental in a @Template[type:emanation|distance:30], dealing [[/r {12d6}[fire]]]{12d6 fire damage} to each creature within the area (@Check[type:reflex|dc:30|basic:true] save).

The elemental inferno can't Inferno Leap again for [[/br 1d4 #Recharge Inferno Leap]]{1d4 rounds}.
