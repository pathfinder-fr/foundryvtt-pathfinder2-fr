Parent Name: Wyvern Name: Powerful Dive Nom: Piqué en puissance État: officielle ------ Description (en) ------

The wyvern Flies up to its fly Speed and must both move forward at least 20 feet and descend at least 10 feet. If it ends the movement within melee reach of at least one enemy its size or smaller, it can make a claw Strike against that enemy. If the claw hits, as a free action the wyvern can either automatically @Compendium[pf2e.bestiary-ability-glossary-srd.Tkd8sH4pwFIPzqTr]{Grab} the target or knock it @Compendium[pf2e.conditionitems.j91X7x0XSomq8d60]{Prone}.
