Requirements The nilith has a creature @Compendium[pf2e.conditionitems.kWc1fhmv9LBiTuei]{Grabbed}.

Effect The nilith reaches into the mind of the grabbed creature and implants disjointed images of the victim's worst fears and nightmares. The grabbed creature takes [[/r {6d6}[mental]]]{6d6 mental damage} (@Check[type:will|dc:31|basic:true] save).
