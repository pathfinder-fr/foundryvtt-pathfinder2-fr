Parent Name: Werebear Name: Hunt Prey Nom: Chasser une proie État: changé État d'origine: officielle ------ Description (en) ------

Requirements The werebear can see or hear the target or is @Compendium[pf2e.actionspf2e.EA5vuSgJfiHH7plD]{Tracking} it in exploration mode.

Effect The werebear gains a +2 circumstance bonus to Perception checks when it @Compendium[pf2e.actionspf2e.BlAOM2X92SI6HMtJ]{Seeks} its prey and a +2 circumstance bonus to Survival checks when it Tracks its prey. The werebear also ignores the penalty for making ranged attacks within its second range increment against the prey it's hunting. The werebear can designate only one creature as its hunted prey at a time. If it uses Hunt Prey against a different creature, the prior creature loses the designation and the new prey gains the designation. In any case, this designation lasts until the werebear's next daily preparation.
