Any creature hit by a roper's strand is @Compendium[pf2e.conditionitems.MIRkyAjyBeXivMa7]{Enfeebled 1} and @Compendium[pf2e.conditionitems.kWc1fhmv9LBiTuei]{Grabbed}. The roper can move while it has a creature grabbed with a strand, but it automatically releases the creature if it moves beyond the strand's reach. The roper can release an grabbed creature as a free action. A creature can sever a strand with a target attack that hits AC 27 and deals at least 18 slashing damage.
