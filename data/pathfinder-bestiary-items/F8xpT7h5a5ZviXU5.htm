Parent Name: Will-o'-Wisp Name: Feed on Fear Nom: Se nourrir de la peur État: changé État d'origine: officielle ------ Description (en) ------

Requirement An enemy is under a fear effect or @Compendium[pf2e.conditionitems.yZRUzMqrMmfLu0V1]{Dying} within 15 feet of the will-o'-wisp.

Effect The will-o'wisp feeds on the creature's terror. It regains [[/r {2d4}[healing]]]{2d4 Hit Points}, and if it has Gone Dark, its glow reignites.

A will-o'-wisp can take this action only once per round.

------ Description (fr) ------
