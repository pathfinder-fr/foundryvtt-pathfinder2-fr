Parent Name: Demilich Name: Contingency Nom: Contingence État: officielle ------ Description (en) ------

A demilich has one permanent 8th-level @Compendium[pf2e.spells-srd.WG91Z5TiR6oO5FOw]{Contingency} spell in effect with one of its arcane innate spells of 5th level or lower as the companion spell-typically @Compendium[pf2e.spells-srd.VlNcjmYyu95vOUe8]{Dimension Door}.

Trigger While the lich is in torpor, a creature disturbs the demilich's remains, touches its treasure, or casts a spell that would affect the demilich.

Effect The demilich ends torpor, rolls initiative, and gains the effect of its contingency's companion spell. The contingency resets after 24 hours.
