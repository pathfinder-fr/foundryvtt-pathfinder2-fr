Parent Name: Ancient Blue Dragon Name: Desert Thirst Nom: Soif du désert État: officielle ------ Description (en) ------

When casting @Compendium[pf2e.spells-srd.WzLKjSw6hsBhuklC]{Create Water}, the dragon can attempt to destroy liquid instead of creating it, turning an equal amount of liquid into sand. This destroys liquid magic or alchemical items if they're of a lower level than the dragon (a creature can attempt a @Check[type:will|dc:37] save to protect all liquids in its possession). This doesn't affect the liquids in a creature's body.

------ Description (fr) ------

Quand il incante @Compendium[pf2e.spells-srd.WzLKjSw6hsBhuklC]{Création d’eau} le dragon peut tenter de détruire des liquides au lieu d’en créer, transformant en sable un volume équivalent de substance.
