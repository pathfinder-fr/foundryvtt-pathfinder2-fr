Whenever the shambler would take electricity damage or is targeted with an electricity effect, it gains 12 temporary HP and is @Compendium[pf2e.conditionitems.nlCjDvLMf2EkV2dl]{Quickened} until the end of its next turn. It can use its extra action to Stride, Strike, or Swim.
