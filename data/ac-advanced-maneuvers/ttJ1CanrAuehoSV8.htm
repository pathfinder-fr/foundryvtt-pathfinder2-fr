Name: Death Roll Nom: Roulade mortelle État: libre ------ Description (en) ------

Requirements The crocodile has a creature grabbed.

The crocodile tucks its legs and rolls rapidly, twisting its victim. It makes a jaws Strike with a +2 circumstance bonus to the attack roll against the grabbed creature. If it hits, it also knocks the creature @Compendium[pf2e.conditionitems.j91X7x0XSomq8d60]{Prone}. If it misses, it releases the creature.
