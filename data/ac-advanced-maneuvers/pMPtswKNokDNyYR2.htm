Name: Defensive Curl Nom: Posture défensive État: libre ------ Description (en) ------

The pangolin coils into a ball to defend itself. It gains a +2 circumstance bonus to AC. Any creature that hits the pangolin with a melee unarmed attack while it's in its Defensive Curl take [[/r {2d6}[persistent,bleed]]]{2d6} @Compendium[pf2e.conditionitems.Persistent Damage]{Persistent bleed Damage}. The pangolin can't move, use its unarmed attacks, or take other actions that require it to use its limbs while in its Defensive Curl, but it can unfurl as a single action, ending the effects of Defensive Curl.
