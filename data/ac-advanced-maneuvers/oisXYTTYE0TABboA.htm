The Strike’s target takes 1d6 @Compendium[pf2e.conditionitems.lDVqvLKA6eF3Df60]{Persistent Damage} bleed damage and takes a –10-foot status penalty to its Speed until it removes the bleed damage. If the hyena is a specialized animal companion, the persistent bleed damage increases to 2d6.
