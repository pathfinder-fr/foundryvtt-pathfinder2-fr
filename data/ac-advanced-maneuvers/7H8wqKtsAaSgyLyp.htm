Name: Grab and Sting Nom: Saisir et piquer État: libre ------ Description (en) ------

The scorpion holds its foe in place with its pincers in order to sting it. It makes a pincer Strike. If the Strike hits, the target creature automatically becomes @Compendium[pf2e.conditionitems.kWc1fhmv9LBiTuei]{Grabbed} by the scorpion, which then makes a stinger Strike against it. The grabbed condition lasts until the end of your next turn.
