Name: Euryale (curse) Nom: Euryale (malédiction) État: officielle ------ Description (en) ------

A medusa's image curses you with a permanent -1 status penalty to all saving throws. This can be removed by only a deity or The Fates card.

Effect: Euryale (Curse) Card
