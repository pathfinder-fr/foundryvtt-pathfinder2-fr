Name: Died Nom: Mort État: officielle ------ Description (en) ------

You died or came so close that you walked the boundary between life and death.

Add the @Compendium[pf2e.feats-srd.I0BhPWqYf1bbzEYg]{Diehard} feat to your background options. If you select it instead of a skill feat, you aren't trained in a background skill.
