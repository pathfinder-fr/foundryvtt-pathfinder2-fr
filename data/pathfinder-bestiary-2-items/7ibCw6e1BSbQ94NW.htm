A creature that takes damage from a lurker in light's mote of light must attempt a @Check[type:will|dc:22] save.

Success The creature is unaffected and is temporarily immune to lurker's glow for 24 hours.

Failure The creature is lined in golden light for 1 minute and can't be @Compendium[pf2e.conditionitems.DmAIPqOBomZ7H95W]{Concealed} during this time. If the creature becomes @Compendium[pf2e.conditionitems.zJxUflt9np0q4yML]{Invisible}, it is concealed rather than being @Compendium[pf2e.conditionitems.VRSef5y1LmL2Hkjf]{Undetected}.

Critical Failure As failure, but the creature sheds bright light in a 20-foot emanation for the duration of the effect. In addition, the creature is @Compendium[pf2e.conditionitems.TkIyaNPgTZFBCCuh]{Dazzled} on its first turn after failing this save.

------ Description (fr) ------

Échec critique Même effet que l'échec, mais la créature diffuse une lumière vive dans une émanation de 6 mètres pendant toute la durée de l'effet. De plus, la créature est @Compendium[pf2e.conditionitems.TkIyaNPgTZFBCCuh]{Éblouie} durant son premier tour après l'échec de son jet de sauvegarde.
