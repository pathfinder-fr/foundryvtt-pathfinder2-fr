Parent Name: Adult Crystal Dragon Name: Crystallize Flesh Nom: Cristallisation de la chair État: officielle ------ Description (en) ------

Trigger The crystal dragon damages a creature made of flesh with a jaws Strike

Frequency once per day

Effect The dragon embeds transformative crystals in the creature's flesh. The creature must attempt a @Check[type:fortitude|dc:30] save.

Critical Success The target is unaffected.

Success The target is @Compendium[pf2e.conditionitems.xYTAsEpcJE1Ccni3]{Slowed 1} for 1 round as portions of its flesh turn crystalline.

Failure The target is @Compendium[pf2e.conditionitems.xYTAsEpcJE1Ccni3]{Slowed 1} and must attempt a Fortitude save at the end of each of its turns; this ongoing save has the incapacitation trait. On a failed save, the slowed condition value increases by 1 (or by 2 on a critical failure). A successful save reduces the slowed condition value by 1. A creature unable to act due to the slowed condition from Crystallize Flesh is @Compendium[pf2e.conditionitems.dTwPJuKgBQCMxixg]{Petrified} permanently, transforming into a crystalline statue. The effect ends if the creature is petrified or the slowed condition is removed.

Critical Failure As failure, but the target is initially @Compendium[pf2e.conditionitems.xYTAsEpcJE1Ccni3]{Slowed 2}.

Fréquence une fois par jour

Une créature incapable d’agir à cause de l’état ralenti provoqué par ce pouvoir est @Compendium[pf2e.conditionitems.dTwPJuKgBQCMxixg]{Pétrifiée} de manière permanente et se transforme en statue de cristal. L’effet se termine quand la créature est pétrifiée ou débarrassée de l’état ralenti.

Échec critique Comme l’échec, mais la cible est initialement @Compendium[pf2e.conditionitems.xYTAsEpcJE1Ccni3]{Ralentie 2}.
