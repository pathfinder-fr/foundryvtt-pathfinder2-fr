Parent Name: Fetchling Scout Name: Sneak Attack Nom: Attaque sournoise État: officielle ------ Description (en) ------

The fetchling scout's Strikes deal an additional 1d6 precision damage to @Compendium[pf2e.conditionitems.AJh5ex99aV6VTggg]{Flat-Footed} creatures.

When the monster Strikes a creature that has the flat-footed condition with an agile or finesse melee weapon, an agile or finesse unarmed attack, or a ranged weapon attack, it also deals the listed precision damage. For a ranged attack with a thrown weapon, that weapon must also be an agile or finesse weapon.
