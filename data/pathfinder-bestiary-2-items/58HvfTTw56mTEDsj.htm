Parent Name: Adult Umbral Dragon (Spellcaster) Name: Breath Weapon Nom: Arme de souffle État: officielle ------ Description (en) ------

The umbral dragon breathes in one of two ways. They can't use Breath Weapon again for [[/br 1d4 #rounds]]{1d4 rounds}.

  • Negative The dragon breathes a blast of darkness in a @Template[type:cone|distance:40] that deals [[/r {16d6}[negative]]]{16d6 negative damage} (@Check[type:reflex|dc:36|basic:true] save). Undead creatures take [[/r {19d6}[force]]]{19d6 force damage} instead of the negative damage.
  • Shadows The dragon breathes a blast of shadows in a @Template[type:cone|distance:40]. Each creature within the cone must attempt a @Check[type:fortitude|dc:36] save.
    • Critical Success The creature is unaffected.
    • Success The creature is @Compendium[pf2e.conditionitems.MIRkyAjyBeXivMa7]{Enfeebled 2} for 1 round.
    • Failure The creature is @Compendium[pf2e.conditionitems.MIRkyAjyBeXivMa7]{Enfeebled 2} for 1 minute.
    • Critical Failure The creature is @Compendium[pf2e.conditionitems.MIRkyAjyBeXivMa7]{Enfeebled 2} for 1 minute and @Compendium[pf2e.conditionitems.XgEqL1kFApUbl5Z2]{Blinded} for 1 round.
