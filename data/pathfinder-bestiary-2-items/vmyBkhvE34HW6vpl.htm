Parent Name: Vampire Squid Name: Glowing Mucus Nom: Mucus luminescent État: officielle ------ Description (en) ------

Frequency once per day

Requirements The vampire squid is in water

Effect The vampire squid ejects a cloud of bioluminescent mucus in a @Template[type:cone|distance:15]. Non-squid creatures within the cloud must attempt a @Check[type:fortitude|dc:16] save or become @Compendium[pf2e.conditionitems.fesd1n5eVhpCSS18]{Sickened 1}. The glowing mucus remains in the area for 1 minute, and any creature that ends its turn in the area must succeed at a @Check[type:fortitude|dc:13] save or become @Compendium[pf2e.conditionitems.fesd1n5eVhpCSS18]{Sickened 1}.
