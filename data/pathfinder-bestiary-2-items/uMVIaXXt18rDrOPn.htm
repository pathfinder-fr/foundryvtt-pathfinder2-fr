The ravener breathes a blast of flame that deals [[/r {20d6}[fire]]]{20d6 fire damage} plus [[/r {4d6}[persistent,negative]]] @Compendium[pf2e.conditionitems.lDVqvLKA6eF3Df60]{Persistent Negative Damage} (@Check[type:reflex|dc:44|basic:true] save).
