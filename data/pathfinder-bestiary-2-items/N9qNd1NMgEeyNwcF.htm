A spellcaster who targets a devourer with a mental spell, @Compendium[pf2e.spells-srd.bay4AfSu2iIozNNW]{Banishment}, @Compendium[pf2e.spells-srd.GYmXvS9NJ7QwfWGg]{Bind Soul}, @Compendium[pf2e.spells-srd.sX2o0HH4RjJDAZ8C]{Divine Decree}, @Compendium[pf2e.spells-srd.hVU9msO9yGkxKZ3J]{Divine Wrath}, @Compendium[pf2e.spells-srd.wU6hNzK8Yfqdmc8m]{Possession}, @Compendium[pf2e.spells-srd.PHVHBbdHeQRfjLmE]{Spirit Blast}, or @Compendium[pf2e.spells-srd.Ht35SDf9PDStJfoC]{Spirit Song} can attempt a counteract check to free a soul the devourer has trapped with Devour Soul.
