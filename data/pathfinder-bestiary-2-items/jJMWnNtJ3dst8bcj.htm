When the dragon deals negative damage to a living creature with its jaws Strike, the umbral dragon gains 20 temporary Hit Points and the creature must succeed at a @Check[type:fortitude|dc:41] save or become @Compendium[pf2e.conditionitems.MIRkyAjyBeXivMa7]{Enfeebled 2}.
