Parent Name: Water Orm Name: Slow Metabolism Nom: Métabolisme lent État: officielle ------ Description (en) ------

A water orm can go for 10 years without feeding. Beyond this limit, the water orm's hunger causes it to become @Compendium[pf2e.conditionitems.xYTAsEpcJE1Ccni3]{Slowed 1}, but doesn't otherwise impact its lifespan. A water orm that's slowed as a result of starvation can remove this condition by using Swallow Whole to gulp down a meal.

------ Description (fr) ------

Un orm aquatique peut passer 10 ans sans se nourrir. Au-delà de cette limite, la faim qu’il ressent le fait devenir @Compendium[pf2e.conditionitems.xYTAsEpcJE1Ccni3]{Ralenti 1}, mais n’a pas d’autre impact sur son espérance de vie.
