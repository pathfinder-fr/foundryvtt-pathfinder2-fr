Parent Name: Giant Crawling Hand Name: Mark Quarry Nom: Proie marquée État: officielle ------ Description (en) ------

A crawling hand can be assigned a quarry by anointing the hand with a drop of the intended quarry's blood. If the hand ever has no quarry, it automatically gains the next creature it damages as its quarry. The hand gains a +1 circumstance bonus to Perception checks when it @Compendium[pf2e.actionspf2e.BlAOM2X92SI6HMtJ]{Seeks} its quarry, to Survival checks when it @Compendium[pf2e.actionspf2e.EA5vuSgJfiHH7plD]{Tracks} its quarry, and damage rolls when it Strikes its quarry.

La main gagne un bonus de circonstances de +1 à ses tests de Perception lorsqu’elle @Compendium[pf2e.actionspf2e.BlAOM2X92SI6HMtJ]{Cherche} sa proie, à ses tests de Survie lorsqu’elle la @Compendium[pf2e.actionspf2e.EA5vuSgJfiHH7plD]{Piste} et à ses jets de dégâts lorsqu’elle la Frappe.
