Parent Name: Nuglub Name: Sneak Attack Nom: Attaque sournoise État: officielle ------ Description (en) ------

A nuglub's Strikes deal an additional [[/r {1d6}[precision]]]{1d6 precision damage} to @Compendium[pf2e.conditionitems.AJh5ex99aV6VTggg]{Flat-Footed} targets, or [[/r {1d10}[precision]]]{1d10 precision damage} if the target is @Compendium[pf2e.conditionitems.j91X7x0XSomq8d60]{Prone}.

When the monster Strikes a creature that has the flat-footed condition with an agile or finesse melee weapon, an agile or finesse unarmed attack, or a ranged weapon attack, it also deals the listed precision damage. For a ranged attack with a thrown weapon, that weapon must also be an agile or finesse weapon.
