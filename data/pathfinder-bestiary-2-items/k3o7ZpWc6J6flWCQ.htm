Parent Name: Orca Name: Breach Nom: Brèche État: officielle ------ Description (en) ------

The orca Swims up to its swim Speed, then @Compendium[pf2e.actionspf2e.d5I6018Mci2SWokk]{Leap} vertically out of the water up to 25 feet in the air, making a Strike against a creature at any point during the jump (this lets it attack a creature within 30 feet of the water's surface).
