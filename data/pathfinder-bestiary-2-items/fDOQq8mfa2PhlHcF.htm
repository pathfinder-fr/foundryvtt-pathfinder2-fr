Parent Name: Yellow Musk Creeper Name: Bore into Brain Nom: Forage de cerveau État: changé État d'origine: officielle ------ Description (en) ------

The creeper bores dozens of tendrils into the brain of a Small, Medium, or Large humanoid creature that's @Compendium[pf2e.conditionitems.fBnFDH2MTzgFijKf]{Unconscious}, willing, or @Compendium[pf2e.conditionitems.AdPVz7rbaVSRxHFg]{Fascinated} by Spray Pollen, and within reach of the creeper's tendrils.

The creature must succeed at a @Check[type:fortitude|dc:18|traits:humanoid] save or become @Compendium[pf2e.conditionitems.e1XGnhKNSQIm5IXg]{Stupefied 1} (@Compendium[pf2e.conditionitems.e1XGnhKNSQIm5IXg]{Stupefied 2} on a critical failure).

Subsequent failed saves against Bore into Brain increase the stupefied value. If the creature reaches stupefied 5, it is turned into a yellow musk thrall. Boring into a creature's brain doesn't end fascination caused by Spray Pollen.

La vigne fait pénétrer des dizaines de vrilles dans le cerveau d’une créature de taille Petite, Moyenne ou Grande, qui est @Compendium[pf2e.conditionitems.fBnFDH2MTzgFijKf]{Inconsciente}, consentante ou Compendium[pf2e.conditionitems.AdPVz7rbaVSRxHFg]{fascinée} par Projection de pollen, et qui se trouve à portée de ses vrilles.
