When a full moon appears in the night sky, the werecreature must enter hybrid form, can't Change Shape thereafter, becomes one size larger, increases their reach by 5 feet, and increases the damage of their jaws Strike (or a similar Strike) by 2. When the moon sets or the sun rises, the werecreature returns to humanoid form and is @Compendium[pf2e.conditionitems.HL2l2VRSaQHu9lUw]{Fatigued} for [[/br 2d4 #hours]] hours.
