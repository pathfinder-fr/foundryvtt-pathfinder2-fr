  • Drain Vitality The urdefhan draws out some of the creature's vital essence. The creature becomes @Compendium[pf2e.conditionitems.MIRkyAjyBeXivMa7]{Enfeebled 1} for 1 hour on a failed save (or @Compendium[pf2e.conditionitems.MIRkyAjyBeXivMa7]{Enfeebled 2} for 1 hour on a critical failure).
