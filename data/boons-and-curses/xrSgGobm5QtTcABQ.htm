Name: Pharasma - Moderate Boon Nom: Pharasma - Bénédiction modérée État: changé État d'origine: libre ------ Description (en) ------

Moderate Boon You gain a greater mastery over the energies of life and death.

Your Strikes deal 1 positive damage to undead.

Your spells that deal positive damage to undead gain a +1 status bonus to damage per spell level, and your spells that heal the living gain a +1 status bonus to the Hit Points restored per spell level.
