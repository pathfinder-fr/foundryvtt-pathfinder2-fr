Moderate Boon You share some of the bravery Cayden espoused during his ascension.

When you roll a success on a saving throw against a fear effect, you get a critical success instead. If you have the fighter @Compendium[pf2e.classfeatures.GJKJafDGuX4BeAeN]{Bravery} class feature, when you roll a critical failure on a save against a fear effect, you get a failure instead.
