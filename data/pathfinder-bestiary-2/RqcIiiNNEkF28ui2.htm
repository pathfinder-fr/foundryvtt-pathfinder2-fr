Name: Orca Nom: Orque Épaulard État: libre ------ Data ------ SensesEN: aquatic echolocation 120 feet, low-light vision SensesFR: écholocalisation aquatique 36 m, vision nocturne ------ Description (en) ------

While many know orcas as "killer whales," they are actually the largest species of dolphin. These powerful animals hunt together in pods to take down seals, sharks, and even true whales. Adult orcas are typically 15-25 feet long and weigh 8,000-12,000 pounds.

Ces animaux puissants chassent ensemble en groupes pour abattre des phoques, des requins et même de vraies baleines.
