Name: Wayfinder, Rugged Nom: État: aucune ------ Description (en) ------

[Item]

Prerequisites: Tier 2 with the Horizon Hunters; You own a @Compendium[pf2e.equipment-srd.gbwr57aT9ou8yKWT]{Wayfinder}

Cost: 2 Fame

Action: Single Action

Effect: You have modified your @Compendium[pf2e.equipment-srd.gbwr57aT9ou8yKWT]{Wayfinder} to absorb a small amount of your exhaustion, allowing you to continue exploring unhindered. When you slot this boon, you apply its benefits to one @Compendium[pf2e.equipment-srd.gbwr57aT9ou8yKWT]{Wayfinder} in your possession, granting you a special ability when the @Compendium[pf2e.equipment-srd.gbwr57aT9ou8yKWT]{Wayfinder} is invested and in your possession.

As an action, you can reduce the severity of your @Compendium[pf2e.conditionspf2e.i3OJZU2nk64Df3xm]{Clumsy}, @Compendium[pf2e.conditionspf2e.MIRkyAjyBeXivMa7]{Enfeebled}, or @Compendium[pf2e.conditionspf2e.fesd1n5eVhpCSS18]{Sickened} condition, reducing that condition’s value by 1.

You can safely use this benefit once per adventure. You can attempt to use it a second time, overcharging the @Compendium[pf2e.equipment-srd.gbwr57aT9ou8yKWT]{Wayfinder} at the risk of destroying it. When you do so, roll a DC 10 flat check. On a success, the @Compendium[pf2e.equipment-srd.gbwr57aT9ou8yKWT]{Wayfinder} is broken. On a failure, the wayfinder is destroyed. If anyone tries to overcharge a @Compendium[pf2e.equipment-srd.gbwr57aT9ou8yKWT]{Wayfinder} that’s already been overcharged, the item is automatically destroyed (even if it has been repaired) and does not provide the activated benefit.
