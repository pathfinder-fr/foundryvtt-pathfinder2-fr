Name: Skulltaker Nom: Volecrâne État: officielle ------ Data ------ LanguagesEN: Skeletal Lore Languages LanguagesFR: langues liées aux Connaissances squelettiques SavesEN: +1 status to all saves vs. positive SavesFR: bonus de statut de +1 à tous les jds contre positif SensesEN: darkvision; true seeing SensesFR: vision dans le noir, vision lucide ------ Description (en) ------

Swirling down from misty peaks and through howling mountain passes like an evil wind, the vortex of bones known as a skulltaker is a terrible manifestation of the delirium and agony experienced by doomed climbers and lost trailblazers just before they met their end. In some places, a skulltaker is also known as a saxra.
