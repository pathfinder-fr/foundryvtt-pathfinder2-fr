Name: Vanishing Coin Nom: Pièce d'invisibilité État: changé État d'origine: officielle ------ Description (en) ------

This copper coin dangles from a leather strip strung through a hole drilled in the center. Until activated, the coin becomes invisible for a few seconds at random intervals every few minutes. When you activate the coin, it casts a 2nd-level @Compendium[pf2e.spells-srd.XXqE1eY3w3z6xJCB]{Invisibility} spell on you, lasting until the end of your next turn.

Activate F Envision; Trigger You attempt a Stealth check for initiative, but you haven't rolled yet; Requirements You are a master in Stealth
