Name: Puff Dragon Nom: Dragon bouffée État: libre ------ Description (en) ------

This cute and unassuming dragon toy activates once a creature moves into its square. It then unleashes a rapid burst of toxic gas in a @Template[type:emanation|distance:10]{10-foot emanation}. Those within the emanation when the snare is activated must attempt a @Check[type:fortitude|dc:25] Fortitude saving throw or take [[/r {3d6}[poison]]]{3d6 Poison Damage}.

Critical Success The creature is unaffected.

Success The creature takes half damage.

Failure The creature takes full damage and is @Compendium[pf2e.conditionitems.fesd1n5eVhpCSS18]{Sickened 1}.

Critical Failure The creature takes double damage and is @Compendium[pf2e.conditionitems.fesd1n5eVhpCSS18]{Sickened 2}.

Ce jouet dragon mignon et discret s'active dès qu'une créature se déplace dans sa case. Échec La créature subit la totalité des dégâts et est @Compendium[pf2e.conditionitems.fesd1n5eVhpCSS18]{Malade 1}.
