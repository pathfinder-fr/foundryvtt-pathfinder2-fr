This weapon has a wooden shaft ending in a large, heavy metal head. Thehead of the hammer might be single‑sided or double‑sided, but it’s always capable of delivering powerful bludgeoning blows.

This weapon has a +1 weapon potency rune (+1 item bonus to attack rolls with the weapon) and a striking rune (one extra damage die).
