Name: Halberd (+1 Striking) Nom: État: aucune ------ Description (en) ------

This polearm has a relatively short, 5‑foot shaft. The business end is a long spike with an axe blade attached.

This weapon has a +1 weapon potency rune (+1 item bonus to attack rolls with the weapon) and a striking rune (one extra damage die).
