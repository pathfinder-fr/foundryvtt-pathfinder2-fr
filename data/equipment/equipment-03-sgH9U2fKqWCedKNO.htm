Name: Skinsaw Mask Nom: Masque de l'écorcheur État: libre ------ Description (en) ------

A patchwork of humanoid flesh makes up this grotesque mask stitched together with black silk or wire. It is distinctive for its bulbous orange eye- crafted from a magical glass bauble-and wide row of teeth.

When worn, the mask amplifies your ability to sense fear in other creatures.

You automatically know the value of the @Compendium[pf2e.conditionitems.TBSHQspnbcqxsmjL]{Frightened} condition of any @Compendium[pf2e.conditionitems.1wQY3JYyhMYeeV2G]{Observed} creature, and you gain a +1 item bonus to Perception checks to @Compendium[pf2e.actionspf2e.BlAOM2X92SI6HMtJ]{Seek} frightened creatures. Whenever you deal precision damage to a frightened creature, you deal 1 additional precision damage.

If you are not evil, you are @Compendium[pf2e.conditionitems.4D2KBtexWXa6oUMR]{Drained 2} while wearing theSkinsaw mask.

Automatically applied effects:

  • +1 item bonus to Perception checks to Seek frightened creatures
