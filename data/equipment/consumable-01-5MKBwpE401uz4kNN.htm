Name: Quicksilver Mutagen (Lesser) Nom: Mutagène de Vif-argent (inférieur) État: changé État d'origine: officielle ------ Description (en) ------

Activate A Interact

Your features become thin and angular. You become swifter and nimbler, but your body also becomes fragile.

Benefit You gain a +1 item bonus to Acrobatics checks, Stealth checks, Thievery checks, Reflex saves, and ranges attack rolls, and you gain a +5 foot status bonus to your Speed.

Drawback You take damage equal to twice your level; you can't recover Hit Points lost this way by any means while the mutagen lasts. You take a -2 penalty to Fortitude saves.

Duration 1 minute.
