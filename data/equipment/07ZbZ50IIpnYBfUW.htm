Name: Light Pick (+1) Nom: Pic de guerre léger État: libre ------ Description (en) ------

A light pick is a modified mining implement with a wooden shaft ending in a pick head crafted more to pierce armor and flesh than chip rocks.

This weapon has a +1 weapon potency rune (+1 item bonus to attack rolls with the weapon).
