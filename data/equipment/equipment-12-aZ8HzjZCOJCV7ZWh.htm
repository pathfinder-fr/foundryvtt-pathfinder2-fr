Name: Marvelous Medicines Nom: Remèdes merveilleux État: changé État d'origine: officielle ------ Description (en) ------

This set of healer’s tools contains a seemingly endless supply of bandages, herbs, and healing items of impeccable quality, granting you a +2 item bonus to Medicine checks.

Activate A Treat Poison or 8 hours (Treat Disease); Effect You can activate the tools when you use them to Treat Poison in order to produce the effects of @Compendium[pf2e.spells-srd.SUKaxVZW2TlM8lu0]{Neutralize Poison}, or when you Treat Disease in order to produce the effects of @Compendium[pf2e.spells-srd.RneiyehRO6f7LP44]{Remove Disease}. Once these medicines have been used to treat a patient’s poison or disease, they can’t be used again to treat the same affliction for that patient.
