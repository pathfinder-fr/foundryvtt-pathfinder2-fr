Activate 1 Strike

A thick, sulfurous, irritating gas fills this golden‑yellow flask. You gain a +3 item bonus to attack rolls. The bomb deals [[/r {4d4}[acid]]]{4d4 acid damage} and [[/r {4}[acid]]]{4 acid damage}. On a hit, the target takes a -1 status penalty to Perception checks and attack rolls until the end of its next turn, or becomes @Compendium[pf2e.conditionitems.fesd1n5eVhpCSS18]{Sickened 1} on a critical hit.
