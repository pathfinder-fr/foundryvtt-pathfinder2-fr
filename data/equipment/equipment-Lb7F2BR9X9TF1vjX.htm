Name: Thundering (Greater) Nom: État: aucune ------ Description (en) ------

This weapon lets out a peal of thunder when it hits, dealing an extra 1d6 sonic damage on a successful Strike. On a critical hit, the target has to succeed at a DC 34 Fortitude save or be deafened for 1 minute (or 1 hour on a critical failure). Sonic damage dealt by this weapon ignores the target’s sonic resistance.
