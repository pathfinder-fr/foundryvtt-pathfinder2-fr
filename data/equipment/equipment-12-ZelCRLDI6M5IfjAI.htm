Name: Cloak of Elvenkind (Greater) Nom: Cape elfique supérieure État: officielle ------ Description (en) ------

This cloak is deep green with a voluminous hood, embroidered with gold trim and symbols significant in elven culture. The cloak allows you to cast the @Compendium[pf2e.spells-srd.atlgGNI1E1Ox3O3a]{Ghost Sound} cantrip as an arcane innate spell.

When you adjust the cloak's clasp (an Interact action), the cloak transforms to match the environment around you and muffles your sounds, granting you a +2 item bonus to Stealth checks.
