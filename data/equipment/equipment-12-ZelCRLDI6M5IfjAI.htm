Name: Cloak of Elvenkind (Greater) Nom: État: aucune ------ Description (en) ------

This cloak is deep green with a voluminous hood, embroidered with gold trim and symbols significant in elven culture. The cloak allows you to cast the @Compendium[pf2e.spells-srd.atlgGNI1E1Ox3O3a]{Ghost Sound} cantrip as an arcane innate spell. When you adjust the cloak’s clasp (an Interact action), the cloak transforms to match the environment around you and muffles your sounds, granting you a +2 item bonus to Stealth checks.

Activate Interact; Frequency once per day; Effect You draw the hood up and gain the effects of a 4th-level @Compendium[pf2e.spells-srd.XXqE1eY3w3z6xJCB]{Invisibility}, with the spell’s normal duration or until you pull the hood back down, whichever comes first. If you’re also wearing @Compendium[pf2e.equipment-srd.T00Xa9aDwHxd60Zh]{Boots of Elvenkind}, you can activate this ability twice per day, and it allows you to Sneak in forest environments even when creatures are currently observing you.
