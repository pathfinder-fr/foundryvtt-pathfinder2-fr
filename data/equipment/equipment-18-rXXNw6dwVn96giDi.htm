Name: Goggles of Night (Major) Nom: Lunettes de nyctalope majeur État: officielle ------ Description (en) ------

The opaque crystal lenses of these sleek goggles do not obscure vision, but rather enhance it. While wearing the goggles, you gain a +3 item bonus to Perception checks involving sight.

Activate A Interact

Frequency once per day

Effect Rotating the lenses 90 degrees, you gain greater darkvision until you rotate the lenses back or the item is no longer invested by you, whichever comes first.

Automatically applied effects:

  • +3 item bonus to Perception checks involving sight
