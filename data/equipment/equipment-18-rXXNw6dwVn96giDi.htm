Name: Goggles of Night (Major) Nom: Lunettes de nyctalope (majeures) État: changé État d'origine: officielle ------ Description (en) ------

The opaque crystal lenses of these sleek goggles do not obscure vision, but rather enhance it. While wearing the goggles, you gain a +3 item bonus to Perception checks involving sight.

Activate Interact; Frequency once per day; Effect Rotating the lenses 90 degrees, you gain greater darkvision until you rotate the lenses back or the item is no longer invested by you, whichever comes first.
