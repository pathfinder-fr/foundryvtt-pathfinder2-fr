Name: Maestro's Instrument (Lesser) Nom: Instrument du maestro (inférieur) État: changé État d'origine: officielle ------ Description (en) ------

A maestro's instrument can be crafted in the form of any variety of handheld musical instrument. A maestro's instrument grants you a +1 item bonus to Performance checks while playing music with the instrument.

Activate D Interact

Frequency once per day

Effect You play the instrument, causing it to cast a DC 17 @Compendium[pf2e.spells-srd.vLA0q0WOK2YPuJs6]{Charm} spell.

Craft Requirements You must supply a casting of charm of the appropriate level.

Automatically applied effects:

  • +1 item bonus to Performance checks while playing music with the instrument
