Name: Arrow-Catching Shield Nom: Bouclier intercepteur de projectiles État: changé État d'origine: officielle ------ Description (en) ------

This wooden shield (Hardness 6, HP 24, BT 12) is carved with images of overlapping fletched arrows.

Activate R Interact; Trigger A ranged weapon Strike targets a creature within 15 feet of you when you have this shield raised, and the attacker has not yet rolled their attack; Effect The triggering Strike targets you instead of its normal target. If it hits, you gain the effects of the Shield Block reaction.
