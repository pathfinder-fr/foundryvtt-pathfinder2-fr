Name: Bestial Mutagen (Lesser) Nom: Mutgène bestial État: changé État d'origine: officielle ------ Description (en) ------

Your features transform into something bestial and you take on muscle mass, but your lumbering form is clumsy.

Benefit You gain a +1 item bonus, your claw deals 1d4 slashing damage, your jaws deal 1d6 piercing damage, and the duration is 1 minute.

Drawback You take a –1 penalty to AC and a –2 penalty to Reflex saves.

Activate Interact
