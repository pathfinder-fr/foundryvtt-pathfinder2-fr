Name: Druid's Vestments Nom: Chasuble de druide État: changé État d'origine: officielle ------ Description (en) ------

This brown-and-green tunic is embroidered with patterns that resemble interlocking elk antlers. You gain a +2 item bonus to Nature checks.

Activate F envision; Frequency once per day; Effect You gain 1 Focus Point, which you can spend only to cast an order spell. If you don’t spend this Focus Point by the end of this turn, it is lost.

Craft Requirements You are a druid.

Automatically applied effects:

  • +2 item bonus to Nature checks
