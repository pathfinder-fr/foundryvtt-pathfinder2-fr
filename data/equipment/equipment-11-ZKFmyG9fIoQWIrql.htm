These folding, wood-framed spectacles hold flat discs of cut crystal as lenses and are held in place with thin cords that wrap around each ear, with another strap holding them around the neck while not in use. While you are wearing the spectacles, you gain a +2 item bonus to checks to Decipher Writing.

Activate A Interact;

Frequency once per day;

Effect Unfolding the spectacles onto the bridge of the nose, you gain the effects of 2nd-level @Compendium[pf2e.spells-srd.vTQvfYu2llKQedmY]{Comprehend Language} until you fold up the spectacles or the item is no longer invested by you, whichever comes first, except that it applies to all languages you see rather than a single language, and it does not apply to language you hear.
