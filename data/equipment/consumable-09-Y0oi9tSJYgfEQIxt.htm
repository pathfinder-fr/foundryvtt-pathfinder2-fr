Ammunition Arrow

Activate 1 Interact

This arrow splits at the far end of the shaft into five branching arrowheads, making it impractical for single targets but deadly in combat. When you fire an activated enfilading arrow, it sails upward and shatters into countless copies. The arrows then rain down in a 10-foot burst at any point within your weapon's first range increment, dealing [[/r {6d8}[piercing]]]{6d8 piercing damage} to all creatures in the area (basic Reflex).

If a creature critically fails the save and you have access to the bow critical specialization effect, the target is @Compendium[pf2e.conditionitems.eIcWbB5o3pP6OIMe]{Immobilized} and must spend an Interact action to attempt a Athletics check to pull the missile free; it can't move from its space until it succeeds.
