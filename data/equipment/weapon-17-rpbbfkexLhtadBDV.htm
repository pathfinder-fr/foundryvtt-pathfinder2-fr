Name: Bottled Lightning (Major) Nom: Foudre en bouteille (majeur) État: officielle ------ Description (en) ------

Activate A Strike

Bottled lightning is packed with volatile reagents that create a blast of electricity when they are exposed to air. Bottled lightning deals 4d6 electricity damage and 4 electricity splash damage. On a hit, the target becomes flat-footed until the start of your next turn. You gain a +3 item bonus to attack rolls.
