Name: Perfect Droplet (Greater) Nom: Goutte parfaite supérieure État: libre ------ Description (en) ------

Intense blue water magically holds its shape-a perfect sphere. The spell DC of any spell cast by Activating this item is 24.

  • Armor You gain resistance 5 to water effects and attacks by water creatures.
  • Weapon After you cast a water spell by Activating the droplet, your body becomes mistlike. Until the end of the turn, you can move through enemies' spaces. You can't move through creatures that have the water trait in this way.
