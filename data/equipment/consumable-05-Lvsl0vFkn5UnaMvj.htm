Name: Ichthyosis Mutagen Nom: État: aucune ------ Description (en) ------

Activate 1 Interact

After you drink this mutagen, your skin continually renews itself, thickening into large, scaly patches.

Benefit For 1 minute, you gain fast healing 2 and automatically succeed at flat checks to recover from persistent bleed damage.

Drawback Your thickened skin makes quick movement difficult, and your excess skin flakes off with the slightest movement. You take a –2 penalty to Reflex saves for 1 minute. Any creature attempting to @Compendium[pf2e.actionspf2e.EA5vuSgJfiHH7plD]{Track} you in the next 24 hours gains a +4 circumstance bonus to their check.
