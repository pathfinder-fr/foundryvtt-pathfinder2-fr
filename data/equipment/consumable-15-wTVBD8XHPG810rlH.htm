Name: Stone Bullet Nom: Bille de pierre État: officielle ------ Description (en) ------

This sling bullet looks like a petrified serpent’s eye. A creature hit by an activated stone bullet is subject to the effects of a 6th-level @Item[Flesh to Stone] spell (DC 34). 

Activate Interact

Craft Requirements Supply one casting of flesh to stone.
