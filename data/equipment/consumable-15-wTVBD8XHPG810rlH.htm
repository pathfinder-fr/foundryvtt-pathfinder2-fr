Name: Stone Bullet Nom: Bille de pierre État: officielle ------ Description (en) ------

Activate A Interact

This sling bullet looks like a petrified serpent's eye. A creature hit by an activated stone bullet is subject to the effects of a 6th-level @Compendium[pf2e.spells-srd.znv4ECL7ZtuiagtA]{Flesh to Stone} spell (DC 34).

Craft Requirements Supply one casting of flesh to stone.
