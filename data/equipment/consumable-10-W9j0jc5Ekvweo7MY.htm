Name: Burning Badger Guts Snare Nom: Piège artisanal intestin brûlant de blaireau État: libre ------ Description (en) ------

When a creature enters the trapped square, putrefied badger guts coated in hyper-flammable oil and several other incendiary reactants catch fire and are dumped in the snare's square, as well as up to two adjacent squares that you choose when you set the snare. The burning oil deals [[/r {5d8}[fire]]]{5d8 fire damage} to any creatures in the affected squares. Those creatures must attempt a @Check[type:fortitude|dc:27|traits:uncommon,damaging-effect|showDC:all] save.

Critical Success The creature is unaffected.

Success The creature takes half damage and [[/r {1d8}[persistent,fire]]] @Compendium[pf2e.conditionitems.lDVqvLKA6eF3Df60]{Persistent Fire Damage}.

Failure The creature takes full damage and [[/r {2d8}[persistent,fire]]] persistent fire damage, and is @Compendium[pf2e.conditionitems.fesd1n5eVhpCSS18]{Sickened 1}.

Critical Failure As failure, but the creature is @Compendium[pf2e.conditionitems.fesd1n5eVhpCSS18]{Sickened 2}.
