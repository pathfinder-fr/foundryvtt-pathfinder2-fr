Name: Flaming (Greater) Nom: Enflamée supérieure (rune) État: changé État d'origine: officielle ------ Description (en) ------

This weapon is empowered by flickering flame. The weapon deals an additional 1d6 fire damage on a successful Strike, plus 2d10 persistent fire damage on a critical hit.

Fire damage dealt by this weapon (including the persistent fire damage) ignores the target's fire resistance.
