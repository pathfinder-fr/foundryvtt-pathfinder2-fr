Name: Envenomed Snare Nom: Piège venimeux État: libre ------ Description (en) ------

This snare is coated with giant wasp venom and tipped with needles, which deliver the venom to the first creature to enter the square. That creature takes [[/r {4d6}[piercing]]]{4d6 piercing damage} (basic Reflex) and, on a failure, the creature is exposed to the @Compendium[pf2e.equipment-srd.aBOPYlfHAcXUmhF7]{Giant Wasp Venom}.

Craft Requirements Supply 2 doses of giant wasp venom.
