Name: Blade of Four Energies Nom: Lame des quatre énergies État: changé État d'origine: libre ------ Description (en) ------

This +2 greater striking shifting shortsword is formed of rapidly vibrating air and magical energy, though it uses the same statistics as iron. The wooden hilt is adorned with four gems, representing the energies of acid, cold, fire, and electricity, that sparkle in sequence at random intervals.

Activate f Interact

Frequency once per round

Effect You draw magical energy from one of the gems. Roll [[/r 1d4]]. Until the start of your next turn, the weapon gains the corresponding rune:

  1. @Compendium[pf2e.equipment-srd.Wm0X7Pfd1bfocPSv]{Corrosive},
  2. @Compendium[pf2e.equipment-srd.XszNvxnymWYRaoTp]{Flaming},
  3. @Compendium[pf2e.equipment-srd.M5M1WJ5KzbYfRGsY]{Frost}, or
  4. @Compendium[pf2e.equipment-srd.NVst7e69agGG9Qwd]{Shock}.

Activate 2 command, envision

Frequency once per day

Requirements The blade has an energy rune from the first activation

Effect You call upon the energy stored in the blade to explode outward. The blade of four energies casts a 5th-level spell that depends on the active rune:

  1. @Compendium[pf2e.spells-srd.f8hRqLJaxBVhF1u0]{Acid Arrow} for corrosive,
  2. @Compendium[pf2e.spells-srd.sxQZ6yqTn0czJxVd]{Fireball} for flaming,
  3. @Compendium[pf2e.spells-srd.3puDanGfpEt6jK5k]{Cone of Cold} for frost, or
  4. @Compendium[pf2e.spells-srd.9AAkVUCwF6WVNNY2]{Lightning Bolt} for shock.
