Name: Swift Block Cabochon Nom: Cabochon de Blocage rapide État: officielle ------ Description (en) ------

Usage affixed to a shield

Activate F Envision

Trigger You take damage from a physical attack while you don't have the affixed shield raised

This clear quartz cabochon attaches to the center of your shield.

When you activate the cabochon, you use the Shield Block reaction even if you hadn't raised the affixed shield (and even if you don't normally have that reaction).
