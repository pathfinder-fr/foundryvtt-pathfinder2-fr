Name: Mending Lattice Nom: Maillage réparateur État: officielle ------ Description (en) ------

This lattice of reinforced iron is shaped into a perfect octagon. When you activate it, it negates the damage and instantly and completely repairs the affixed item.

Activate F Command; Trigger The affixed item would take damage; Requirements You are a master in Crafting
