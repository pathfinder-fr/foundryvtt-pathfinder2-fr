Name: Bullhook (Greater) Nom: Crochet de taureau supérieur État: libre ------ Description (en) ------

A bullhook is a stout rod about 4 feet long with a blunt hook on the end. Too dull to be useful as a weapon, a bullhook is instead used to direct animals in training or in performing their tasks. A bullhook grants you a +2 item bonus to Nature checks to Command an Animal while you are holding it.

Activate D command, Interact

Frequency once per day

Effect You wave the bullhook to produce the effects of a fifth level @Compendium[pf2e.spells-srd.aIHY2DArKFweIrpf]{Command} spell (DC 30). This spell loses the linguistic trait and can target only animals.

Craft Requirements Supply a casting of command of the appropriate level.

Automatically applied effects:

  • +2 item bonus to Nature checks to Command an Animal
