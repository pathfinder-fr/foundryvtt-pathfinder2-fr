Name: Leaper's Elixir (Lesser) Nom: Élixir du sauteur inférieur État: officielle ------ Description (en) ------

Activate 1 Interact

This tingly solution increases the elasticity and contraction of your leg muscles. For 1 minute after drinking this elixir, you can @Compendium[pf2e.actionspf2e.2HJ4yuEFY1Cast4h]{High Jump} or @Compendium[pf2e.actionspf2e.JUvAvruz7yRQXfz2]{Long Jump} as a single action instead of 2 actions. If you do, you don't perform the initial Stride (nor do you fail if you don't Stride 10 feet).
