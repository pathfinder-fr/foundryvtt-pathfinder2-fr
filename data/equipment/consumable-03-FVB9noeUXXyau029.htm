Name: Blast Boots (Moderate) Nom: Bottes d'explosion modéré État: libre ------ Description (en) ------

Activate 1 Interact

These sets of rockets come in pairs and strap onto existing footwear (or a creature's feet). Inserting them and aligning them properly takes 1 minute. When you Activate the moderate blast boots to @Compendium[pf2e.actionspf2e.2HJ4yuEFY1Cast4h]{High Jump}, you can increase the vertical distance of your High Jump by up to 30 feet. When you Activate them to @Compendium[pf2e.actionspf2e.JUvAvruz7yRQXfz2]{Long Jump}, you can increase the horizontal distance of your Long Jump by up to 45 feet.
