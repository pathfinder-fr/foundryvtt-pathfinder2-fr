Name: Pendant of the Occult Nom: Pendentif des sciences occultes État: officielle ------ Description (en) ------

This amulet is hollow and shaped in the form of an unblinking eye. Its cavity typically holds some fragment of occult text. While wearing the pendant, you gain a +1 item bonus to Occultism checks, and you can cast the @Compendium[pf2e.spells-srd.izcxFQFwf3woCnFs]{Guidance} cantrip as an occult innate spell.
