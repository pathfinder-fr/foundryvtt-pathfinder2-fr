The blade of this +2 greater striking @Compendium[pf2e.equipment-srd.Sexud7FdxIrg50vU]{Frost (Greater)} greatsword appears to be nothing but icy shards. The frost brand automatically extinguishes non-magical fires in a @Template[type:emanation|distance:20]. While wielding it, you gain fire resistance 5.
