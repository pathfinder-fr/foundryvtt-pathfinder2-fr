Name: Sneezing Powder Nom: Poudre à éternuer État: libre ------ Description (en) ------

You can toss sneezing powder at an adjacent creature as an Interact action. The target must attempt a @Check[type:fortitude|dc:15] save to avoid sneezing. On a failed save, the creature sneezes uncontrollably, becoming @Compendium[pf2e.conditionitems.xYTAsEpcJE1Ccni3]{Slowed 1} for 1 round. On a critical failure, the creature is instead slowed 1 for 3 rounds.
