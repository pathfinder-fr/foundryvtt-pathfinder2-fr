Name: Stone Body Mutagen (Moderate) Nom: État: aucune ------ Description (en) ------

This mutagen calcifies your body making it as hard as stone.

Benefit You gain resistance 5 to physical damage (except bludgeoning) and the duration is 1 hour. 

Drawback Your Speeds are reduced by 10 feet (minimum 5 feet) and you take a –2 penalty to Reflex saves.

Activate  Interact
