Name: Primal Scroll Case of Simplicity Nom: Étui à parchemin de simplicité primordiale État: changé État d'origine: libre ------ Description (en) ------

The four different types of scroll cases of simplicity often bear adornments appropriate to their magical tradition, such as angelic wings or otherworldly lettering. On the inside, intricate runic diagrams spiral out to surround the scroll stored within. A scroll placed within the case can be converted into energy to cast consistently useful spells depending on its type. You must be able to cast spells of a given tradition to use a scroll case of simplicity of a corresponding type.

Activate A Interact; Requirements The scroll case contains a single scroll of a 1st-level spell; Effect You transfer the scroll's energy into the scroll case, consuming the scroll, and you can immediately begin casting one of the scroll case's spells. If you use any action other than to Cast a Spell from the scroll case after activating the scroll case of simplicity, the scroll and its energy are lost.

The scroll case can be used to cast @Compendium[pf2e.spells-srd.KcLVELhCUcKXxiKE]{Longstrider} or @Compendium[pf2e.spells-srd.zlnXpME1T2uvn8Lr]{Pass Without Trace}
