Name: Eye of Fortune Nom: Oeil de chance État: changé État d'origine: officielle ------ Description (en) ------

Activate F envision (fortune)

Trigger You attack a concealed or hidden creature and haven't attempted the flat check yet

Effect You can roll the flat check for the concealed or hidden condition twice and use the higher result.

Adherents of @Compendium[pf2e.deities.mm1j6UbHqFCI8fwB]{Erastil}, god of the hunt, create these magical eye patches. An eye of fortune has a jeweled eye symbol on its front, allowing you to magically see through the eye patch as though it were transparent.

Les fidèles d’Érastil, @Compendium[pf2e.deities.mm1j6UbHqFCI8fwB]{Erastil} le dieu de la chasse, créent ces cacheyeux magiques.
