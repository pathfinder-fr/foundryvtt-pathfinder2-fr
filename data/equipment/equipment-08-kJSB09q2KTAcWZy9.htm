Name: Bag of Cats Nom: Sac des félins État: libre ------ Description (en) ------

This beautiful leather bag is lined in soft fur and purrs quietly when pet.

Activate 2 Interact

Frequency once per day

Effect You shake the bag to produce a single cat. You cast a 3rd-level @Compendium[pf2e.spells-srd.4YnON9JHYqtLzccu]{Summon Animal} to produce a @Compendium[pf2e.pathfinder-bestiary.wqPYzMNgYvrO6oEP]{Leopard}.

Activate 2 envision, Interact

Frequency once per day

Effect You pull the bag over your head and it covers you completely. You then exit the bag in the form of a large cat. You gain the effects of 3rd-level @Compendium[pf2e.spells-srd.wp09USMB3GIW1qbp]{Animal Form} but must use the cat battle form.
