Name: Crafter's Eyepiece (Greater) Nom: Oculaire d'artisan supérieur État: officielle ------ Description (en) ------

This rugged metal eyepiece etched with square patterns is designed to be worn over a single eye. Twisting the lens reveals a faint three-dimensional outline of an item you plan to build or repair, with helpful labels on the component parts.

While worn, this eyepiece gives you a +2 item bonus to Crafting checks. When you Repair an item, increase the Hit Points restored by 15 per proficiency rank instead of 10.

Activate 1 minute (Interact)

Frequency once per day

Effect You calibrate the eyepiece to have it cast a 5th-level @Compendium[pf2e.spells-srd.TUj8eugNqAvB1vVR]{Creation} spell over the course of 1 minute to construct a temporary item.

Automatically applied effects:

  • +2 item bonus to checks to Crafting checks
