Name: Wand of Continuation (7th-Level Spell) Nom: Baguette de prolongation (sort de niveau 7) État: changé État d'origine: officielle ------ Description (en) ------

This wand increases a spell's duration. Yellow embers spiral over its surface until the spell ends.

Activate Cast a Spell; The activation takes 2 if the spell normally takes 1 to cast, or 3 if the spell normally takes 2

Frequency Once per day, plus overcharge

Effect You Cast the Spell, and its duration is increased by half.

Craft Requirements Supply a casting of a spell of the appropriate level. The spell must have a casting time of 1 or 2 and a duration no less than 10 minutes and no greater than 1 hour.
