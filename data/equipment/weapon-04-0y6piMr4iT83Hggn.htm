Name: Falchion (+1 Striking) Nom: État: aucune ------ Description (en) ------

This weapon is a heavier, two‑handed version of the curved‑bladed scimitar. It is weighted toward the blade’s end, making it a powerful slashing weapon.

This weapon has a +1 weapon potency rune (+1 item bonus to attack rolls with the weapon) and a striking rune (one extra damage die).
