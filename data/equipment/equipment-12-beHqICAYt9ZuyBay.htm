Name: Grim Sandglass (Major) Nom: Sablier sinistre majeur État: libre ------ Description (en) ------

One bulb of this tiny hourglass contains black sand, the other white. After even a few grains pass from one side to the other, it reverses its flow to keep the two sides in equilibrium. The spell DC of any spell cast by Activating this item is 29.

  • Armor You gain resistance 10 to negative.
  • Weapon After you cast a necromancy spell by Activating the sandglass, your Strikes with the weapon deal an additional 1d8 negative damage until the end of your next turn.
