Name: Breastplate of Command (Greater) Nom: Cuirasse de commandement supérieur État: officielle ------ Description (en) ------

This +2 greater resilient breastplate is made from shining bronze overlaid with reinforcing golden panels in the shape of lion's heads. Wearing this breastplate grants you a commanding aura. You gain a +3 item bonus to Diplomacy checks, but you take a -3 item penalty to Stealth checks to Hide and Sneak and Deception checks to Impersonate.

ActivateACommand

Frequency once per day

Effect You grant allies within 100 feet a +2 status bonus to saves against fear effects for 1 minute. When you activate this ability, each affected ally who's frightened reduces their frightened value by 1.
