Name: Breastplate of Command (Greater) Nom: État: aucune ------ Description (en) ------

This +1 resilient breastplate is made from shining bronze overlaid with reinforcing golden panels in the shape of lion’s heads. Wearing this breastplate grants you a commanding aura. You gain a +3 item bonus to Diplomacy checks, but you take a –2 item penalty to Stealth checks to Hide and Sneak and Deception checks to Impersonate.

Activate Command; Frequency once per day; Effect The armor sprouts glowing wings that grant you a fly Speed of 30 feet. The wings shed bright light in a 40-foot radius (and dim light to the next 40 feet). The wings fade away after 10 minutes.
