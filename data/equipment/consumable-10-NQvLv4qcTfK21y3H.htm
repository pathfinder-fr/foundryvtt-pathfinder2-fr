Name: Spirit-Sealing Fulu (Greater) Nom: Fulu scelle-esprit supérieur État: libre ------ Description (en) ------

Usage affixed to one undead creature

Duration 1 round or 4 rounds

This classic fulu seals the negative energy within an undead, freezing it in its tracks. Upon affixing, the target undead must attempt a @Check[type:will|dc:27|name:spirit-sealing fulu|showDC:all] save or be @Compendium[pf2e.conditionitems.6uEgoh53GbXuHpTF]{Paralyzed} for 1 round. On a critical failure, the undead is paralyzed for 4 rounds. At the end of each of its turns, it can attempt a new Will save to reduce the remaining duration by 1 round, or end it entirely on a critical success.
