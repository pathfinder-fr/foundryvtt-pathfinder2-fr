Name: Wet Shock Snare Nom: Piège artisanal de choc humide État: changé État d'origine: libre ------ Description (en) ------

A hidden copper plate conceals a bag of electric eels. When a creature steps on the plate, the eels become agitated, and the creature takes [[/r {4d8}[electricity]]]{4d8 electricity damage} (@Check[type:reflex|dc:21|basic:true|traits:uncommon|showDC:all]). On a critical failure, the current causes the creature to become @Compendium[pf2e.conditionitems.dfCMdR4wnpbYNTix]{Stunned 2}. The eels are unharmed after use, but the mechanism is broken as usual for snares.
