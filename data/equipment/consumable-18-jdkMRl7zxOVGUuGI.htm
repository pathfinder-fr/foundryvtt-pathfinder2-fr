Name: Universal Solvent (Major) Nom: État: aucune ------ Description (en) ------

Activate 1 Interact

Originally formulated as a means of dissolving Soverign Glue, this powerful solvent can break almost any adhesive’s grip. As universal solvent is particularly effective against sovereign glue, it automatically dissolves sovereign glue. It attempts to counteract any other adhesives, such as Tanglefoot Bags, with a counteract modifier of 1d20+28.
