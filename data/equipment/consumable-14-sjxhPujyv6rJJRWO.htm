Name: Viper's Fang Nom: Croc de vipère État: officielle ------ Description (en) ------

Usage affixed to a weapon

Activate R Envision

Trigger A creature within your reach uses a manipulate or move action, makes a ranged attack, or leaves a square during a moving action it's using

Requirements You are a master with the affixed weapon

When you activate this resin-strengthened viper skull, you make an Attack of Opportunity against the triggering creature.

If you have Attack of Opportunity, you can activate the viper's fang as a free action.
