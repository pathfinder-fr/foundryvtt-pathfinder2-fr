Name: Demolishing Nom: Arme démolisseuse État: libre ------ Description (en) ------

A demolishing weapon is made to destroy constructs. Damage inflicted on a construct with a demolishing weapon continues to spread throughout the creature-cracks form, linkages fail, surfaces erode-and otherwise dismantle its body. When you damage a construct using a demolishing weapon, you deal an extra [[/r {1d6}[persistent,force]]] @Compendium[pf2e.conditionitems.lDVqvLKA6eF3Df60]{Persistent Force Damage}. On a critical hit, you deal an extra [[/r {1d12}[persistent,force]]] @Compendium[pf2e.conditionitems.lDVqvLKA6eF3Df60]{Persistent Force Damage} instead
