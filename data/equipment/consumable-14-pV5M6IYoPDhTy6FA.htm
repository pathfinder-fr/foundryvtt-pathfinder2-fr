Name: Chopping Evisceration Snare Nom: Piège artisanal d'éviscération hachante État: changé État d'origine: libre ------ Description (en) ------

An almost-impossible number of axes spring out at a target with lethal force. When a creature enters the snare's square, it's nearly buried beneath a storm of sharpened metal, which deals [[/r {16d8}[slashing]]]{16d8 slashing damage} (@Check[type:reflex|dc:33|basic:true|showDC:all]).
