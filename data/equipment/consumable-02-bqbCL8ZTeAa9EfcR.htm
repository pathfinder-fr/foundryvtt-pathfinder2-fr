When a creature enters this snare's square, it triggers an extremely loud clockwork device, which explodes with a bang that can be heard from 200 feet away and deals [[/r {1d8}[fire]]]{1d8 fire damage}. The creature must attempt a Reflex save.

Critical Success The creature is unaffected.

Success The creature takes half damage.

Failure The creature takes full damage and is @Compendium[pf2e.conditionitems.9PR9y0bi4JPKnHPR]{Deafened} for 1 round.

Critical Failure The creature takes double damage and is @Compendium[pf2e.conditionitems.9PR9y0bi4JPKnHPR]{Deafened} for 1 minute.
