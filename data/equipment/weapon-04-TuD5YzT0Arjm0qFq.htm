Name: Sai (+1 Striking) Nom: État: aucune ------ Description (en) ------

This piercing dagger is a metal spike flanked by a pair of prongs that can be used to trap an enemy’s weapon.

This weapon has a +1 weapon potency rune (+1 item bonus to attack rolls with the weapon) and a striking rune (one extra damage die).
