Name: Fungal Armor Nom: Armure fongique État: libre ------ Description (en) ------

This +2 greater resilient @Compendium[pf2e.equipment-srd.ewQZ0VeL38v3qFnN]{Studded Leather Armor} is made of hardened fungus. Every day the armor grows dozens of unusual mushrooms that can be used for cooking, infused reagents, or common spell material components.

Activate r or a Envision

Frequency once per day

Trigger You are hit by a melee, ranged, or spell attack

Effect The attack misses you, dealing no damage as you transform into a cloud of spores. You gain the benefits of a @Compendium[pf2e.spells-srd.V8wXOsoejQhe6CyG]{Gaseous Form} spell for 1 minute.

For the duration, a cloud of deadly spores surrounds you. Each breathing creature that begins its turn within 10 feet of you takes [[/r {6d8}[poison]]]{6d8 poison damage} (@Check[type:fortitude|dc:35|basic:true|name:Fungal Armor Spores]).

You can @Compendium[pf2e.actionspf2e.AjLSHZSWQ90exdLo]{Dismiss} the cloud form.

Craft Requirements Supply a casting of @Compendium[pf2e.spells-srd.MlpbeZ61Euhl0d60]{Cloudkill} and gaseous form.
