Name: Spore Sap Nom: Matraque de spores État: changé État d'origine: libre ------ Description (en) ------

This flexible +2 striking sap is crafted from the stalks of cave fungi and bound around a mesh of spore-infused gills. Demon-worshipping xulgaths use spore saps to capture spellcasters alive for later sacrifice.

Activate R Interact (poison); Frequency three times per day; Trigger You successfully Strike a creature with the spore sap; Effect The spore sap releases a puff of stupefying fungal spores. The target must succeed at a DC 28 Fortitude save or become stupefied 1 for 1 minute (stupefied 2 on a critical failure).
