Name: Halberd (+1) Nom: Hallebarde (+1) État: libre ------ Description (en) ------

This polearm has a relatively short, 5‑foot shaft. The business end is a long spike with an axe blade attached.

This weapon has a +1 weapon potency rune (+1 item bonus to attack rolls with the weapon).
