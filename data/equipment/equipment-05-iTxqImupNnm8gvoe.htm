Name: Glamered Nom: État: aucune ------ Description (en) ------

This armor can be disguised with a mere thought.

Activate envision; Effect You change the shape and appearance of this armor to appear as ordinary or fine clothes of your imagining. The armor’s statistics do not change. Only a creature that is benefiting from true seeing or a similar effect can attempt to disbelieve this illusion, with a DC of 25.
