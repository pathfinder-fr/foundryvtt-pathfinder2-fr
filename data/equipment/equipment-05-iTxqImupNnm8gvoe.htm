Name: Glamered Nom: Mimétisme (rune) État: changé État d'origine: officielle ------ Description (en) ------

This armor can be disguised with a mere thought.

Activate A envision; Effect You change the shape and appearance of this armor to appear as ordinary or fine clothes of your imagining. The armor's statistics do not change. Only a creature that is benefiting from true seeing or a similar effect can attempt to disbelieve this illusion, with a DC of 25.
