This sharp-taloned +1 striking spiked gauntlet appears to have been crafted from dragon scales, with claws built from iron. In addition to the typical weapon traits, a Cinderclaw gauntlet has the versatile S trait.

On a critical hit, the Cinderclaw gauntlet deals an additional 1d6 fire damage.

Activate R command

Trigger Your attack roll with the Cinderclaw gauntlet is a critical success.
