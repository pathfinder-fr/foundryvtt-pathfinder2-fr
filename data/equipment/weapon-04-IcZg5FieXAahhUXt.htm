Name: War Flail (+1 Striking) Nom: État: aucune ------ Description (en) ------

This large flail has a long shaft connected to a shorter piece of stout wood or metal that’s sometimes inlaid with spikes.

This weapon has a +1 weapon potency rune (+1 item bonus to attack rolls with the weapon) and a striking rune (one extra damage die).
