Activate A Interact

Your mind becomes clear and cognition flows freely, but physical matters seem ephemeral.

Benefit You gain a +4 item bonus to Arcana, Crafting, Lore, Occultism, and Society checks and all checks to @Compendium[pf2e.actionspf2e.1OagaWtBpVXExToo]{Recall Knowledge}.
