Name: Anchoring (Greater) Nom: Ancrage supérieur (rune) État: libre ------ Description (en) ------

This rune prevents enemies from escaping your grasp by @Compendium[pf2e.conditionitems.sDPxOjQ9kx2RZE8D]{Fleeing} to other planes.

When you critically hit a target with a weapon with the anchoring rune, the weapon casts 8th-level @Compendium[pf2e.spells-srd.ksLCg62cLOojw3gN]{Dimensional Anchor} on the target (DC 38 Will, counteract modifier [[/r 1d20+28 #counteract]]{+28}), except that if the target critically succeeds at its Will save, instead of having no effect, the dimensional anchor lasts for 1 round. When you hit a target with the weapon but don't critically hit, the target is affected by 4th-level dimensional anchor for 1 round without a save (this still uses a counteract modifier of +28).
