Name: Shrinking Potion (Greater) Nom: Potion de rétrécissement État: changé État d'origine: officielle ------ Description (en) ------

This fungus-flavored potion conveys the effects of the shrink spell to make you and all your gear smaller. This potion has no onset, lasts for 1 hour, and grants the effects of a 4th-level @Compendium[pf2e.spells-srd.PjhUmyKnq6K5uDby]{Shrink} spell. In addition, you gain a +2 item bonus to Stealth checks while shrunken.
