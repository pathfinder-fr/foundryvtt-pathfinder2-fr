On a hit, if the target is suffering from @Compendium[pf2e.conditionitems.lDVqvLKA6eF3Df60]{Persistent Acid or Persistent Fire Damage}, it can attempt a flat check to end that persistent damage immediately. As this is particularly effective assistance, the DC of the flat check is reduced from 15 to 10 for this check. Except on a critical failure, the bomb extinguishes non-magical fires within its splash area.
