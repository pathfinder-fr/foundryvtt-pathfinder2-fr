Name: Thunderstone (Lesser) Nom: Pierre à tonnerre inférieur État: changé État d'origine: officielle ------ Description (en) ------

When this stone hits a creature or a hard surface, it explodes with a deafening bang. A thunderstone deals the listed sonic damage and sonic splash damage, and each creature within 10 feet of the space in which the stone exploded must succeed at a @Check[type:fortitude|dc:17|name:Lesser Thunderstone|showDC:all] saving throw with the listed DC or be @Compendium[pf2e.conditionitems.9PR9y0bi4JPKnHPR]{Deafened} until the end of its next turn. Many types of thunderstone grant an item bonus to attack rolls.

The bomb deals 1d4 sonic damage and 1 sonic splash damage, and the DC is 17.
