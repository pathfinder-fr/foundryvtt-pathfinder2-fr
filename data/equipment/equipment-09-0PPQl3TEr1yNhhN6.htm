Name: Persona Mask (Greater) Nom: Masque de représentation (supérieur) État: officielle ------ Description (en) ------

Despite covering the entire face, this alabaster mask does not hinder vision or other senses. Wearing the mask grants a +1 item bonus to Performance checks while acting, orating, performing comedy, or singing.

The mask grants a +2 bonus and can be activated.

Activate  envision (fortune); Frequency once per day; Trigger You fail a Performance check that benefits from the mask’s bonus; Effect You change the mask’s character and reroll the Performance check, using the second result.
