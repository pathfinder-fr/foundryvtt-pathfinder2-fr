Name: Arctic Vigor Nom: Vigueur arctique (Tatouage) État: libre ------ Description (en) ------

This tattoo takes the shape of the face of a roaring polar bear with piercing, ice-blue eyes. You don't take damage from extreme cold or severe cold.

Activate 2 command;

Frequency once per day;

Effect You call forth a blast of polar wind in a 10-foot burst within a range of 30 feet that deals [[/r {7d6}[cold]]]{7d6 cold damage}. All creatures in the area must attempt a basic Fortitude save.
