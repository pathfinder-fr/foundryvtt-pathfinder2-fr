Name: Primeval Mistletoe (Greater) Nom: Gui primitif supérieur État: officielle ------ Description (en) ------

This sprig of berry-festooned holly and mistletoe doesn't wilt or rot. It can be used as a primal focus, and it also grants the creature holding it a +2 item bonus to Nature checks.

Activate D Interact

Frequency once per 10 minutes

Effect You squeeze juice from one of the berries and smear it onto a non-magical club or staff to cast @Compendium[pf2e.spells-srd.s3abwDbTV43pGFFW]{Shillelagh} upon it.

Activate D Interact

Frequency once per day

Effect You can twine the sprig around the wrist of one hand and touch a tree to cast @Compendium[pf2e.spells-srd.dileJ0Yxqg76LMvu]{Tree Shape} upon yourself, except instead of a becoming a tree, you become a vine on the touched tree.

Activate D Interact

Frequency once per day

Effect You plant the primeval mistletoe into an area of natural earth or stone. Once planted, the plant immediately sprouts into an area of holly bushes that don't impede movement and that pulse with positive energy, replicating the effects of a @Compendium[pf2e.spells-srd.x5rGOmhDRDVQPrnW]{Field of Life} spell. This lasts for up to 1 minute, as long as you continue to Sustain the Activation. When this magic ends, the holly bushes revert back into the original primeval mistletoe.

Automatically applied effects:

  • +2 item bonus to Nature checks
