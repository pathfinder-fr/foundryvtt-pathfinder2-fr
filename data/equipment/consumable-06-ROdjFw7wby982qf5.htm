Name: Terrifying Ammunition Nom: Munition terrifiante État: libre ------ Description (en) ------

Activate 1 Interact

This black-and-gray ammunition is etched with occult symbols and tiny, grinning skulls. When activated terrifying ammunition damages a creature, it fills the creature's mind with visions of their own failures, tragedies, and eventually, their own death. The creature must attempt a @Check[type:will|dc:20] save.

Critical Success The creature is unaffected.

Success The creature is frightened 1.

Failure The creature is frightened 1 and can't reduce its frightened value below 1 until it spends an action, which has the concentrate trait, to calm itself down.

Critical Failure As failure, but the creature is frightened 2.
