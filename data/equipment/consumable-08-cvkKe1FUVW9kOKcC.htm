Name: Seven-Color Raw Fish Salad Nom: Salade de poisson cru aux sept couleurs État: libre ------ Description (en) ------

An appetizer popularized in Goka, seven-color raw fish salad (qicai yusheng) is both fun and tasty to eat. This large platter of salad of raw fish, seafood, fruits, and vegetables of various hues is dressed in generous drizzlings of crushed peanuts, plum sauce, and sesame oil. During the Lunar New Year, consuming the seven-color raw fish salad (as 'seven colors' sounds similar to 'seven riches') is a grand, often messy, affair of spectacle and skill. Diners gather around the platter with long chopsticks to toss the salad, lifting the assorted ingredients as high as possible without dropping them off the table; erudite diners lead the table in uttering auspicious sayings to wish good fortune for the new year.

Activate 1 minute (command, Interact)

Critical Success You manage to toss the ingredients deftly without wasting food, and your enthusiastic recitation of auspicious sayings is well received-a sign of impending good fortune! For the first month of the coming year, you enjoy a +2 bonus to skill checks to @Compendium[pf2e.actionspf2e.QyzlsLrqM0EEwd7j]{Earn Income} and Diplomacy checks made to Gather Information about job opportunities.

Success You toss the ingredients adroitly, wasting a minimum of seafood, and your delivery of auspicious sayings is respectable. For the first month of the coming year, you gain a +1 bonus to skill checks to Earn Income and Diplomacy checks made to Gather Information about job opportunities.

Failure You fumble too many of the ingredients to have enough left to eat.

------ Description (fr) ------

Entrée populaire à Goka, la salade de poisson cru aux sept couleurs (qicai yusheng) est à la fois amusante et savoureuse. Ce grand plateau de salade de poisson cru, de fruits de mer, de fruits et de légumes de différentes teintes est assaisonné de généreux arrosages de cacahuètes écrasées, de sauce aux prunes et d'huile de sésame. Pendant le Nouvel An lunaire, la consommation de la salade de poisson cru aux sept couleurs (car "sept couleurs" sonne comme "sept richesses") est une affaire grandiose, souvent désordonnée, de spectacle et de savoir-faire. Les convives se rassemblent autour du plateau avec de longues baguettes pour mélanger la salade, en soulevant les ingrédients assortis aussi haut que possible sans les faire tomber de la table ; des convives érudits mènent les convives en prononçant des dictons de bon augure pour souhaiter la bonne fortune pour la nouvelle année.
