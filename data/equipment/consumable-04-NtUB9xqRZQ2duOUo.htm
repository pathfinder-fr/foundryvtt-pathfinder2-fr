Small flecks of mica, sand, and ground glass are packed into a spring-loaded canister that can be rigged to a trip wire or hidden in a container. The first creature to enter the square or open the container must attempt a Reflex save as the area is showered with bright, glittering dust that sticks to skin, fur, and clothing.

Critical Success The creature is unaffected.

Success The creature takes a -1 status penalty to Stealth checks for 1 minute or until it uses an Interact action to remove the glitter.
