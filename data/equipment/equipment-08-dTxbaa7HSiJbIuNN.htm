Name: Thundering Nom: Tonnerre (rune) État: officielle ------ Description (en) ------

This weapon lets out a peal of thunder when it hits, dealing an extra 1d6 sonic damage on a successful Strike. On a critical hit, the target has to succeed at a @Check[type:fortitude|dc:24] save or be deafened for 1 minute (or 1 hour on a critical failure).
