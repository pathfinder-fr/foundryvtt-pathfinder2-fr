Name: Spectacles of Understanding Nom: Lunettes de compréhension État: libre ------ Description (en) ------

These folding, wood-framed spectacles hold flat discs of cut crystal as lenses and are held in place with thin cords that wrap around each ear, with another strap holding them around the neck while not in use. While you are wearing the spectacles, you gain a +1 item bonus to checks to Decipher Writing.

Activate A Interact

Frequency once per day

Effect Unfolding the spectacles onto the bridge of the nose, you gain the effects of 2nd-level @Compendium[pf2e.spells-srd.vTQvfYu2llKQedmY]{Comprehend Language} for one hour except that it applies to all languages you see rather than a single language, and it does not apply to language you hear.

Automatically applied effects:

  • +1 item bonus to checks to Decipher Writing
