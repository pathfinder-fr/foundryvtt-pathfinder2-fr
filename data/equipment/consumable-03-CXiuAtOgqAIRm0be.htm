Name: Quicksilver Mutagen (Moderate) Nom: Mutagène de vif-argent (modéré) État: officielle ------ Description (en) ------ Your features become thin and angular. You become swifter and nimbler, but your body also becomes fragile. The bonus to rolls is +2, the bonus to Speed is +10 feet, and the duration is 10 minutes. ------ Description (fr) ------ Vous devenez fin et anguleux.
