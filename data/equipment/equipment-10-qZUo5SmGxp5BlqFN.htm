The barbed vest doesn't fuse to you in the usual way. It can be removed and discarded, but 1 day after doing so, you feel driven to recollect the vest at any cost until the curse is removed with a @Compendium[pf2e.spells-srd.OyFCwQuw8XRazsNr]{Remove Curse} spell.

Anytime you activate the barbed vest, its chains tighten around you, dealing [[/r {1d6}[persistent,piercing]]] @Compendium[pf2e.conditionitems.lDVqvLKA6eF3Df60]{Persistent Piercing Damage}. You can remove this persistent damage as normal, but only after deactivating the item's magic as a single action.
