Name: Sparkblade Nom: État: changé État d'origine: auto-trad ------ Description (en) ------

Faint, delicate etchings of lightning trace across the blade of this +1 cold iron shortsword. Cold iron is made from particularly pure sources of iron and shaped with little to no heat, resulting in weapons deadly to demons and fey alike.

Activate A Interact

Once per day, you can spend 1 action to point the sparkblade at a foe within 30 feet of you and shoot an arc of lightning from the blade. This lightning can leap from your chosen foe to another creature you choose within 30 feet, dealing [[/r 2d4+4]] electricity damage to each creature (DC 19 basic Reflex save).
