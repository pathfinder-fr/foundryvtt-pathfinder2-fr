Name: Dwarven Daisy (Lesser) Nom: Marguerite naine inférieure État: changé État d'origine: libre ------ Description (en) ------

A dwarven daisy is a string of dozens of firecrackers that create tiny, loud explosions. The string ends with a short, wrapped fuse. Pulling off the wrapping in a quick motion lights the fuse and is done as part of the action to Strike. A dwarven daisy deals the listed fire damage and splash damage. A dwarven daisy also has a chance to dazzle its target. A creature struck by a dwarven daisy must succeed at a @Check[type:fortitude|dc:16|name:Lesser Dwarven Daisy|traits:damaging-effect|showDC:all] save or become @Compendium[pf2e.conditionitems.TkIyaNPgTZFBCCuh]{Dazzled} for 1 round.

Activate A Strike

The firework deals 1d6 fire damage and 1 fire splash damage. The DC is 16.
