Name: Katar (+1 Striking) Nom: État: aucune ------ Description (en) ------

Also known as punching daggers, katars are characterized by their H‑shaped hand grip that allows the blade to jut out from the knuckles.

This weapon has a +1 weapon potency rune (+1 item bonus to attack rolls with the weapon) and a striking rune (one extra damage die).
