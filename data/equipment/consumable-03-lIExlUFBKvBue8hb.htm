Name: Silvertongue Mutagen (Moderate) Nom: Mutagène de langue dorée (Modéré) État: officielle ------ Description (en) ------

Activate A Interact

Your features become striking and your voice becomes musical and commanding, though facts and figures become hazy for you and emotion clouds your ability to reason.

Benefit You gain a +2 item bonus to Deception, Diplomacy, Intimidation and Performance checks. Your critical failures with any of these skills become failures instead.

Drawback You take a -2 item penalty to Arcana, Crafting, Lore, Occultism, and Society checks. Choose one skill in which you are trained; for the duration, you become untrained in that skill. All your failures to @Compendium[pf2e.actionspf2e.1OagaWtBpVXExToo]{Recall Knowledge} become critical failures.

Duration 10 minutes.
