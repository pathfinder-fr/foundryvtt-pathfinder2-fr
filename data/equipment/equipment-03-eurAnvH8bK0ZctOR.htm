Name: Bracers of Missile Deflection Nom: État: aucune ------ Description (en) ------

These bracers are made from plates of durable mithral and gleam like the summer sun.

Activate Interact; Frequency once per day; Trigger A ranged weapon attack hits you but doesn’t critically hit; Requirements You are aware of the attack and not flat-footed; Effect The bracers send the missile off-course. You gain a +2 circumstance bonus to AC against the triggering attack. If this would cause the attack to be a failure, the attack misses you.
