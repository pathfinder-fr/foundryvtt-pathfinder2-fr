Name: Stunning Snare Nom: Piège étourdissant État: changé État d'origine: libre ------ Description (en) ------

You rig a snare to disorient a creature with a quick bash, leaving it with little ability to defend itself. The trap deals 6d6 bludgeoning damage to the first creature to enter its square; that creature must attempt a Reflex save.

Critical Success The creature is unaffected.
Success The creature takes half damage and is flat-footed for 1 round and stunned 1.
Failure The creature takes full damage and is flat-footed for 1 round and stunned 2.
Critical Failure The creature takes double damage and is flat-footed for 1 minute and stunned 4.

Succès critique La créature n'est pas affectée.
