Name: Stunning Snare Nom: Piège artisanal étourdissant État: libre ------ Description (en) ------

You rig a snare to disorient a creature with a quick bash, leaving it with little ability to defend itself. The trap deals 6d6 bludgeoning damage to the first creature to enter its square; that creature must attempt a @Check[type:reflex|dc:31] save.

Critical Success The creature is unaffected.

Success The creature takes half damage and is flat-footed for 1 round and stunned 1.

Failure The creature takes full damage and is flat-footed for 1 round and stunned 2.

Critical Failure The creature takes double damage and is flat-footed for 1 minute and stunned 4.
