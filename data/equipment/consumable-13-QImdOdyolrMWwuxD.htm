Name: Gorgon's Breath Nom: État: aucune ------ Description (en) ------

Gorgon’s breath is a fine powder that can easily enter living creatures’ bloodstreams through their lungs before binding to mucous membranes and causing any nearby soft tissues to harden.

Saving Throw DC 32 Fortitude; Onset 1 round; Maximum Duration 6 rounds; Stage 1 slowed 1 (1 round); Stage 2 4d6 bludgeoning damage and slowed 1 (1 round); Stage 3 petrified (1 round); Stage 4 petrified permanently
