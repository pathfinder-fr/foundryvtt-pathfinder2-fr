Name: Sickle (+1 Striking) Nom: État: aucune ------ Description (en) ------

Originally a farming tool used for reaping grain, this one‑handed weapon has a short wooden handle ending in a curved blade, sometimes sharpened on both sides.

This weapon has a +1 weapon potency rune (+1 item bonus to attack rolls with the weapon) and a striking rune (one extra damage die).
