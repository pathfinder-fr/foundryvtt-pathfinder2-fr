You weave plant matter among loose stones in an area with trees or a ceiling overhead. When a creature enters the square, the snare's square and each adjacent square becomes difficult terrain, and the triggering creature must attempt a @Check[type:reflex|dc:19|traits:uncommon|showDC:all] saving throw.
