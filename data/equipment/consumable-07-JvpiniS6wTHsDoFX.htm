Name: Sloughing Toxin Nom: Toxine de mollesse État: libre ------ Description (en) ------

Activate 2 Interact

This complex toxin makes the muscles beneath a creature's skin loose and malleable, which fleshwarpers find useful in their work. Rough or jerky movements the victim performs concentrate the toxin in subdermal muscles and eventually cause skin and muscle to slough away.

Saving Throw @Check[type:fortitude|dc:25]

Onset 1 round

Maximum Duration 1 hour

Stage 1 [[/r {1d6}[poison]]]{1d6 poison damage} for each action with the attack or move trait the victim uses (1 round)

Stage 2 [[/r {1d6}[poison]]]{1d6 poison damage} and [[/r {1d6}[slashing]]]{1d6 slashing damage} for each action with the attack, manipulate, or move trait the victim uses (1 round)

Stage 3 as Stage 2, but [[/r {1d6}[poison]]]{1d6 poison damage} and [[/r {2d6}[slashing]]]{2d6 slashing damage}, and forced movement also causes damage (1 hour)

------ Description (fr) ------

Cette toxine complexe rend les muscles sous la peau d'une créature mous et malléables, ce que les créateurs de distordus trouvent fort utile dans l'exercice de leur travail. Les mouvements brusques ou saccadés de la victime concentrent la toxine dans les muscles sous-cutanés et finissent par faire disparaître la peau et les muscles.
