Name: Unholy Nom: Impie (rune) État: changé État d'origine: officielle ------ Description (en) ------

An unholy rune instills fiendish power into the etched weapon. A weapon with this rune deals an additional 1d6 evil damage when it hits a good target. If you are good, you are enfeebled 2 while carrying or wielding this weapon.

Activate R command; Frequency once per day; Trigger You critically succeed at an attack roll against a good creature with the weapon; Effect The target takes persistent bleed damage equal to 1d8 per weapon damage die of the etched weapon.
