Name: Onyx Panther Nom: Panthère d'onyx État: changé État d'origine: officielle ------ Description (en) ------

This small stone is a stylized panther shape. When you activate it, you can move your full Speed (instead of half) during the triggering Sneak and any other time you Sneak this turn.

Activate Envision; Trigger You use a Sneak action; Requirements You are trained in Stealth
