Name: Marbles Nom: Billes de jeu État: libre ------ Description (en) ------

These tiny round balls are made of polished stone and colorful glass, and come in a bag of 200. You can pour marbles in an empty square adjacent to you with an Interact action. The first creature that moves into that square must succeed at a @Check[type:acrobatics|dc:13] check or @Check[type:reflex|dc:13] save (its choice) or fall @Compendium[pf2e.conditionitems.j91X7x0XSomq8d60]{Prone}. Once a creature enters a space with marbles, enough marbles are scattered that other creatures moving into that space don't need to attempt a roll to avoid falling.
