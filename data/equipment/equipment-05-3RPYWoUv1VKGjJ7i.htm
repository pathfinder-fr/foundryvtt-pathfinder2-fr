Name: Ring of Minor Arcana Nom: Anneau d'arcane mineures État: libre ------ Description (en) ------

This rose-gold ring is adorned in the center by a somewhat ominous-looking horned skull. This ring gives you the power to cast the following innate arcane cantrips any number of times each day: @Compendium[pf2e.spells-srd.gpzpAAAJ1Lza2JVl]{Detect Magic}, @Compendium[pf2e.spells-srd.pwzdSlJgYqN7bs2w]{Mage Hand}, and @Compendium[pf2e.spells-srd.Qw3fnUlaUbnn7ipC]{Prestidigitation}. Each is cast as a 1st-level spell. If you are an arcane spellcaster, these can instead be heightened to the level of your cantrips.
