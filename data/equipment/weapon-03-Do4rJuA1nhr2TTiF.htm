Name: Dwarven Daisy (Moderate) Nom: Marguerite naine (moyenne) État: changé État d'origine: libre ------ Description (en) ------

A dwarven daisy is a string of dozens of firecrackers that create tiny, loud explosions. The string ends with a short, wrapped fuse. Pulling off the wrapping in a quick motion lights the fuse and is done as part of the action to Strike. A dwarven daisy deals the listed fire damage and splash damage. A dwarven daisy also has a chance to dazzle its target. A creature struck by a dwarven daisy must succeed at a Fortitude save or become @Compendium[pf2e.conditionitems.TkIyaNPgTZFBCCuh]{Dazzled} for 1 round.

Activate A Strike

The firework deals 2d6 fire damage and 2 fire splash damage. The DC is 18.
