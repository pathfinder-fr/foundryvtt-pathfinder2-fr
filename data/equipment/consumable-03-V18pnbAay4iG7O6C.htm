Name: Detonating Gears Snare Nom: Piège d'engrenages détonant État: libre ------ Description (en) ------

This snare uses clockwork stressed almost to the breaking point, which activates with a powerful explosion that deals [[/r {3d8}[piercing]]]{3d8 Piercing Damage} to the first creature entering the snare's square. The creature must attempt a Reflex saving throw.

Critical Success The creature is unaffected.

Success The creature takes half damage.

Failure The creature takes full damage and [[/r {3}[persistent,bleed]]]{3 Persistent Bleed Damage} @Compendium[pf2e.conditionitems.lDVqvLKA6eF3Df60]{Persistent Bleed Damage}.

Critical Failure The creature takes double damage and [[/r {6}[persistent,bleed]]]{6 Persistent Bleed Damage} @Compendium[pf2e.conditionitems.lDVqvLKA6eF3Df60]{Persistent Bleed Damage}.
