Name: Elemental Wayfinder (Air) Nom: Guide élémentaire (air) État: libre ------ Description (en) ------

Pathfinder Society researchers recently created elemental wayfinders with the assistance of the Society's elemental allies. In addition to the effects of a @Compendium[pf2e.equipment-srd.gbwr57aT9ou8yKWT]{Wayfinder}, these wayfinders each grant the ability to cast a particular elemental spell once per day, and can assist Pathfinders in exploring areas associated with their element. Each has the trait associated with its element.

Activate 2 command

Frequency once per day

Effect The wayfinder can cast 4th-level @Compendium[pf2e.spells-srd.9AAkVUCwF6WVNNY2]{Lightning Bolt}.

Additionally, you can cast @Compendium[pf2e.spells-srd.TTwOKGqmZeKSyNMH]{Feather Fall} as a primal innate spell once every 10 minutes.
