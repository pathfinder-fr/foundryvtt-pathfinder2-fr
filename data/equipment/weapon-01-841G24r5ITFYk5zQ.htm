Name: Pernicious Spore Bomb (Lesser) Nom: Bombe de spores pernicieuses inférieure État: libre ------ Description (en) ------

This flask contains fast-growing mold spores, which quickly take root but just as quickly rot away. The bomb deals [[/r {1}[poison]]]{1poison Damage}, [[/r {1d4}[persistent,piercing]]] @Compendium[pf2e.conditionitems.lDVqvLKA6eF3Df60]{Persistent Piercing Damage}, and [[/r {1}[splash,poison]]]{1 poison splash damage}. Except on a critical failure, the bomb's splash area is coated in vegetation, becoming difficult terrain for 1 round.
