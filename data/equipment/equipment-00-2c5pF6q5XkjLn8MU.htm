Name: Mask (Ordinary) Nom: État: aucune ------ Description (en) ------

A mask helps you use the @Compendium[pf2e.actionspf2e.AJstokjdG6iDjVjE]{Impersonate} action to pass yourself off as someone you aren't.

This ordinary mask is made out of cheap material, such as paper-mâché or simple cloth. This can be specially fitted over another mask.
