This coin usually resembles ancient currency. An intricate matrix of gold and platinum wires inside the coin causes it to float an inch in the air and spin if placed on a wayfinder.

Activate command; Requirements The coin is levitating above a wayfinder; Effect You give the coin a message of 25 words or fewer. The message repeats in your voice the next time the coin is floated above a wayfinder. A Pathfinder’s coin can hold only one message at a time, and it replays its message only once.
