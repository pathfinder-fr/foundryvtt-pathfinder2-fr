Name: Dust of Disappearance Nom: Poudre de disparition État: officielle ------ Description (en) ------

This powder shimmers like a thousand tiny motes of light. Activating the dust by sprinkling it on yourself or a creature within reach casts a 4th-level @Compendium[pf2e.spells-srd.XXqE1eY3w3z6xJCB]{Invisibility} spell with a duration of 1 minute on that creature. This invisibility can't be negated or seen through by any spell of 3rd level or lower or any item of 5th level or lower.
