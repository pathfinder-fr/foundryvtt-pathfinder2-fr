Name: Mudrock Snare Nom: Piège artisanal de boue solidifiée État: libre ------ Description (en) ------

Fired clay covers a pit of thin mud interspersed with fragile vials of blue dragon blood. The first creature to step into the square breaks through the clay and sinks into the pit, fracturing the vials and rapidly hardening the mud as it touches the blue dragon blood. That creature must attempt a @Check[type:fortitude|dc:29] save as the mud hardens over its legs.

Critical Success The creature is unaffected.

Success The creature takes a -5-foot circumstance penalty to its Speed for 1 minute or until it Escapes (DC 27).
