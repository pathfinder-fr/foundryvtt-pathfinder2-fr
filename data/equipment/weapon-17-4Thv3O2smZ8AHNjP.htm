Name: Pernicious Spore Bomb (Major) Nom: Bombe de spores pernicieuses majeure État: libre ------ Description (en) ------

This flask contains fast-growing mold spores, which quickly take root but just as quickly rot away. You gain a +3 item bonus to attack rolls. The bomb deals [[/r {4}[poison]]]{4 poison Damage}, [[/r {4d4}[persistent,piercing]]] @Compendium[pf2e.conditionitems.lDVqvLKA6eF3Df60]{Persistent Piercing Damage}, and [[/r {4}[splash,poison]]]{4 poison splash damage}. Except on a critical failure, the bomb's splash area is coated in vegetation, becoming difficult terrain for 1 minute.
