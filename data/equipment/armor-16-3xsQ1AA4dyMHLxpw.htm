This suit of +2 greater resilient dragonhide full plate makes you look like a fearsome dragon. The armor comes in 10 varieties corresponding to the 10 common dragon types, though other varieties undoubtedly exist.

Activate Interact; Frequency once per day; Effect You unleash a bout of dragon breath with a shape, damage type, and saving throw corresponding to the type of dragon used to make the armor (shown on the table below). The breath weapon deals 14d6 damage; each creature in the area must attempt a DC 36 basic saving throw.

Craft Requirements The initial raw materials must include 1,250 gp of dragonhide.
