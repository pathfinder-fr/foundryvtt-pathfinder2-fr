Name: Pernicious Spore Bomb (Moderate) Nom: Bombe de spores pernicieuses modérée État: libre ------ Description (en) ------

This flask contains fast-growing mold spores, which quickly take root but just as quickly rot away. You gain a +1 item bonus to attack rolls. The bomb deals [[/r {2}[poison]]]{2 poison Damage}, [[/r {2d4}[persistent,piercing]]] @Compendium[pf2e.conditionitems.lDVqvLKA6eF3Df60]{Persistent Piercing Damage}, and [[/r {2}[splash,poison]]]{2 poison splash damage}. Except on a critical failure, the bomb's splash area is coated in vegetation, becoming difficult terrain for 1 round.
