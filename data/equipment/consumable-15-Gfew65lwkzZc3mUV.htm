Name: Disintegration Bolt Nom: État: aucune ------ Description (en) ------

The shaft of this bolt is scorched and blackened, and handling it coats your fingers with a fine black powder. When an activated disintegration bolt hits a target, it is subject to a @Compendium[pf2e.spells-srd.r7ihOgKv19eJQnik]{Disintegrate} spell requiring a DC 34 Fortitude save. As with the spell, a critical hit on the attack roll causes the target’s saving throw outcome to be one degree worse.

Activate Interact

Craft Requirements Supply one casting of disintegrate.
