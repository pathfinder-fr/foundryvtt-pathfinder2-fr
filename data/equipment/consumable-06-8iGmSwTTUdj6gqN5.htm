Name: Dust of Appearance Nom: Poudre d'apparition État: officielle ------ Description (en) ------

Stored in a small reed, this powder looks like a fine metallic dust. When you fling it in the air, it coats all creatures in a @Template[type:burst|distance:10] centered on a point within 5 feet of you. For 1 minute, the coated creatures can't be concealed or invisible, nor can they benefit from @Compendium[pf2e.spells-srd.j8vIoIEWElvpwkcI]{Mirror Image} or similar abilities that create illusory duplicates. Any illusions in the area of 3rd level or lower are revealed as such, although this does not end their effect.
