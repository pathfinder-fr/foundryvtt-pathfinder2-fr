Name: Dagger of Venom Nom: Dague venimeuse État: changé État d'origine: officielle ------ Description (en) ------

The serrated blade of this +1 striking dagger has a greenish tinge, and the hilt is sculpted to look like the head of a serpent about to strike.

When you critically succeed at an attack roll with the dagger of venom, the target becomes sickened 1 unless it succeeds at a DC 19 Fortitude save. This is a poison effect.

In addition, you can activate the dagger to poison a creature with a more potent poison.

Activate F Interact

Frequency once per day

Trigger You damage a creature with the dagger of venom

Effect You poison the creature you hit with dagger venom.

Dagger Venom (poison)

Saving Throw Fortitude DC 21

Maximum Duration 4 rounds.

Stage 1 1d8 poison damage and enfeebled 1.
