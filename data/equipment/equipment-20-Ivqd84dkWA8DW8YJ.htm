Name: Ring of Spell Turning Nom: Anneau de renvoi de sorts État: changé État d'origine: officielle ------ Description (en) ------

This golden ring has three diamonds set into its face.

Activate R envision

Trigger You are targeted by a spell

Effect The ring replicates the effects of an 8th-level @Compendium[pf2e.spells-srd.OOELvfkTedBMlWtq]{Spell Turning} with a counteract modifier of [[/r 1d20+35]], possibly causing the triggering spell to reflect back on its caster.

The ring can reflect no more than 9 total levels of spells per day. If you activate the ring to reflect a spell that would exceed this limit, the attempt fails, but the attempted usage of the ring does not count toward the daily limit.
