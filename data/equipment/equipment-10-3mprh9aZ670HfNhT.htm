Name: Maestro's Instrument (Moderate) Nom: Instrument de maestro modéré État: officielle ------ Description (en) ------

A maestro's instrument can be crafted in the form of any variety of handheld musical instrument. A moderate maestro's instrument grants you a +2 item bonus to Performance checks while playing music with the instrument.

Activate D Interact

Frequency once per day

Effect You can play the instrument to produce the effects of an 4th level (DC 27) @Compendium[pf2e.spells-srd.vLA0q0WOK2YPuJs6]{Charm} spell.

Craft Requirements You must supply a casting of charm of the appropriate level.

Automatically applied effects:

  • +2 item bonus to Performance checks while playing music with the instrument
