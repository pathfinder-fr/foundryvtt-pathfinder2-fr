Name: Fire Box Nom: État: aucune ------ Description (en) ------

Anyone who opens the box triggers a clockwork mechanism that unleashes a @Template[type:cone|distance:15]{15‑foot cone} of fire. The cone issues forth in a random direction determined by the GM but always including the creature who opened the box. Those within the cone must succeed at a basic Reflex save or take [[/r {4d6}[fire]]]{4d6 fire damage}.
