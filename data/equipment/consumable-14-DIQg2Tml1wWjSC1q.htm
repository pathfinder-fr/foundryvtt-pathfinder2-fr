Name: Engulfing Snare Nom: Piège artisanal engloutissant État: libre ------ Description (en) ------

You arrange a spiky cage of bones, particularly tough vegetation, or other material to spring up when disturbed. The snare deals [[/r {9d8}[piercing]]]{9d8 piercing damage} to the first creature to enter this square; that creature must attempt a @Check[type:reflex|dc:31] save.

Critical Success The creature is unaffected.

Success The creature is knocked aside by the snare's deployment, taking half damage, and is @Compendium[pf2e.conditionitems.AJh5ex99aV6VTggg]{Flat-Footed} until the end of its next turn.

Failure The creature is captured by the cage, taking full damage and falling @Compendium[pf2e.conditionitems.j91X7x0XSomq8d60]{Prone}. It is @Compendium[pf2e.conditionitems.eIcWbB5o3pP6OIMe]{Immobilized} while it remains within the cage. It can get free by @Compendium[pf2e.actionspf2e.SkZAQRkLLkmBQNB9]{Escaping} (DC 31) or by destroying the cage (Hardness 5, HP 30, object immunities).

Critical Failure As failure, but the creature takes double damage.

Échec La créature est capturée par la cage, subit tous les dégâts et tombe @Compendium[pf2e.conditionitems.j91X7x0XSomq8d60]{A terre}. Elle est @Compendium[pf2e.conditionitems.eIcWbB5o3pP6OIMe]{Immobilisée} tant qu'elle reste dans la cage. Elle peut se libérer en tentant de @Compendium[pf2e.actionspf2e.SkZAQRkLLkmBQNB9]{s'Échapper} (DD 31) ou en détruisant la cage (solidité 5, PV 30, immunités aux objets).

Échec critique Comme un échec, mais la créature subit le double des dégâts.
