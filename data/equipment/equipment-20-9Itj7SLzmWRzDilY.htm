Name: Unending Youth Nom: État: auto-trad ------ Description (en) ------

You cease aging, and if you were older than young adult in age, you become a young adult again. You gain a +4 status bonus to saves against death effects and resistance to negative damage equal to half your level.

Once per day, from any distance, Abrogail Thrune II can recite a mandatory appearance clause in your Thrune contract as a 3-action activity to summon you to her side, where you are controlled by Abrogail for 1 minute before you return.
