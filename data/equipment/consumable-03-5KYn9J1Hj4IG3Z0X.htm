Name: Snapleaf Nom: Bouton de feuille État: libre ------ Description (en) ------

Usage affixed to armor

Activate R Interact

Trigger You begin to fall

This small, crystalline carving takes the shape of a tree leaf and attaches to armor or clothing with a simple strap.

When you activate the snapleaf, you gain the benefits of @Compendium[pf2e.spells-srd.TTwOKGqmZeKSyNMH]{Feather Fall} and a 2nd-level @Compendium[pf2e.spells-srd.XXqE1eY3w3z6xJCB]{Invisibility} spell for 1 minute or until you stop falling, whichever comes first.
