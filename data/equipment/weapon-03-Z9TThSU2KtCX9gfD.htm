Name: Vexing Vapor (Moderate) Nom: Vapeur contrariante modérée État: libre ------ Description (en) ------

Activate 1 Strike

This flask contains a fine red powder made from toxic berries. You gain a +1 item bonus to attack rolls. A moderate vexing vapor bomb deals [[/r {2d6}[mental]]]{2d6 mental damage} and [[/r {2}[splash,mental]]]{2 mental splash damage}. On a hit, the target must succeed at a @Check[type:flat|dc:5] check before taking actions with the concentrate trait. This lasts until the end of its next turn (1 minute on a critical hit).
