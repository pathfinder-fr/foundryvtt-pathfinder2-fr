Name: Mask of the Banshee (Greater) Nom: Masque de la Banshie supérieur État: changé État d'origine: libre ------ Description (en) ------

This ice-blue half-mask is adorned with a wicked silver grin that covers the wearer's mouth, leaving the rest of the face uncovered. You gain a +3 item bonus to Intimidation checks.

Activate 2 envision, Interact

Frequency once per day

Effect The mask emits a soul-chilling scream that deals [[/r 8d10]] negative damage to each living creature in a @Template[type:emanation|distance:40] (@Check[type:fortitude|dc:41|basic:true|name:Greater Mask of the Banshee] save).

Craft Requirements Supply a casting of wail of the banshee.

Automatically applied effects:

  • +3 item bonus to Intimidation checks
