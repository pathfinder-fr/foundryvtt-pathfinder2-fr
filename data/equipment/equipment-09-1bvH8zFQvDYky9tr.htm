Name: Pendant of the Occult (Greater) Nom: Pendentif des sciences occultes supérieur État: officielle ------ Description (en) ------

This amulet is hollow and shaped in the form of an unblinking eye. Its cavity typically holds some fragment of occult text. While wearing the pendant, you gain a +2 item bonus to Occultism checks, and you can cast the @Compendium[pf2e.spells-srd.izcxFQFwf3woCnFs]{Guidance} cantrip as an occult innate spell.

The pendant can be activated.

Activate 10 minutes (Cast a Spell)

Frequency once per day

Effect You cast a 4th-level @Compendium[pf2e.spells-srd.yM3KTTSAIHhyuP14]{Dream Message} spell.

Automatically applied effects:

  • +2 item bonus to Occultism checks
