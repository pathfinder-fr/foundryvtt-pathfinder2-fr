Name: Wand of Slaying (9th-Level Spell) Nom: État: aucune ------ Description (en) ------

This polished black wand has a green gem at the tip, and anyone who looks into it sees a reflection of a grinning skull instead of their face.

Activate Cast a Spell; Frequency once per day, plus overcharge; Effect You cast @Item[finger of death] of the indicated level. If the spell slays its target, the corpse releases negative energy in a 20-foot emanation, dealing negative damage equal to double the spell’s level.

Craft Requirements Supply a casting of finger of death of the appropriate level.
