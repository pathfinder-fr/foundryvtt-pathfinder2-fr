Name: Lich Dust Nom: Poussière de liche État: officielle ------ Description (en) ------

Dust salvaged from the remains of a destroyed lich has paralytic properties that make it a valuable poison.

Saving Throw DC 28 Fortitude; Onset10 minutes; Maximum Duration 6 minutes; Stage 1 fatigued (1 minute); Stage 2 5d6 poison damage and fatigued (1 minute); Stage 3 5d6 poison damage, fatigued, and paralyzed (1 minute)

Activate Interact
