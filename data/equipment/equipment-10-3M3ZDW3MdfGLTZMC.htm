This chime's carvings depict skulls and other grim icons. When activated, it creates an aura of @Compendium[pf2e.spells-srd.4koZzrnMXhhosn0D]{Fear} in a @Template[type:emanation|distance:5] for 1 round. Les sculptures de ce carillon représentent des crânes et autres icônes sinistres.
