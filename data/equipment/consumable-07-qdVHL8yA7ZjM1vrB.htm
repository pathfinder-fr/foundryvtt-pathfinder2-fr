This complex toxin makes the muscles beneath a creature’s skin loose and malleable, which fleshwarpers find useful in their work. Rough or jerky movements the victim performs concentrate the toxin in subdermal muscles and eventually cause skin and muscle to slough away.

Saving Throw DC 25 Fortitude;

Onset 1 round;

Maximum Duration 1 hour;

Stage 1 [[/r 1d6 #poison]] poison damage for each action with the attack or move trait the victim uses (1 round);

Stage 2 [[/r 1d6 #poison]] poison damage and [[/r 1d6 #slashing]] slashing damage for each action with the attack, manipulate, or move trait the victim uses (1 round);

Stage 3 as Stage 2, but 1d6 poison damage and [[/r 2d6 #slashing]] slashing damage, and forced movement also causes damage (1 hour)
