Name: Acid Spitter Nom: Crachat d'acide État: libre ------ Description (en) ------

This tin clockwork lizard is activated when a creature moves adjacent to it, at which point it spits out a glob of acid. The target must succeed at a Reflex saving throw or take [[/r {3d6}[acid]]]{3d6 acid damage}.

Critical Success The creature is unaffected.

Success The creature takes full damage.

Failure The creature takes full damage and [[/r {5}[persistent,acid]]] @Compendium[pf2e.conditionitems.lDVqvLKA6eF3Df60]{Persistent Acid Damage}.

Critical Failure The creature takes double damage and [[/r {10}[persistent,acid]]] @Compendium[pf2e.conditionitems.lDVqvLKA6eF3Df60]{Persistent Acid Damage}.
