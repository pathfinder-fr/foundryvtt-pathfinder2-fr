Ammunition any

Activate 1 Interact

Juxtaposition ammunition quivers with anticipation, as if the projectile wishes to constantly be in motion. When an activated juxtaposition ammunition hits a creature, it doesn't deal the Strike's normal damage. Instead, the target must succeed at a Will save or be teleported to a safe, unoccupied square of your choice within 60 feet of its original position and within the weapon's first range increment. On a critical failure, the target is also @Compendium[pf2e.conditionitems.fesd1n5eVhpCSS18]{Sickened 1} from the gut‑wrenching sensation of sudden movement. Any relocation from the juxtaposition ammunition is forced movement.
