Name: Cape of the Mountebank Nom: Cape de prestidigateur État: officielle ------ Description (en) ------

This bright red-and-gold cape is often interlaced with glittery threads and serves as a distraction. While wearing the cape, you gain a +2 item bonus to Deception checks.

Activate D Interact

Frequency once per day

Effect You cast @Compendium[pf2e.spells-srd.VlNcjmYyu95vOUe8]{Dimension Door}.
