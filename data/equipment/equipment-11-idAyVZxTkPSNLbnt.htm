Name: Quill of Passage Nom: Plume de passage État: libre ------ Description (en) ------

This black-feathered quill has a glowing nib with a small amount of glowing ink inside it.

Activate 2 command, Interact

Frequency once per day

Effect Placing the tip of the quill against a wall of wood, plaster, or stone and speaking a command word causes the ink to flow from the nib onto the wall in the shape of a glowing doorway, casting @Compendium[pf2e.spells-srd.115Xp9E38CJENhNS]{Passwall} on the touched surface.
