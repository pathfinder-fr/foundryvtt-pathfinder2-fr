Name: Hat of Disguise (Greater) Nom: Couvre-chef de déguisement supérieur État: officielle ------ Description (en) ------

This ordinary-looking hat allows you to cloak yourself in illusions.

Activate D Interact

Frequency any number of times per day

Effect The hat casts a 2nd-level @Compendium[pf2e.spells-srd.i35dpZFI7jZcRoBo]{Illusory Disguise} spell on you. While setting up the disguise, you can magically alter the hat to appear as a comb, ribbon, helm, or other piece of headwear.
