Name: Glaive (+1 Striking) Nom: État: aucune ------ Description (en) ------

This polearm consists of a long, single‑edged blade on the end of a 7‑foot pole. It is extremely effective at delivering lethal cuts at a distance.

This weapon has a +1 weapon potency rune (+1 item bonus to attack rolls with the weapon) and a striking rune (one extra damage die).
