Name: Death Knell Powder Nom: Poudre de mise à mort État: changé État d'origine: libre ------ Description (en) ------

Activate 2 Interact;

This poison is made from toxic spores of death knell mushrooms, which are reduced to a gray powder and suffused with necromantic energy. If you're reduced to 0 Hit Points while under the effect of death knell powder, you must succeed at a Will save or die (this is a death effect). If you die, a random creature adjacent to you is bolstered by your departing life force and gains 20 temporary HP and a +1 status bonus to attack and damage rolls for 10 minutes.

Saving Throw Fortitude;

Maximum Duration 6 rounds;

Stage 1[[/r 7d6 #poison]] poison damage (1 round);

Stage 2[[/r 9d6 #poison]] poison damage (1 round);

Stage 3 [[/r 12d6 #poison]] poison damage (1 round)

Craft Requirements Supply one casting of @Compendium[pf2e.spells-srd.dLdRqT6UxTKlsPgp]{Death Knell}.
