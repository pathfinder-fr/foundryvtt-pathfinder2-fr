Name: Horn of Fog Nom: État: aucune ------ Description (en) ------

This large ram’s horn perpetually glistens with tiny droplets of water, much like condensation.

Activate Interact; Frequency once per hour; Effect You activate the horn by blowing deeply into it, causing it to issue forth a low blast and cast a 2nd-level @Compendium[pf2e.spells-srd.9XHmC2JgTUIQ1CCm]{Obscuring Mist} spell. You can Dismiss the mist at any time by blowing a second note on the horn using an Interact action.
