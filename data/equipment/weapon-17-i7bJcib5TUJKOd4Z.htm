Name: Ghost Charge (Major) Nom: Charge fantôme majeure État: libre ------ Description (en) ------

Usage held in 1 hand

Activate A Strike

These spring-loaded metal canisters contain a mixture of chemicals and salts that drain and disintegrate nearby undead creatures.

A ghost charge gives a +3 item bonus to attack rolls, deals [[/r 4d8]] positive damage and 4 positive splash damage, though as usual for positive damage, this damage harms only undead and creatures with negative healing. A primary target that takes damage from a ghost charge becomes @Compendium[pf2e.conditionitems.MIRkyAjyBeXivMa7]{Enfeebled 2} until the start of your next turn.

Ghost charges are designed to explode even on contact with a spiritual substance, making them ideal for damaging incorporeal undead.
