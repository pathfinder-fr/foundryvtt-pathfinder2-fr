Name: Spear (+1 Striking) Nom: État: aucune ------ Description (en) ------

A long metal shaft ending with a metal spike, a spear can be used one‑handed as a melee weapon and can be thrown.

This weapon has a +1 weapon potency rune (+1 item bonus to attack rolls with the weapon) and a striking rune (one extra damage die).
