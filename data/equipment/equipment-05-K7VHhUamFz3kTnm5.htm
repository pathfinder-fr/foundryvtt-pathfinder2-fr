Name: Goggles of Night Nom: Lunettes de nyctalope État: officielle ------ Description (en) ------

The opaque crystal lenses of these sleek goggles do not obscure vision, but rather enhance it. While wearing the goggles, you gain a +1 item bonus to Perception checks involving sight.

Activate Interact; Frequency once per day; Effect Rotating the lenses 90 degrees, you gain darkvision for 1 hour.
