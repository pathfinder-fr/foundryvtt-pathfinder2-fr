Using a mixture of dinosaur bones and the bones of your ancestors to focus your magic, you animate lizards and dinosaurs to carry messages or serve as mounts. You can cast @Compendium[pf2e.spells-srd.yhz9fF69uwRhnHix]{Animal Messenger} and @Compendium[pf2e.spells-srd.WPKJOhEihhcIm2uQ]{Phantom Steed} as primal innate spells once per day each. The animal messenger is always a Tiny dinosaur or lizard, and your innate spell transforms it from bones to flesh, even if there are no dinosaurs or lizards nearby. The phantom steed looks like a dinosaur rather than a horse but functions identically.
