Name: Eye For Treasure Nom: État: aucune ------ Description (en) ------

You know good artisanship when you see it and can wax poetic about crafting techniques and forms.

You become trained in Crafting and gain a +1 circumstance bonus on all Crafting checks made to @Compendium[pf2e.actionspf2e.B0Eu3EfwIa9kyDEA]{Recall Knowledge}. If you would automatically become trained in Crafting (from your background or class, for example), you instead become trained in a skill of your choice.

In addition, you gain the @Compendium[world.feats-apg-data-entry.54QgGnYbgw6hM3Uc]{Crafter’s Appraisal} skill feat, enabling you to identify magic items using the Crafting skill.
