Name: Frightful Condemnation Nom: Condamnation effrayante PrereqEN: |Vigilante Dedication|Member of the Grey Gardeners|Harsh Judgement PrereqFR: Dévouement de justicier|Membre des Jardiniers gris|Jugement sévère État: libre ------ Description (en) ------

Trigger You hit your condemned foe with a melee Strike in the same turn during which you condemned it with Harsh Judgment.

Requirements You have condemned a foe to death with Harsh Judgment.

Your willingness to follow through on your pitiless judgment is frightful to the person you have condemned, and they begin to dread what you will do to them next. Your condemned foe is @Compendium[pf2e.conditionitems.TBSHQspnbcqxsmjL]{Frightened 1}, or @Compendium[pf2e.conditionitems.TBSHQspnbcqxsmjL]{Frightened 2} if the triggering Strike was a critical hit.

Déclencheur Vous touchez votre ennemi condamné avec une arme au corps-à-corps avec une Frappe au corps-à-corps au cours du tour durant lequel vous l'avez condamné avec Jugement sévère.
