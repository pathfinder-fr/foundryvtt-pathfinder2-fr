Name: Scalding Spit Nom: Crachat brûlant État: libre ------ Description (en) ------

Prerequisites Torch Goblin

Your bodily fluids burn with surprising volatility, as if you ran on oil instead of blood. As long as you are taking @Compendium[pf2e.conditionspf2e.lDVqvLKA6eF3Df60]{Persistent fire Damage}, you gain a boiling spit ranged unarmed attack with a range of 30 feet that deals 1d6 fire damage.
