Avantage: Vous gagnez le sort initial d'un autre ordre de druide. ------ Description (en) ------

You have delved deeper into the teaching of a new order, gaining access to a coveted order spell. Choose an order you have selected with @Compendium[pf2e.feats-srd.9onf3uGvZRnNLPR6]{Order Explorer}. You gain the initial order spell from that order.

Special You can take this feat multiple times. Each time you do, you must choose a different order you have selected with Order Explorer.
