Requirements You haven't used this ability since you last Raged

You breathe deeply and exhale powerful energy in a @Template[type:cone|distance:30] or @Template[type:line|distance:60], dealing [[/r (@actor.level)d6#Dragon's Rage Breath]]{1d6 damage per level}. The area and damage type match those of your dragon (see @Compendium[pf2e.classfeatures.VDot7CDcXElxmkkz]{Table 3-4}).

If you used this ability in the last hour, the area and the damage are halved (15-foot cone or @Template[type:line|distance:30]; [[/r (floor(@actor.level/2))d6#Dragon's Rage Breath]]{1d6 damage for every 2 levels}).

Each creature in the area must attempt a basic Reflex save.
