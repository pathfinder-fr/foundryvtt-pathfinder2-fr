Members of your cult frequently pass themselves off as worshippers of other religions. You can use Occultism instead of Deception to @Compendium[pf2e.actionspf2e.AJstokjdG6iDjVjE]{Impersonate} a typical worshipper of another faith or to @Compendium[pf2e.actionspf2e.ewwCglB7XOPLUz72]{Lie} specifically to claim you are a member of the faith you are Impersonating.
