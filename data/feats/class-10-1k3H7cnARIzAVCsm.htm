Fear makes your foes weak and more vulnerable to your attacks.

You gain a circumstance bonus to damage rolls for Strikes against @Compendium[pf2e.conditionitems.TBSHQspnbcqxsmjL]{Frightened} creatures. The bonus is equal to double the target's frightened value.

If you have master proficiency in Intimidation, increase the bonus to triple the target's frightened value.
