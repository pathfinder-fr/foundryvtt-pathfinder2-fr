You've learned to apply your flashy techniques to thrown weapons as easily as melee attacks.

When you have @Compendium[pf2e.classfeatures.LzYi0OuOoypNb6jd]{Panache}, you apply your precise strike damage on ranged Strikes you make with a thrown weapon within that weapon's first range increment. The thrown weapon must be an agile or finesse weapon.

This also allows you to make a thrown weapon ranged Strike for @Compendium[pf2e.classfeatures.pyo0vmxUFIFX2GNl]{Confident Finisher} and any other finisher that includes a Strike that can benefit from your precise strike.
