You take on a celestial countenance, appearing like a type of celestial who serves your deity; for example, as an angel, you would gain a halo and feathery wings.

You gain a fly Speed equal to your Speed. You gain @Compendium[pf2e.ancestryfeatures.HHVQDp61ehcpdiU8]{Darkvision} if you don't already have it, and you gain the celestial trait and the trait appropriate to the type of servitor you've become (archon, angel, or azata, for example).
