You favor traditional halfling weapons, so you've learned how to use them more effectively. You have the trained proficiency with the @Compendium[pf2e.equipment-srd.UCH4myuFnokGv0vF]{Sling}, @Compendium[pf2e.equipment-srd.DV4qelKHrviM0O5i]{Halfling Sling Staff}, and @Compendium[pf2e.equipment-srd.7tKkkF8eZ4iCLJtp]{Shortsword}.

You gain access to all uncommon halfling weapons. For the purpose of determining your proficiency, martial halfling weapons are simple weapons and advanced halfling weapons are martial weapons.
