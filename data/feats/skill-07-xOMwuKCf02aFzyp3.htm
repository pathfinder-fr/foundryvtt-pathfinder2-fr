Kassi Aziril taught you her techniques that originated the modern use of Battle Medicine. If you are legendary in Medicine, you can choose to reduce the target's @Compendium[pf2e.conditionitems.TBSHQspnbcqxsmjL]{Frightened} or @Compendium[pf2e.conditionitems.dfCMdR4wnpbYNTix]{Stunned} condition by 1 instead, and if you have the @Compendium[pf2e.feats-srd.yTLGclKtWVFZLKIz]{Godless Healing} feat , you can choose to reduce the target's @Compendium[pf2e.conditionitems.e1XGnhKNSQIm5IXg]{Stupefied} or @Compendium[pf2e.conditionitems.4D2KBtexWXa6oUMR]{Drained} condition by 1 instead. If you have the @Compendium[pf2e.feats-srd.mEk2POFNU1Q0TQg2]{Mortal Healing} feat, you can reduce all available conditions by 1 for a target who hasn't benefited from positive or healing magic in the last 24 hours, and if you roll a critical success before applying the effects of the feat, you reduce all available conditions by 2 for that target instead.
