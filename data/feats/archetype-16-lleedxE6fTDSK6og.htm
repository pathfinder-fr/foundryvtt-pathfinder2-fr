Name: Resuscitate Nom: État: aucune ------ Description (en) ------

Prerequisites @Compendium[pf2e.feats-srd.MJg24e9fJd7OASvF]{Medic Dedication}, legendary in Medicine

Requirements You are holding healer’s tools or are wearing them and have a hand free. Also, the target’s body is mostly intact.

You can use Medicine to resuscitate the dead. Attempt a DC 40 Medicine check to revive a dead creature that has been dead for no more than 3 rounds. If you succeed, the target returns to life with the effects of @Compendium[pf2e.spells-srd.IkGYwHRLhkuoGReG]{Raise Dead}, except it still has the @Compendium[pf2e.conditionspf2e.Yl48xTdMh3aeQYL2]{Wounded} condition it had before @Compendium[pf2e.conditionspf2e.yZRUzMqrMmfLu0V1]{Dying}, increased by 1 (or wounded 1 if it wasn’t wounded before dying). Whether you succeed or fail, the target is temporarily immune to Resuscitate for 1 day.
