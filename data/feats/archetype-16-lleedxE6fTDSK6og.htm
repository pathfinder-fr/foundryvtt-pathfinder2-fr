Name: Resuscitate Nom: Ressusciter PrereqEN: |Medic Dedication|legendary in Medicine PrereqFR: Dévouement de Médecin|Légendaire en Médecine État: libre ------ Description (en) ------

Requirements You are holding @Compendium[pf2e.equipment-srd.s1vB3HdXjMigYAnY]{Healer's Tools} or are wearing them and have a hand free. Also, the target's body is mostly intact.

You can use Medicine to resuscitate the dead. Attempt a @Check[type:medicine|dc:40|showDC:all] check to revive a dead creature that has been dead for no more than 3 rounds. If you succeed, the target returns to life with the effects of @Compendium[pf2e.spells-srd.IkGYwHRLhkuoGReG]{Raise Dead}, except it still has the @Compendium[pf2e.conditionitems.Yl48xTdMh3aeQYL2]{Wounded} condition it had before @Compendium[pf2e.conditionitems.yZRUzMqrMmfLu0V1]{Dying}, increased by 1 (or wounded 1 if it wasn't wounded before dying). Whether you succeed or fail, the target is temporarily immune to Resuscitate for 1 day.
