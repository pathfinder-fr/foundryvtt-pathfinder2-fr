Name: Magic Sense (Sorcerer) Nom: Perception de la magie État: officielle ------ Description (en) ------

You have a literal sixth sense for ambient magic in your vicinity. You can sense the presence of magic auras as though you were always using a 1st-level detect magic spell. This detects magic in your field of vision only. When you Seek, you gain the benefits of a 3rd-level detect magic spell on things you see (in addition to the normal benefits of Seeking). You can turn this sense off and on with a free action at the start or the end of your turn. Special (Wizard) This feat has the Arcane trait
