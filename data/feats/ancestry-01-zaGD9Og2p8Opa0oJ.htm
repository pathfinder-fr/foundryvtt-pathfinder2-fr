You're always on the lookout for supplies and valuables. Each time you use the @Compendium[pf2e.actionspf2e.BlAOM2X92SI6HMtJ]{Seek} action to search for objects (including secret doors and hazards), you can search for objects in your choice of a @Template[type:emanation|distance:10] around you or an adjacent 15-foot-by-15-foot area, rather than a single adjacent 10-foot-by-10-foot area.

Vous êtes toujours à l'affût de fournitures ou de choses de valeur.
