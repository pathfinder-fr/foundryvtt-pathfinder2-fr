Avantage: Votre compagnon animal bénéficie du don Camouflage ------ Description (en) ------

You've trained your animal companion to blend in to its surroundings. Your animal companion gains the benefit of the @Compendium[pf2e.feats-srd.qqe5VmcO1s8iTOfd]{Camouflage} feat. If your companion is a specialized ambusher, its proficiency rank for Stealth increases to master (or legendary if it was already master).
