Avantage: Vous pouvez utiliser Forme de plante. ------ Description (en) ------

You can take the form of a plant creature.

Add the forms listed in @Compendium[pf2e.spells-srd.zCcfPS4y5SrZzU2x]{Plant Form} to your wild shape list; if you don't have wild shape, you can instead cast plant form once per day, heightened to the same level as your highest-level druid spell slot.

Whenever you're polymorphed into another form using wild shape, you gain resistance 5 to poison.
