Name: Dragonslayer Oath (Paladin) Nom: Serment du pourfendeur de dragons (Paladin) PrereqEN: |tenets of good|paladin cause PrereqFR: principes du bien|cause de paladin État: libre ------ Description (en) ------

You've sworn to slay evil dragons. Add the following tenet to your code after the others: "You must slay evil dragons you encounter as long as you have a reasonable chance of success.

Your @Compendium[pf2e.actionspf2e.EAP98XaChJEbgKcK]{Retributive Strike} gains a +4 circumstance bonus to damage against an evil dragon, or +6 if you have master proficiency with the weapon you used.

You don't consider evil dragons to be legitimate authorities, even in nations they rule.
