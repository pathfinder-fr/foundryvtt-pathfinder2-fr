You can use Survival in place of Perception to roll initiative when underground in a natural location (not a structure) even if you weren't tracking or otherwise using Survival before the encounter.

You can also use Survival instead of Perception to notice traps in natural locations when underground; if you find a snare in this way, you can also use Survival instead of Thivery to Disable the Device.

Special You can select this feat more than once. Each time you select it, the feat applies to a new type of terrain.
