Avantage: Vous gagnez Vision nocturne ou Vision dans le noir. ------ Description (en) ------

As your connection to water grows, you can see more easily in places with less light, such as the depths of the ocean. You gain @Compendium[pf2e.ancestryfeatures.DRtaqOHXTRtGRIUT]{Low-Light Vision}, or you gain @Compendium[pf2e.ancestryfeatures.HHVQDp61ehcpdiU8]{Darkvision} if your ancestry already has low-light vision.

Special If your ancestry has neither low-light vision nor darkvision, you can take this ancestry feat a second time to gain darkvision. You can't retrain out of this feat.
