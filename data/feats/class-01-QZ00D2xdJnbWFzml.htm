Name: Subtle Delivery Nom: État: aucune ------ Description (en) ------

You can capably deliver toxins with a @Compendium[pf2e.equipment-srd.FPwsiGqMCNPLHmjX]{Blowgun}. Your blowgun @Compendium[pf2e.actionspf2e.VjxZFuUXrCU94MWR]{Strikes} can apply injury poisons even if they deal no damage due to a creature’s resistance.

If you critically succeed at an attack roll using a blowgun loaded with a dart you’ve poisoned and the target rolls a failure on the poison’s initial save, the target critically fails instead.
