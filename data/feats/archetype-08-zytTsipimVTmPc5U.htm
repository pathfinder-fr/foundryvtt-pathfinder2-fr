If you have @Compendium[pf2e.feats-srd.mf2cdCRV8uowOMOm]{Dueling Riposte}, you can use it when an enemy within your reach critically fails a @Compendium[pf2e.actionspf2e.VjxZFuUXrCU94MWR]{Strike} against an ally adjacent to you, not just against yourself.
