You draw life from the water around you. If you submerge in water and rest for 10 minutes, you regain Hit Points equal to your [[/r ((floor(@actor.level/2))*(@actor.abilities.con.mod))[healing]]]{Constitution modifier × half your level}.

Vous vous régénérez en puisant la vie de l'eau qui vous entoure.
