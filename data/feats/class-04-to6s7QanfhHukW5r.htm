Name: Barreling Charge Nom: État: aucune ------ Description (en) ------

Prerequisites trained in Athletics

You rush forward, moving enemies aside to reach your foe. You @Compendium[pf2e.actionspf2e.Bcxarzksqt9ezrs6]{Stride}, attempting to move through your enemies’ spaces, and make a melee @Compendium[pf2e.actionspf2e.VjxZFuUXrCU94MWR]{Strike}.

Roll an Athletics check and compare the result to the Fortitude DC of each creature whose space you attempt to move through during your Stride, moving through their space on a success but ending your movement before entering their space on a failure.

You can use Barreling Charge to @Compendium[pf2e.actionspf2e.H6v1VgowHaKHnVlG]{Burrow}, @Compendium[pf2e.actionspf2e.pprgrYQ1QnIDGZiy]{Climb}, @Compendium[pf2e.actionspf2e.cS9nfDRGD83bNU1p]{Fly}, or @Compendium[pf2e.actionspf2e.c8TGiZ48ygoSPofx]{Swim} instead of Stride if you have the corresponding movement type.
