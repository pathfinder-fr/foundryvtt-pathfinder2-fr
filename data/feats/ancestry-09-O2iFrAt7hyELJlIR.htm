Avantage: Vous vous échappez avec facilité des effets qui vous entravent, vous saisissent ou vous immobilisent. ------ Description (en) ------

Requirements You are @Compendium[pf2e.conditionitems.kWc1fhmv9LBiTuei]{Grabbed}, @Compendium[pf2e.conditionitems.eIcWbB5o3pP6OIMe]{Immobilized}, or @Compendium[pf2e.conditionitems.VcDeM8A5oI6VqhbM]{Restrained}.

You have some feature that allows you to quickly evade effects that restrain you. Attempt a check to Escape. If you succeed, you can Stride, Step, or make a Strike with a melee unarmed attack targeting the creature you Escaped from. If you fail, you instead attempt to Escape a second time.

You increase your multiple attack penalty as appropriate for the actions you took, but only after completing both actions.

------ Description (fr) ------

Conditions Vous êtes @Compendium[pf2e.conditionitems.kWc1fhmv9LBiTuei]{Agrippé/Empoigné}, @Compendium[pf2e.conditionitems.eIcWbB5o3pP6OIMe]{Immobilisé} ou @Compendium[pf2e.conditionitems.VcDeM8A5oI6VqhbM]{Entravé}.
