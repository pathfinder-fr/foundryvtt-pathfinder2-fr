You can react whenever a foe leaves a gap in their defenses. At the start of each enemy's turn, you gain an extra reaction you can use only during that turn to perform an @Compendium[pf2e.classfeatures.Jtn7IugykXDlIoZq]{Opportune Riposte}, or an @Compendium[pf2e.classfeatures.hmShTfPOcTaKgbf4]{Attack of Opportunity} if you have that reaction.
