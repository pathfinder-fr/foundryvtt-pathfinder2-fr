You open vestigial eyes, unfurl tympanic flaps of skin, or otherwise enhance your senses. Until the start of your next turn, you gain the following benefits: you can't be flanked; when you Seek for creatures, you can scan a @Template[type:cone|distance:60] or a @Template[type:burst|distance:30] instead of the normal area; and when you Seek for hidden objects, you can search a 15-foot square instead of the normal area.
