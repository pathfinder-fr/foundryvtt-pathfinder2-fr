Name: Dodge Away Nom: S'esquiver au loin PrereqEN: |Acrobat Dedication PrereqFR: Dévouement d'Acrobate État: libre ------ Description (en) ------

Trigger You are the target of a melee attack.

Requirements You're aware of the attack and aren't @Compendium[pf2e.conditionitems.AJh5ex99aV6VTggg]{Flat-Footed}.

You use your acrobatic prowess to evade an attack, using momentum to keep yourself moving, if you choose.

You gain a +1 circumstance bonus to AC against the triggering attack. If the attack misses you, you can @Compendium[pf2e.actionspf2e.UHpkTuCtyaPqiCAB]{Step} after the Strike.

If you're a master in Acrobatics, you can move 10 feet on this Step instead of 5 feet.
