Name: Brutal Critical Nom: Critique brutal PrereqEN: PrereqFR: État: officielle ------ Benefits ---- Benefits: Your critical hits are particularly devastating. Avantage: Vous ajoutez un dé de dégât en cas de coup critique et obtenez des dégâts de saignement. ------ Description (en) ------

Your critical hits are particularly devastating. On a critical hit with a melee Strike, add one extra damage die. This is in addition to any extra dice you gain if the weapon is deadly or fatal. The target also takes @Compendium[pf2e.conditionitems.lDVqvLKA6eF3Df60]{Persistent Bleed Damage} equal to two damage dice.
