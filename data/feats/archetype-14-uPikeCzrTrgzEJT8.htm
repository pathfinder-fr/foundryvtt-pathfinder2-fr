Name: Talismanic Sage Nom: Sage talismanique PrereqEN: Talisman Dabbler Dedication PrereqFR: Dévouement d'Amateur de talismans État: libre ------ Description (en) ------

You have forgotten more about talismans than lesser warriors have ever known. Normally, affixing more than one talisman to an item causes the talismans to be suppressed, but when you @Compendium[pf2e.actionspf2e.UAaQk93a30nx0nYY]{Affix a Talisman}, you can specially treat one item you're working on, allowing it to have two active talismans at once.

This special treatment ends if you use Affix a Talisman to treat a new item for this ability.
