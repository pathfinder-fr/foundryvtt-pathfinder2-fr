Frequency once per round

Requirements You’re holding an unloaded hand crossbow.

You can fire off a single shot even when unprepared. You Interact to reload your hand crossbow and Strike with it.

Special If you have the @Compendium[pf2e.feats-srd.HvPvyeXM2iMK4OYf]{Repeating Hand Crossbow Training} feat, you can use this feat with a @Compendium[pf2e.equipment-srd.zcrIuILnp6oh4dr9]{Repeating Hand Crossbow} to load either a @Compendium[pf2e.equipment-srd.AITVZmakiu3RgfKo]{Bolt} or a @Compendium[pf2e.equipment-srd.9w8MrBhFtvM7RrYN]{Magazine}, but the speed means you can fire only one bolt, then the magazine jams. You must spend a 3-action Interact activity to remove the jammed magazine and clear it before it can be used again.
