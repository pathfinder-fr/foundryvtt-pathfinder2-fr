Name: Martial Artist Dedication Nom: Dévouement d'Artiste martial PrereqEN: PrereqFR: État: libre ------ Description (en) ------

You have trained to use your fists as deadly weapons. The damage die for your fist unarmed attacks becomes [[/r 1d6]] instead of [[/r 1d4]]. You don't take the -2 circumstance penalty for making a lethal attack with your nonlethal unarmed attacks.

Whenever you gain a class feature that grants you expert or greater proficiency in certain weapons, you also gain that proficiency rank in all unarmed attacks.

Special You can't select another dedication feat until you have gained two other feats from the martial artist archetype.
