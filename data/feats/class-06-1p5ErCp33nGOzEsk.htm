Avantage: Vous améliorez votre capacité à Désarmer. ------ Description (en) ------

Requirements You are wielding only a single one-handed melee weapon and have your other hand or hands free.

You adopt a fencing stance that improves your control over your weapon.

While you are in this stance, you gain a +1 circumstance bonus to Athletics checks to @Compendium[pf2e.actionspf2e.Dt6B1slsBy8ipJu9]{Disarm} and a +2 circumstance bonus to your Reflex DC when defending against checks to Disarm you. In addition, you can attempt to Disarm creatures up to two sizes larger than you.
