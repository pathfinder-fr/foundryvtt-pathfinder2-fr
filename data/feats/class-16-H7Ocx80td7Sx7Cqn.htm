Name: Expand Aura Nom: Aura étendue État: libre ------ Description (en) ------

Prerequisites You have at least one aura from a champion feat or feature.

You focus your divine power to extend your influence. Select one aura you have from a champion feat or feature, such as @Compendium[pf2e.feats-srd.TIwk07T0OxSbcOpJ]{Aura of Courage} or @Compendium[pf2e.feats-srd.YawVDUc9uzREIAnO]{Aura of Life}. The radius of the aura is doubled until the start of your next turn.
