If your next action is to cast @Compendium[pf2e.spells-srd.wdA52JJnsuQWeyqz]{Harm} or @Compendium[pf2e.spells-srd.rfZpqmj0AIIdkVIs]{Heal} from your healing or harmful font to restore Hit Points to a single ally, you can channel your own vitality along with the spell.
