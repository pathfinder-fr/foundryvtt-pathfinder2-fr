Name: Martyr Nom: Martyr État: libre ------ Description (en) ------

Prerequisites @Compendium[pf2e.classfeatures.gblTFUOgolqFS9v4]{Divine Font}

You go to extreme lengths to support your allies, even when it means bringing harm to yourself.

If your next action is to cast @Compendium[pf2e.spells-srd.wdA52JJnsuQWeyqz]{Harm} or @Compendium[pf2e.spells-srd.rfZpqmj0AIIdkVIs]{Heal} from your healing or harmful font to restore Hit Points to a single ally, you can channel your own vitality along with the spell. You lose [[/r 1d8]] Hit Points per level of the spell, which can't be reduced or mitigated in any way, and your ally regains an equal number of Hit Points.
