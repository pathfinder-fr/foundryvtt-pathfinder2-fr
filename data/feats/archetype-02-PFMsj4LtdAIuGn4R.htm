Name: Pistol Phenom Dedication Nom: Dévouement : Phénomène du pistolet PrereqEN: |trained in at least one type of one-handed firearm|trained in Deception|trained in Performance PrereqFR: Qualifié en au moins un type d'arme à feu à une main|Qualifié en Duperie|Qualifié en Représentation État: libre ------ Description (en) ------

You catch an opponent off-guard by spinning your weapon. You gain the @Compendium[pf2e.feats-srd.A2EBy2L4acrehiAA]{Pistol Twirl} gunslinger feat, enabling you to Feint against creatures within your weapon's first range increment. This otherwise serves as Pistol Twirl for the purpose of meeting prerequisites, although as normal, it doesn't count as another pistol phenom feat for the purpose of meeting Pistol Phenom Dedication's special entry and taking another archetype. Whenever you Feint with a one-handed firearm, you can choose to attempt a Performance check instead of a Deception check.

Special You can't select another dedication feat until you've gained at least two other feats from the pistol phenom archetype.

Vous prenez un adversaire au dépourvu en faisant tourner votre arme.
