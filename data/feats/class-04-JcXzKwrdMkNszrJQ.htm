Prerequisites good alignment, healing font

You pour invigorating positive energy into a living ally. If the next action you use is to cast @Compendium[pf2e.spells-srd.rfZpqmj0AIIdkVIs]{Heal} to restore Hit Points to a single living creature, the target deals an additional [[/r 1d6 #positive]] positive damage with its melee weapons and unarmed attacks until the end of its next turn.
