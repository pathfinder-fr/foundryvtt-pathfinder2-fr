You gain access to a powerful new school spell depending on your arcane school.

If you're an abjurer, you gain @Compendium[pf2e.spells-srd.LoBjvguamA12iyW0]{Energy Absorption}; if you're a conjurer, you gain @Compendium[pf2e.spells-srd.zjG6NncHyAKqSF7m]{Dimensional Steps}; if you're a diviner, you gain @Compendium[pf2e.spells-srd.4LSf04FFvDgMyDk6]{Vigilant Eye}; if you're an enchanter, you gain @Compendium[pf2e.spells-srd.KPGGkyBFbKse7KpK]{Dread Aura}; if you're an evoker, you gain @Compendium[pf2e.spells-srd.vfHr1N8Rf2bBpdgn]{Elemental Tempest}; if you're an illusionist, you gain @Compendium[pf2e.spells-srd.Nun72GTmb31YqSKh]{Invisibility Cloak}; if you're a necromancer, you gain @Compendium[pf2e.spells-srd.zvvHOQV78WKUB33l]{Life Siphon}; and if you're a transmuter, you gain @Compendium[pf2e.spells-srd.SDkIFrrO1PsE02Kd]{Shifting Form}.

Increase the number of Focus Points in your focus pool by 1.
