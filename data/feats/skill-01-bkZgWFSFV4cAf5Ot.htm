Avantage: Infliger des dégâts à un patient pour gagner un bonus de +2 pour Soigner les blessures ------ Description (en) ------

Your surgery can bring a patient back from the brink of death, but might push them over the edge. When you @Compendium[pf2e.actionspf2e.1kGNdIIhuglAjIp9]{Treat Wounds}, you can deal [[/r {1d8}[slashing]]]{1d8 slashing damage} to your patient just before applying the effects of Treat Wounds.

If you do, you gain a +2 circumstance bonus to your Medicine check to Treat Wounds, and if you roll a success, you get a critical success instead.
