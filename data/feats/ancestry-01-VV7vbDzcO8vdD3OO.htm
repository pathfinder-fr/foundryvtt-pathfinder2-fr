Name: Skull Creeper Nom: PrereqEN: PrereqFR: État: aucune ------ Description (en) ------

You wear skulls to demoralize foes. You become trained in Intimidation and gain the @Compendium[pf2e.feats-srd.xQMz6eDgX75WX2ce]{Intimidating Glare} skill feat. If you’re already trained in Intimidation, you instead become trained in a skill of your choice. If you pay 50 gp for a splendid skull mask, the mask grants you a +1 item bonus to Intimidation; its usage is worn (mask).
