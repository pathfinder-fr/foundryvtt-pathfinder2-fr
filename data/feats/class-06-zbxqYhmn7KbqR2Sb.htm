Your offense exploits your enemy's fear.

Make a melee Strike against a @Compendium[pf2e.conditionitems.TBSHQspnbcqxsmjL]{Frightened} creature. If you hit and deal damage, the target becomes @Compendium[pf2e.conditionitems.AJh5ex99aV6VTggg]{Flat-Footed} until its frightened condition ends.

If the target was already flat-footed to you when you damaged it with this Strike, it can't reduce its frightened value below 1 until the start of your next turn.
