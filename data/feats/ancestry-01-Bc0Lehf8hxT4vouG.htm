Name: Ceremony of the Evened Hand (Claws) Nom: PrereqEN: PrereqFR: État: aucune ------ Description (en) ------

You have cultivated your exoskeleton’s form for close combat, using knots, whorls, and reinforced branches. When you select this feat, you gain a claws unarmed attack that deals 1d4 slashing damage and has the agile and finesse traits. This unarmed attacks is in the brawling weapon group and uses one of your hands.

Special You can select this feat twice, choosing the branch attack the second time you take it.
