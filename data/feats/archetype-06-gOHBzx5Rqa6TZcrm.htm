Name: Mesmerizing Gaze Nom: Regard hypnotisant PrereqEN: Celebrity Dedication PrereqFR: Dévouement de Célébrité État: libre ------ Description (en) ------

When you meet someone's gaze, they are unable to look away.

Choose one target creature. That creature must succeed at a Will save or be @Compendium[pf2e.conditionitems.AdPVz7rbaVSRxHFg]{Fascinated} until the end of your next turn; the DC for this save is equal to your class DC or spell DC, whichever is higher. If the creature succeeds at its save or its fascination ends due to a hostile action, it becomes temporarily immune to your Mesmerizing Gaze for 1 day.

When you select this feat, it gains either the arcane or occult trait; once you make this choice, you can't change it.

Lorsque vous choisissez ce don, il obtient soit le trait arcanique, soit le trait occulte ; une fois que vous avez effectué ce choix, vous ne pouvez plus en changer.
