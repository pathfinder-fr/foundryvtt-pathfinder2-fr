Name: Invulnerable Juggernaut Nom: Juggernaut invulnérable PrereqEN: PrereqFR: État: libre ------ Benefits ---- Benefits: Avantage: Vous obtenez une résistance à tous les dégâts et pouvez mettre un terme à votre rage pour revenir à 1 pv. ------ Description (en) ------

You are unstoppable, able to shrug off mortal wounds with ease. You gain resistance equal to 3 + your Constitution modifier to all damage, and your resistance from raging resistance increases to 8 + your Constitution modifier.

In addition, if you are reduced to 0 Hit Points, you can end your rage as a reaction to stay at 1 Hit Point. If you do, you become @Compendium[pf2e.conditionitems.Yl48xTdMh3aeQYL2]{Wounded 2} (or increase your wounded condition by 2 if you are already wounded).
