You gain your mystery's initial revelation spell (@Compendium[pf2e.spells-srd.AspA30tzKCHFWRf0]{Incendiary Aura}). Casting this spell progresses your @Compendium[pf2e.classfeatures.ibX2EhKkyUtbOHLj]{Oracular Curse}. Use the minor curse from your mystery, but when you progress it again, instead of your mystery's moderate curse effect, you become @Compendium[pf2e.conditionitems.AJh5ex99aV6VTggg]{Flat-Footed} in addition to the effects of the minor curse.

If you don't have one, you gain a focus pool of 1 Focus Point. You can @Compendium[pf2e.actionspf2e.OSefkMgojBLqmRDh]{Refocus} by reconciling the conflicting nature of your mystery, which also reduces your curse to minor.

Minor Curse of Flames

The smoke, heat, and crackling flames of your curse fill your vision and all your other senses.

Creatures further than 30 feet are @Compendium[pf2e.conditionitems.DmAIPqOBomZ7H95W]{Concealed} from you. You can't benefit from effects that would allow you to ignore or mitigate this concealment, as normal for effects of an oracular curse.
