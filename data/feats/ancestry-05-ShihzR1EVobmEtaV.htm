You have been exposed to powerful fey magic. You become trained in primal DCs and spell attack rolls.

  • Gremlin You have long, bat-like ears and gain bane.
  • Monarch You have vestigial, insectile features and gain spider sting. This feat gains the trait appropriate for your ancestry (human for human, goblin for goblin, etc.)
