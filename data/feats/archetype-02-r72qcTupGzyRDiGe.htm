Name: Duelist Dedication Nom: Dévouement : Duelliste PrereqEN: trained in light armor and simple weapons PrereqFR: qualifié en armure légères|qualifié avec les armes simples État: libre ------ Description (en) ------

You are always ready to draw your weapon and begin a duel, no matter the circumstances. You gain the @Compendium[pf2e.feats-srd.qFt6zyWVX1njJf1l]{Quick Draw} ranger feat, enabling you to both draw and attack with a weapon as 1 action. This serves as Quick Draw for the purpose of meeting prerequisites.

Special You cannot select another dedication feat until you have gained two other feats from the duelist archetype.
