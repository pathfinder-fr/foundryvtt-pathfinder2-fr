Make three ranged @Compendium[pf2e.actionspf2e.VjxZFuUXrCU94MWR]{Strikes} against a single target with the required weapon, each using your current multiple attack penalty, and you take an additional –2 penalty. Your @Compendium[pf2e.feats-srd.8bMmGBO6gsMfWZk3]{Stunning Fist} benefit applies to Triangle Shot, even though it isn't a @Compendium[pf2e.actionspf2e.nbfNETdpee8CVM17]{Flurry of Blows}. If all three Strikes hit, the target takes [[/r 3d6 #persistent bleed]] persistent bleed damage.
