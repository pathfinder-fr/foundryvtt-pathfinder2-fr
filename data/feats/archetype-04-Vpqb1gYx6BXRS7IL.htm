Name: First Revelation (Cosmos Mystery) Nom: Première révélation (Mystère du cosmos) PrereqEN: |Oracle Dedication PrereqFR: Dévouement d'oracle|Mystère du cosmos État: libre ------ Description (en) ------

You gain your mystery's initial revelation spell (@Compendium[pf2e.spells-srd.mlNYROcFrUF8nFgk]{Spray of Stars}). Casting this spell progresses your @Compendium[pf2e.classfeatures.ibX2EhKkyUtbOHLj]{Oracular Curse}. Use the minor curse from your mystery, but when you progress it again, instead of your mystery's moderate curse effect, you become @Compendium[pf2e.conditionitems.AJh5ex99aV6VTggg]{Flat-Footed} in addition to the effects of the minor curse.

If you don't have one, you gain a focus pool of 1 Focus Point. You can @Compendium[pf2e.actionspf2e.OSefkMgojBLqmRDh]{Refocus} by reconciling the conflicting nature of your mystery, which also reduces your curse to minor.

Minor Curse of Cosmos

Your unnatural lightness makes it hard to keep your footing and interact with other physical objects. You are @Compendium[pf2e.conditionitems.MIRkyAjyBeXivMa7]{Enfeebled 1} and take a -2 penalty to saves and DCs against @Compendium[pf2e.actionspf2e.PMbdMWc2QroouFGD]{Grapple}, @Compendium[pf2e.actionspf2e.7blmbDrQFNfdT731]{Shove}, and other forms of forced movement.
