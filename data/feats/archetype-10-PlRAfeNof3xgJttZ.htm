Name: Surrounding Flames Nom: Flammes environnantes PrereqEN: |Spell Trickster Dedication|ability to cast wall of fire PrereqFR: Dévouement de mystificateur de sort|capacité à lancer Mur de feu État: libre ------ Description (en) ------

When you cast wall of fire, you can modify its standard effects as follows: Instead of a 5-foot-thick, 10-foot-radius ring of flame, you can form the wall into a 10-foot-radius hemisphere of fire.
