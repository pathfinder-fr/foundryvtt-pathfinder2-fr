In combat, you favor the brutal weapons that are traditional for your orc ancestors. You are trained with the @Compendium[pf2e.equipment-srd.XGtIUZ4ZNKuFx1uL]{Falchion} and @Compendium[pf2e.equipment-srd.8COlYvHe6hKCXY8x]{Greataxe}.

In addition, you gain access to all uncommon orc weapons. For the purpose of determining your proficiency, martial orc weapons are simple weapons and advanced orc weapons are martial weapons.
