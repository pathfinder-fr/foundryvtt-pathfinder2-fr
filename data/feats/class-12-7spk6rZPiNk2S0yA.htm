Requirements An enemy you've damaged with a ranged weapon during the last minute is @Compendium[pf2e.conditionitems.DmAIPqOBomZ7H95W]{Concealed} or @Compendium[pf2e.conditionitems.iU0fEDdBp3rXpTMC]{Hidden} from you.

Once you've locked on to a target, little can obscure your aim. Make a ranged weapon Strike against the required target. This Strike ignores the target's @Compendium[pf2e.conditionitems.DmAIPqOBomZ7H95W]{Concealed} condition and reduces the flat check for the @Compendium[pf2e.conditionitems.iU0fEDdBp3rXpTMC]{Hidden} condition from 11 to 5.
