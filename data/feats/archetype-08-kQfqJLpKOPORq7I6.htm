Name: Work Yourself Up Nom: Faites-vous plaisir PrereqEN: |Cathartic Mage Dedication PrereqFR: Dévouement de mage cathartique État: libre ------ Description (en) ------

Requirements You have a reaction available and aren't prevented from using @Compendium[pf2e.actionspf2e.2424g94rpoiN1IPh]{Catharsis}.

You concentrate on your mental state, tapping into that raw emotional energy. Take your Catharsis reaction, causing the catharsis activation and entering your emotional fervor as normal. The fervor lasts only until the end of your turn. At the end of your emotional fervor, you experience the effects of your emotional fallout, as normal.

------ Description (fr) ------

Conditions Vous avez une réaction disponible et n'êtes pas empêché d'utiliser @Compendium[pf2e.actionspf2e.2424g94rpoiN1IPh]{Catharsis}.
