Name: Scout Dedication Nom: Dévouement : Éclaireur PrereqEN: trained in Stealth and Survival PrereqFR: qualifié en Discrétion|qualifié en Survie État: libre ------ Description (en) ------

You are a highly skilled scout, capable of providing your allies a timely warning of any danger. You gain the @Compendium[pf2e.feats-srd.Tpcq3Lk7qEOZ3LDP]{Scout's Warning} ranger feat.

When you're using the @Compendium[pf2e.actionspf2e.kV3XM0YJeS2KCSOb]{Scout} exploration activity, you grant your allies a +2 circumstance bonus to their initiative rolls instead of a +1 circumstance bonus.

Special You can't select another dedication feat until you have gained two other feats from the scout archetype.
