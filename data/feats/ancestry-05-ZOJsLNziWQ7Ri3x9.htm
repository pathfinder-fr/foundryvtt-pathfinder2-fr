Name: Undead Slayer Nom: Tueur de morts-vivants PrereqEN: PrereqFR: État: libre ------ Description (en) ------

Your knowledge of your own not-quite-living anatomy, combined with your experience in combat, helps you take down undead foes with ease. Against undead creatures, you gain a circumstance bonus to damage with weapons and unarmed attacks equal to the number of weapon damage dice for the @Compendium[pf2e.actionspf2e.VjxZFuUXrCU94MWR]{Strike}. This bonus increases to double the number of weapon damage dice if your target has the vampire trait.
