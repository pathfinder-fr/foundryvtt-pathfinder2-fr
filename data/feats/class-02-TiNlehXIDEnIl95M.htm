You draw the life force out of your enemies to heal your own wounds. When you cast a @Compendium[pf2e.spells-srd.wdA52JJnsuQWeyqz]{Harm} spell and damage at least one living creature, you regain Hit Points equal to the spell level of your harm spell. If you aren't a living creature, you gain no benefit from this feat.
