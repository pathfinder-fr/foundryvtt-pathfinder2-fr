Name: Ranged Combatant (Bludgeoning) Nom: Combattant à distance (contondant) PrereqEN: PrereqFR: État: libre ------ Description (en) ------

Spines, flame jets, and holy blasts are just some of the ways your eidolon might strike from a distance. It gains a ranged unarmed attack with a range increment of 30 feet that deals 1d4 bludgeoning damage and has the magical and propulsive traits.
