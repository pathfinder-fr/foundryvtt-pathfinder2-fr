Name: Scorching Disarm Nom: PrereqEN: PrereqFR: État: aucune ------ Description (en) ------

Frequency once per day

You make a weapon unbearable to hold. Attempt an Athletics check to Disarm a target creature. You don’t need to have a hand free to make this Disarm attempt. If the Disarm attempt succeeds, the target takes [[/r 4d6 #fire]] fire damage unless it drops the weapon, and on a critical success, it takes [[/r 4d6 #fire]] fire damage and drops the weapon. If you’re legendary in Athletics, this increases to [[/r 8d6 #fire]] fire damage.
