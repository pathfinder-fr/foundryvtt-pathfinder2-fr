Avantage: Vous utilisez les sens de votre familier aussi facilement que les vôtres. ------ Description (en) ------

You use your familiar's senses as easily as your own.

You project your senses into your familiar. When you do, you lose all sensory information from your own body, but you can sense through your familiar's body until you @Compendium[pf2e.actionspf2e.AjLSHZSWQ90exdLo]{Dismiss} the effect.

While projecting your senses in this way, you can converse telepathically with your familiar if it understands a language.

Special This feat has the trait corresponding to the tradition of spells you cast (arcane, divine, occult, or primal).
