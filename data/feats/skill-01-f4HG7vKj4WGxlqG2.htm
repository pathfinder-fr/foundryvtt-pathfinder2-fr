Name: Battle Medicine (Forensic Medicine) Nom: Médecine militaire (Médecine forensique) État: libre ------ Description (en) ------

Prerequisites trained in Medicine, @Compendium[pf2e.classfeatures.O3IX7rTxXWWvDVM3]{Forensic Medicine Methodology}

You can patch up yourself or an adjacent ally, even in combat. Attempt a Medicine check with the same DC as for Treat Wounds, and restore a number of Hit Points equal to your level; this does not remove the @Compendium[pf2e.conditionspf2e.Yl48xTdMh3aeQYL2]{Wounded} condition.  The target is then temporarily immune to your Battle Medicine for 1 hour.
