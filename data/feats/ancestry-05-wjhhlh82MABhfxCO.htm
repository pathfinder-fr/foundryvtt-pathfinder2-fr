Avantage: Vous infligez plus de dégâts à un ennemi qui vous porte un coup critique ou à un allié Strix. ------ Description (en) ------

Frequency once per 10 minutes

Trigger You, or a strix ally you can see, are damaged by an enemy's critical hit.

You dedicate yourself to destroying those who harm your kin. Until the end of your next turn, you deal an additional +1d6 damage on Strikes against the triggering enemy. The bonus increases to +2d6 if you use a @Compendium[pf2e.equipment-srd.DxCuJKynlnMQZHgp]{Striking} weapon or unarmed attack and +3d6 if you use a @Compendium[pf2e.equipment-srd.woxl2FrrgAcJDu0t]{Striking (Major)} weapon or unarmed attack.
