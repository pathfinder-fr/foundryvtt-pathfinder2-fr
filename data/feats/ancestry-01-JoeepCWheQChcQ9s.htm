Your elemental lineage manifests as magma and molten rock, and you can focus the magma into your hand to attack your foes. You gain a magma spike unarmed attack, which requires a free hand to use. This is an agile, finesse, unarmed attack in the brawling weapon group that deals [[/r {1d4}[piercing]]]{1d4 piercing damage}, as well as [[/r {1}[fire]]]{1 additional fire damage}. Instead of the normal critical specialization effect, the target takes [[/r {1d6}[persistent,fire]]] @Compendium[pf2e.conditionitems.lDVqvLKA6eF3Df60]{Persistent Fire Damage}; you gain a bonus on this persistent damage equal to your item bonus to attack rolls on unarmed attacks.
