When you @Compendium[pf2e.actionspf2e.rmwa3OyhTZ2i2AHl]{Craft} an alchemical item that restores Hit Points (rather than create it with advanced alchemy, Quick Alchemy, or another means), it becomes a blessed alchemical item and gains the positive trait.
