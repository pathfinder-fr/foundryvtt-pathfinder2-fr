Frequency once every 10 minutes

Your alchemical mastery can resuscitate the recently slain. You can administer a @Compendium[pf2e.equipment-srd.qK964kZz1ALcysOa]{True Elixir of Life} to a creature who has been dead for no more than 2 rounds. When you do, that creature is immediately returned to life with 1 Hit Point and becomes wounded 1.
