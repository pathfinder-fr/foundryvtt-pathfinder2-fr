Name: Enchanting Arrow Nom: Flèche enchantée État: libre ------ Description (en) ------

With a single whisper carried on the wind, you enchant your ammunition to make a foe more vulnerable to your attacks.

Make a bow @Compendium[pf2e.actionspf2e.VjxZFuUXrCU94MWR]{Strike}. On a hit, the target takes an additional [[/r 2d6 #mental]] mental damage. On a critical hit, the target also becomes @Compendium[pf2e.conditionitems.dfCMdR4wnpbYNTix]{Stunned} 1. The mental damage increases to 3d6 if your bow has a greater striking rune, or to 4d6 if your bow has a major striking rune.
