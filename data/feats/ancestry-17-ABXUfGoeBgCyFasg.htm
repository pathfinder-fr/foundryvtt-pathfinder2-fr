Name: Great Tengu Form Nom: Forme du grand tengu État: libre ------ Description (en) ------

Prerequisites @Compendium[pf2e.feats-srd.WPz97m5FNlbLIQ6p]{Long-Nosed Form}

You take on the imposing form of a large, winged oni.

Once per day, as part of using Long-Nosed Form, you also gain the benefits of 4th-level @Compendium[pf2e.spells-srd.wzctak6BxOW8xvFV]{Enlarge}and @Compendium[pf2e.spells-srd.A2JfEKe6BZcTG1S8]{Fly}.

This lasts for 5 minutes or until you shift out of your Long-Nosed Form, whichever happens first.
