When an enemy's movement is compromised, you deliver a more deadly blow.

Make a Strike against a creature that is @Compendium[pf2e.conditionitems.kWc1fhmv9LBiTuei]{Grabbed}, @Compendium[pf2e.conditionitems.j91X7x0XSomq8d60]{Prone}, or @Compendium[pf2e.conditionitems.VcDeM8A5oI6VqhbM]{Restrained}. You gain a circumstance bonus to damage on this Strike equal to the number of weapon damage dice, or that number + 2 if you're wielding the weapon in two hands. The Strike gains the following failure effect.

Failure You deal damage to the target equal to the number of weapon damage dice, or that number + 2 if you're wielding the weapon in two hands. This damage has the same damage type as the weapon.
