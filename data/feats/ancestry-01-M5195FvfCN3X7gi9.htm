Name: Tusks (Orc) Nom: État: aucune ------ Description (en) ------

You have particularly long, jagged tusks perfect for tearing meat from bone. You gain a tusks unarmed attack that deals [[/r 1d6 #piercing]] piercing damage. Your tusks are in the brawling group and have the finesse and unarmed traits.

Special You can take this feat only at 1st level, and you can’t retrain into this feat. You can retrain out of this feat only through drastic measures such as breaking or filing your tusks.
