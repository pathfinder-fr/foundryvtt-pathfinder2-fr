You can cast @Compendium[pf2e.spells-srd.qsNeG9KZpODSACMq]{Foresight} once per day as an innate spell, either arcane if you have Wizard Dedication or primal if you have Druid Dedication, but the duration is 10 minutes instead of 1 hour.
