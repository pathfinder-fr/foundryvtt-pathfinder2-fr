Fire fascinates you. Your spells and alchemical items that deal fire damage gain a status bonus to damage equal to half the spell's level or one-quarter the item's level (minimum 1). You also gain a +1 status bonus to any @Compendium[pf2e.conditionitems.lDVqvLKA6eF3Df60]{Persistent Fire Damage} you deal.
