Name: Elite Dracomancer Nom: Dracomancien d'élite PrereqEN: Dracomancer PrereqFR: Dracomancien État: libre ------ Description (en) ------

Your magic rivals that of lesser dragons. Choose one 3rd-level spell and one 4th-level spell from those listed for a dragon spellcaster of your draconic exemplar's type, as on pages 104-126 of the Bestiary (such as @Compendium[pf2e.spells-srd.DCQHaLrYXMI37dvW]{Paralyze} and @Compendium[pf2e.spells-srd.GKpEcy9WId6NJvtx]{Stinking Cloud} for a black dragon exemplar).

You can cast each of these spells once per day as arcane innate spells.

------ Description (fr) ------

Votre magie rivalise avec celui des dragons inférieurs.
