You've mastered combat practices that let you get up close and bring down perpetrators alive.

You can use your Intelligence modifier on attack rolls when you @Compendium[pf2e.actionspf2e.m0f2B7G9eaaTmhFL]{Devise a Strategem} if you're using a weapon of the club group in one hand (like a club, staff, or sap), in addition to the usual weapons, and those Strikes qualify for your @Compendium[pf2e.classfeatures.2Fe4YZCvAr9Yf6w7]{Strategic Strike}.

Additionally, you can make any of your Strikes nonlethal without taking the normal -2 penalty.
