You've sworn to slay evil dragons. Add the following tenet to your code after the others: "You must slay evil dragons you encounter as long as you have a reasonable chance of success.

If you use @Compendium[pf2e.actionspf2e.IX1VlVCL5sFTptEE]{Liberating Step} triggered by an evil dragon, your ally gains a +4 circumstance bonus to checks granted by your Liberating Step, and the ally can Step twice afterward.
