Name: Fear No Law, Fear No One Nom: Peur d'aucune loi, ni de personne PrereqEN: Order of the Scourge PrereqFR: Ordre du Fléau État: auto-trad ------ Description (en) ------

When you roll a success on a save against a fear effect, you get a critical success instead. In addition, any time you gain the @Compendium[pf2e.conditionitems.TBSHQspnbcqxsmjL]{Frightened} condition, reduce the condition’s value by 1. As usual, this ability reduces the initial frightened condition by 1, so if you have multiple similar abilities, they don’t both take effect.
