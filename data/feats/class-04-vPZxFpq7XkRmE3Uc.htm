You @Compendium[pf2e.actionspf2e.d5I6018Mci2SWokk]{Leap} and discharge your firearm to add a +10-foot status bonus to the distance traveled. If you spend 2 actions for Black Powder Boost, you @Compendium[pf2e.actionspf2e.2HJ4yuEFY1Cast4h]{High Jump} or @Compendium[pf2e.actionspf2e.JUvAvruz7yRQXfz2]{Long Jump} instead.
