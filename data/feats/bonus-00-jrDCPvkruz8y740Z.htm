Name: Locate Lawbreakers Nom: Localisation des hors-la-loi PrereqEN: Order of the Gate PrereqFR: État: changé État d'origine: officielle ------ Description (en) ------

Your pursuit is relentless, and you are able to magically locate stolen objects and outlaws. You gain @Compendium[pf2e.spells-srd.LQzlKbYjZSMFQawP]{Locate} as an innate spell of a tradition of your choice, which you can cast once per day. If you are a member of the Order of the Gate, when you reach 14th level, the spell is heightened to 5th level.
