You've trained with the weapons of the Bright Lions. You gain access to @Compendium[pf2e.equipment-srd.Ak3oHoPDJoMR14yg]{Lion Scythes} and @Compendium[pf2e.equipment-srd.zWp7Kcn7mhKyGFxW]{Sun Slings}.
