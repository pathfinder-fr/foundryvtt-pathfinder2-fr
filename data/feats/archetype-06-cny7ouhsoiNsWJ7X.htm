If you succeed, you can Stride up to half your Speed (or up to your full Speed if you critically succeed) without triggering reactions from the target due to your movement, and the target is @Compendium[pf2e.conditionitems.AJh5ex99aV6VTggg]{Flat-Footed} against the next melee attack you attempt against it before the end of your turn.
