You can channel your core's power through the magical gem that serves as your eye. You gain an energy beam ranged unarmed attack that deals [[/r {1d4}[fire]]]{1d4 fire damage}. Your eye beam does not add critical specialization effects.

Enhancement You channel greater power. Increase the damage die of your energy beam by one step, from 1d4 to 1d6.
