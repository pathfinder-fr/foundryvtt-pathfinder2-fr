Name: Warding Light Nom: Lumière protectrice PrereqEN: Bright Lion Dedication PrereqFR: Dévouement de Lion radieux État: libre ------ Description (en) ------

You gain a tattoo that serves as a special ward to defend you against Walkena's forces.

The tattoo allows you to cast the @Compendium[pf2e.spells-srd.WBmvzNDfpwka3qT4]{Light} cantrip as a divine innate spell at will. The cantrip is heightened to a spell level equal to half your level rounded up. If you die, the tattoo immediately casts @Compendium[pf2e.spells-srd.xRgU9rrhmGAgG4Rc]{Gentle Repose} on your corpse, heightened to the same spell level.
