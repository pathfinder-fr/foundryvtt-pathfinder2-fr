Avantage: Vous profitez de la distraction fournie par un allié. ------ Description (en) ------

You capitalize on the distraction provided by an ally. You @Compendium[pf2e.actionspf2e.VMozDqMMuK5kpoX4]{Sneak} up to a foe adjacent to one of your allies. You can roll against the foe you're Sneaking up on, even if it's currently observing you, as though you were hidden. You take a -2 penalty on your Stealth check.

If your Stealth check against the chosen foe succeeds, you can make a melee @Compendium[pf2e.actionspf2e.VjxZFuUXrCU94MWR]{Strike} against that foe at the end of your Sneak.
