Trigger An opponent adjacent to you critically hits you with a melee weapon or melee unarmed attack.

Requirements You have at least 1 available Focus Point

You lash out, directing a burst of storming fury toward a creature that has harmed you.

You cast @Compendium[pf2e.spells-srd.ho1jSoYKrHUNnM90]{Tempest Surge} on the triggering opponent and push that creature, moving it 5 feet away from you if it fails its Reflex save, or 10 feet if it critically fails. This movement is forced movement.

Conditions Votre réserve contient au moins 1 point de focalisation
