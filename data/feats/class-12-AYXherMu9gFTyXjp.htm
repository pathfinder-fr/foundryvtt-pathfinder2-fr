Your poisons are devastatingly potent. When you apply a simple poison with Poison Weapon, the poison deals [[/r 4d4 #poison]] poison damage instead of [[/r 1d4 #poison]] poison damage. When you apply an injury poison to your weapon, the DC to resist that poison increases by 2, to a maximum of your class DC.
