When you successfully @Compendium[pf2e.feats-srd.deoHKUzpzT7iwWhL]{Counterspell} a spell of the tradition that matches your bloodline, you consume it, replenishing yourself with its energy.

When you do, you are nourished as if you had eaten a meal and regain Hit Points equal to twice the level of the counteracted spell.
