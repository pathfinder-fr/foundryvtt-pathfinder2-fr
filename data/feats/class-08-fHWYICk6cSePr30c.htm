Name: Surreptitious Spellcaster Nom: État: aucune ------ Description (en) ------

Prerequisites Spellmaster Dedication

You’ve learned that it pays for a Pathfinder to be able to cast spells without being noticed. You gain the @Compendium[pf2e.feats-srd.sIeuPW0j39fTZm08]{Conceal Spell} wizard class feat, though it’s not a wizard feat for you. You also gain a +2 circumstance bonus to Stealth checks to Conceal a Spell and to Deception checks to conceal verbal components.
