You tumble around your foes, expertly avoiding their reactions. Stride up to half your Speed and roll an Acrobatics check. Compare the result to the Reflex DC of each foe whose reach you began in or enter during the movement, in sequence.

Critical Success This movement doesn't trigger reactions from the foe, the foe is @Compendium[pf2e.conditionitems.AJh5ex99aV6VTggg]{Flat-Footed} to you until the end of your turn,

and you gain Panache.

Success This movement doesn't trigger reactions from the foe, and you gain panache.

Critical Failure Your movement immediately stops when you enter the creature's reach; if you began in the creature's

reach, you don't move.
