You are one of the raiders eagerly planning to launch an assault by sea on the Whispering Tyrant's Isle of Terror. You gain the @Compendium[pf2e.feats-srd.qQt3CMrhLkUV1wCv]{Sudden Charge} fighter feat, though it's a viking feat for you instead. If you already had Sudden Charge, you can retrain it as part of gaining this feat. When you use Sudden Charge to Strike a target in shallow water, the target is @Compendium[pf2e.conditionitems.AJh5ex99aV6VTggg]{Flat-Footed} against your Strike, as you use the water kicked up by your passage to camouflage your angle of attack.
