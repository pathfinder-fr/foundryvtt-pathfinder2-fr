Name: Kobold Weapon Familiarity Nom: Familiarité avec les armes kobold PrereqEN: PrereqFR: État: libre ------ Description (en) ------

You’ve trained with weapons ideal for subterranean efficiency. You are trained with the crossbow, greatpick, light pick, pick, and spear. You also gain access to all uncommon kobold weapons. For the purpose of determining your proficiency, martial kobold weapons are simple weapons, and advanced kobold weapons are martial weapons.
