Disobeying your @Compendium[pf2e.actionspf2e.M8RCbthRhB4bxO9t]{Iron Command} has lasting consequences. If an enemy refuses to kneel to you, you can deal @Compendium[pf2e.conditionitems.lDVqvLKA6eF3Df60]{Persistent Mental Damage} instead of normal mental damage. You must decide whether the mental damage will be persistent before your enemy chooses whether to kneel or not. The amount of damage is unchanged.
