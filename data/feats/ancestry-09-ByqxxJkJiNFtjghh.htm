Name: Mask Of Pain Nom: Masque de douleur PrereqEN: expert in Intimidation|Orc Warmask PrereqFR: État: changé État d'origine: libre ------ Description (en) ------

Trigger You succeed at a Demoralize check.

Requirements You’re wearing your warmask.Your mask has grown into a manifestation of your pain, which you can release. The creature you successfully Demoralized takes [[/r 2d6 #mental]] mental damage, or [[/r 4d6 #mental]] mental damage if you’re legendary in Intimidation. The target is then temporarily immune to additional damage from Mask of Pain for 24 hours.
