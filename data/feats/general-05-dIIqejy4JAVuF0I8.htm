You have been exposed to powerful fey magic. You become trained in primal DCs and spell attack rolls. You gain the fey trait and one of the following features which grant an innate primal spell that can be used once per day.

  • Anteater You can launch your tongue forward as a deadly attack, gaining @Compendium[pf2e.spells-srd.k34hDOfIIMAxNL4a]{Grim Tendrils}.
  • Dryad Your body is covered in elegant vines, granting you @Compendium[pf2e.spells-srd.jSRAyd57kd4WZ4yE]{Summon Plant or Fungus}
  • Gremlin You have long, bat-like ears and gain @Compendium[pf2e.spells-srd.7ZinJNzxq0XF0oMx]{Bane}
  • Monarch You have vestigial, insectile features and gain @Compendium[pf2e.spells-srd.DYdvMZ8G2LiSLVWw]{Spider Sting}.

This feat gains the trait appropriate for your ancestry (human for human, goblin for goblin, etc.)
