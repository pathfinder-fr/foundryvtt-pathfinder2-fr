Avantage: Lorsque vous trompez un ennemi, vous pouvez l'inciter à dépasser sa prochaine attaque. ------ Description (en) ------

When you trick a foe, you can goad them into overextending their next attack.

On a @Compendium[pf2e.actionspf2e.QNAVeNKtHA0EUw4X]{Feint}, you can use the following success and critical success effects instead of any other effects you would gain when you Feint; if you do, other abilities that adjust the normal effects of your Feint no longer apply. You can choose whether to use the Goading Feint benefits or the normal benefits each time you Feint a given foe.

Critical Success The target takes a -2 circumstance penalty to

all attack rolls against you before the end of its next turn.

Success The target takes a -2 circumstance penalty to its next

attack roll against you before the end of its next turn.
