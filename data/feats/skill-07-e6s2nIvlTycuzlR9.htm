You can @Compendium[pf2e.actionspf2e.ewwCglB7XOPLUz72]{Lie} about having taken an action so convincingly that you acquire all the tangible benefits of having successfully completed it.

You can roll Deception in place of a different skill when attempting to @Compendium[pf2e.actionspf2e.QyzlsLrqM0EEwd7j]{Earn Income}, @Compendium[pf2e.actionspf2e.OX4fy22hQgUHDr0q]{Make an Impression}, @Compendium[pf2e.actionspf2e.DCb62iCBrJXy0Ik6]{Request}, or @Compendium[pf2e.actionspf2e.49y9Ec4bDii8pcD3]{Subsist}.
