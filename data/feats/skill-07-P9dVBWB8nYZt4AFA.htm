Name: Sanctify Water Nom: Sanctifier l'eau PrereqEN: master in Religion|good or evil deity PrereqFR: maitre en Religion, divinité bonne ou mauvaise État: libre ------ Benefits and spoiler ---- Benefits: You imbue water with your deity’s blessing Avantage: Vous bénissez de l'eau qui devient temporairement bénite ou maudite. ------ Description (en) ------

You imbue water with your deity's blessing. Choose a container of water on your person that is of light Bulk. For 1 round, if you or an ally throw this container at a foe, it has the damaging effects of @Compendium[pf2e.equipment-srd.efFe4EK7ThUrH446]{Holy Water} (if you follow a good deity) or @Compendium[pf2e.equipment-srd.uplCUQwMwBOBHz0E]{Unholy Water} (if you follow an evil deity). The water's transient power doesn't make it gain any value, allow it to be used to supply the cost for a spell or ritual, and so on. If you have legendary proficiency in Religion, you can sanctify three containers held by you or allies within your reach instead of one.
