Name: Virtuosic Performer (Singing) Nom: Artiste virtuose (Chant) PrereqEN: trained in Performance Avantage: PrereqFR: qualifié en Représentation État: officielle ------ Description (en) ------

You have exceptional talent with one type of performance. You gain a +1 circumstance bonus when singing. If you are a master in Performance, this bonus increases to +2.

Example performances Ballad, chant, melody, rhyming

If it's unclear whether the specialty applies, the GM decides.
