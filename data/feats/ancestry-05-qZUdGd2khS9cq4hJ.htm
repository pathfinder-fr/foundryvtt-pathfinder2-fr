Name: Shifting Faces Nom: Visages changeants PrereqEN: tailless alternate form PrereqFR: forme alternative sans queue État: changé État d'origine: libre ------ Description (en) ------

Frequency once per day

You wear many faces, even those that don’t belong to you. When you Change Shape into your tailless form, you gain the effects of 3rd-level @Compendium[pf2e.spells-srd.i35dpZFI7jZcRoBo]{Illusory Disguise} for 1 hour or until you shift back, except it’s a transmutation effect rather than an illusion.
