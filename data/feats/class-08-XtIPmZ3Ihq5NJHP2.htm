Unsuspecting targets are especially vulnerable to your poisons. When you successfully Strike a flat-footed creature with a poisoned weapon or expose a @Compendium[pf2e.conditionitems.AJh5ex99aV6VTggg]{Flat-Footed} creature to an inhaled poison, the flat-footed condition also gives that creature a -2 circumstance penalty to its initial save against that poison.
