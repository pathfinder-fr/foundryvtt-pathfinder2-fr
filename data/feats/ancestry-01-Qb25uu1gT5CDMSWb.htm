Name: Hag Claws Nom: État: aucune ------ Description (en) ------

When you came of age, your fingernails grew long and sharp. You gain a claw unarmed attack that deals [[/r 1d4 #slashing]] slashing damage. Your claws are in the brawling group and have the agile, finesse, and unarmed traits.

Special Hag claws can develop at any point in a changeling’s life, allowing you to select this feat anytime you would gain an ancestry feat, but once you’ve selected it, you can’t retrain out of it.
