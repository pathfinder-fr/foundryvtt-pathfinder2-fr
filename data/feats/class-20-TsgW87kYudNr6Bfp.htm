Avantage: Une fois par jour, vous pouvez lancer un sort arcanique courant de votre choix de niveau 8 ou inférieur. ------ Description (en) ------

You have learned Artokus Kirran's most hidden secrets, granting you the ability to create alchemy infused with the power of wishes.

Once per day when you use @Compendium[pf2e.classfeatures.Pe0zmIqyTBc2Td0I]{Advanced Alchemy} during your daily preparations, you can spend a batch of infused reagents to create a wish vial containing a single common arcane spell of your choice of 8th level or lower.

The spell must have a casting time of no more than 3 actions, no Cost, and must be able to target you. Only you can Activate the wish vial, which takes the same number of @Compendium[pf2e.actionspf2e.pvQ5rY2zrtPI614F]{Interact} actions as the spell's casting time and grants you the effects of the spell.
