Name: Virtuosic Performer (Oratory) Nom: Artiste virtuose (Art oratoire) PrereqEN: trained in Performance Avantage: +1 avec un certain type de représentation PrereqFR: qualifié en Représentation État: officielle ------ Description (en) ------

You have exceptional talent with one type of performance. You gain a +1 circumstance bonus when orating. If you are a master in Performance, this bonus increases to +2.

Example performances Epic, ode, poetry, storytelling

If it's unclear whether the specialty applies, the GM decides.
