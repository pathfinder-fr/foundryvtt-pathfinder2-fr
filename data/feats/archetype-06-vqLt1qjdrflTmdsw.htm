Name: Sun's Fury Nom: Furie du soleil PrereqEN: |Bright Lion Dedication PrereqFR: Dévouement de Lion radieux État: libre ------ Description (en) ------

The Old Sun Gods have granted you a gift in your fight against Walkena. You gain the @Compendium[pf2e.spells-srd.LrFUj76CHDBV0vHW]{Sun's Fury} focus spell. If you don't already have one, you gain a focus pool of 1 Focus Point, which you can regain using the Refocus activity to pray to the Old Sun Gods; if you already have a focus pool, increase the number of Focus Points in your focus pool by 1. Your Bright Lion focus spells are divine spells; when you gain this feat, if you aren't already, you become trained in divine spell attacks and spell DCs, using Charisma as your spellcasting ability score.

Les anciens dieux du soleil vous ont accordé un don pour combattre contre Walkena.

Vous obtenez le sort de focalisation @Compendium[pf2e.spells-srd.LrFUj76CHDBV0vHW]{Furie du soleil}.
