Name: Environmental Grace Nom: Grâce environnementale PrereqEN: master in Survival|deity who grants the cold, fire, nature, or travel domain PrereqFR: maître en Survie|divinité qui accorde le domaine froid, feu, nature ou voyage État: libre ------ Benefits and spoiler ---- Benefits: You rapidly adapt to environments through a combination of skill, meditation, and divine grace Avantage: Vous vous adaptez rapidement aux environnements froids et chauds. ------ Description (en) ------

You rapidly adapt to environments through a combination of skill, meditation, and divine grace. After spending an hour in an environment of severe cold or colder, you are protected from the effects of severe cold until you leave that environment. After spending an hour in an environment of severe heat or hotter, you are protected from the effects of severe heat until you leave that environment.
