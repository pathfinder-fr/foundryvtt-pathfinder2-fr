Your mercy transcends the bounds of life and death.

When you use @Compendium[pf2e.feats-srd.zilwynzk8lIujgZo]{Mercy}, you can cast @Compendium[pf2e.spells-srd.zNN9212H2FGfM7VS]{Lay on Hands} on a creature that died since your last turn to return it to life.
