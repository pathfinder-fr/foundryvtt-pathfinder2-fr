Your mercy transcends the bounds of life and death.

When you use @Compendium[pf2e.feats-srd.zilwynzk8lIujgZo]{Mercy}, you can cast @Compendium[pf2e.spells-srd.zNN9212H2FGfM7VS]{Lay on Hands} on a creature that died since your last turn to return it to life. The target returns to life with 1 hit point and becomes wounded 1. You can't use Ultimate Mercy if the triggering effect was disintegrate or a death effect.
