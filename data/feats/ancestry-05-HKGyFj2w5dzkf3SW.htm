By drawing upon the First World's magic, you can siphon a portion of that malleable world to create a convincing illusion. Once per day, you can cast @Compendium[pf2e.spells-srd.i35dpZFI7jZcRoBo]{Illusory Disguise}, @Compendium[pf2e.spells-srd.4ZGte0i9YbLh4dRi]{Item Facade}, or @Compendium[pf2e.spells-srd.yV7Ouzaoe7DHLESI]{Ventriloquism}. At 7th level, the spell is heightened to 2nd level, and every 2 levels thereafter, the spell is heightened an additional spell level.
