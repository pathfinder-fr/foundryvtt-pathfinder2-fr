AccessVarisian ethnicity or New Thassilon nationality

You have tattoos on your body corresponding to one of the ancient Thassilonian schools of magic. Choose one of the following schools of magic: abjuration (@Compendium[pf2e.spells-srd.TVKNbcgTee19PXZR]{Shield}), conjuration (@Compendium[pf2e.spells-srd.uZK2BYzPnxUBnDjr]{Tanglefoot}), enchantment (@Compendium[pf2e.spells-srd.4gBIw4IDrSfFHik4]{Daze}), evocation (@Compendium[pf2e.spells-srd.kBhaPuzLUSwS6vVf]{Electric Arc}), illusion (@Compendium[pf2e.spells-srd.atlgGNI1E1Ox3O3a]{Ghost Sound}), necromancy (@Compendium[pf2e.spells-srd.mAMEt4FFbdqoRnkN]{Chill Touch}), or transmutation (@Compendium[pf2e.spells-srd.AUctDF2fqPZN2w4W]{Sigil}). You can cast the associated cantrip (listed in parentheses) as an innate arcane spell at will.
