If the next action you use is to cast @Compendium[pf2e.spells-srd.wdA52JJnsuQWeyqz]{Harm} or @Compendium[pf2e.spells-srd.rfZpqmj0AIIdkVIs]{Heal} to damage one creature, the target is knocked prone if it takes any damage from the spell.
