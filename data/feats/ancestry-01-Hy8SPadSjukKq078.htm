You gain the @Compendium[pf2e.feats-srd.xQMz6eDgX75WX2ce]{Intimidating Glare} skill feat, as well as a +2 circumstance bonus on Deception checks to @Compendium[pf2e.actionspf2e.AJstokjdG6iDjVjE]{Impersonate} when pretending to be a version of yourself who is a tiefling.
