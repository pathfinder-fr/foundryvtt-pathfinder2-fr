Name: Virtuosic Performer (Comedy) Nom: Artiste virtuose (Comique) PrereqEN: trained in Performance PrereqFR: qualifié en Représentations État: officielle ------ Benefits and spoiler ---- Benefits: +1 with a certain type of performance. Avantage: +1 avec un certain type de représentation ------ Description (en) ------

You have exceptional talent with one type of performance. You gain a +1 circumstance bonus when performing comedy. If you are a master in Performance, this bonus increases to +2.

Example performances Buffoonery, joke telling, limericks

If it's unclear whether the specialty applies, the GM decides.
