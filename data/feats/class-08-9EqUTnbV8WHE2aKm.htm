Name: Dualistic Synergy Nom: Synergie duale État: changé État d'origine: libre ------ Description (en) ------

Prerequisites Halcyon Speaker Dedication

If your next action is to Cast a Spell from your spell slots, you gain a benefit. If the spell is arcane, you can attempt a skill check to @Compendium[pf2e.actionspf2e.1OagaWtBpVXExToo]{Recall Knowledge} with a +1 status bonus to the check after casting the spell, or a +2 status bonus if the spell is 7th level or higher. If the spell is primal, you gain a number of temporary Hit Points equal to the spell’s level for 1 round. If the spell is a halcyon spell, you gain both benefits.
