Name: Fade Away Nom: Disparition PrereqEN: Easily Dismissed PrereqFR: Facilement négligé État: libre ------ Description (en) ------

Your ability to blend into the background allows you to fade away entirely or appear innocuous even to magical effects. You gain @Compendium[pf2e.spells-srd.XXqE1eY3w3z6xJCB]{Invisibility} and @Compendium[pf2e.spells-srd.c2bTWBNO1BYX4Zfg]{Misdirection} as 2nd-level innate occult spells. You can target only yourself with invisibility, and you must be the primary target of misdirection. You can cast each spell once per day.
