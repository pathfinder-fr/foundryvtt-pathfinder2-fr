Name: Duelist's Challenge Nom: Défi du duelliste PrereqEN: Duelist Dedication PrereqFR: État: changé État d'origine: libre ------ Description (en) ------

Select one foe that you can see and proclaim a challenge. That foe is your dueling opponent until they are defeated, flee, or the encounter ends.

Any time you hit that enemy using a single one-handed melee weapon while your other hand or hands are free, you gain a circumstance bonus to the @Compendium[pf2e.actionspf2e.VjxZFuUXrCU94MWR]{Strike's} damage equal to the number of damage dice your weapon deals.

If you attack a creature other than your dueling opponent, you take a circumstance penalty to damage equal to the number of damage dice your weapon deals.
