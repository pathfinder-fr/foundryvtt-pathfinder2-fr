Name: Terrain Advantage Nom: Avantage du terrain État: libre ------ Description (en) ------

You can take advantage of the terrain to bypass foes’ defenses. Non-lizardfolk creatures in difficult terrain are @Compendium[pf2e.conditionspf2e.AJh5ex99aV6VTggg]{Flat-Footed} to you. If you have a swim Speed, non-lizardfolk creatures that are in water and lack a swim Speed are also flat-footed to you.
