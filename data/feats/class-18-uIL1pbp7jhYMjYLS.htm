Name: Parry And Riposte Nom: Parade et riposte État: libre ------ Description (en) ------

Prerequisites @Compendium[pf2e.classfeatures.Jtn7IugykXDlIoZq]{Opportune Riposte}

Your parries and finishers allow you to riposte with the slightest provocation.

You can use Opportune Riposte against an enemy who fails at a @Compendium[pf2e.actionspf2e.VjxZFuUXrCU94MWR]{Strike} against you (not just critically fails), provided you have both damaged that creature with a finisher on your last turn and currently have a circumstance bonus to AC from the parry weapon trait, @Compendium[pf2e.feats-srd.7sso1g3AAejpZ8ar]{Dueling Parry}, or @Compendium[pf2e.feats-srd.Y8LHfkzGyOhPlUou]{Twin Parry}.

Prérequis @Compendium[pf2e.classfeatures.Jtn7IugykXDlIoZq]{Riposte opportune}

Vos parades et vos bottes vous permettent de riposter à la moindre provocation.
