Name: Steady Spellcasting (Sorcerer) Nom: État: aucune ------ Description (en) ------

You are confident in your spellcasting technique, and you are more easily able to retain your concentration when you Cast a Spell. If a reaction would disrupt your spellcasting action, attempt a DC 15 fiat check. If you succeed, your action isn’t disrupted
