Name: Keep Pace (Game Hunter) Nom: Conserver le rythme (Chasseur de gibier) PrereqEN: |Game Hunter Dedication PrereqFR: Dévouement de Chasseur de gibier État: libre ------ Description (en) ------

When your hunted prey tries to bolt, you follow. You Stride up to your Speed, following your hunted prey and keeping it in reach throughout its movement until it stops moving or you've moved your full Speed.

You can use Keep Pace to @Compendium[pf2e.actionspf2e.H6v1VgowHaKHnVlG]{Burrow}, @Compendium[pf2e.actionspf2e.pprgrYQ1QnIDGZiy]{Climb}, @Compendium[pf2e.actionspf2e.cS9nfDRGD83bNU1p]{Fly}, or @Compendium[pf2e.actionspf2e.c8TGiZ48ygoSPofx]{Swim} instead of Stride if you have the appropriate movement type.
