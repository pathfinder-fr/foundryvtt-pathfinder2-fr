Name: Cloak Of Poison Nom: Cape de poison PrereqEN: PrereqFR: État: libre ------ Description (en) ------

Frequency once per day

You secrete a cloak of concentrated poison that harms any who dare attack you. For 1 minute, any creature that touches you or damages you with an unarmed attack or melee weapon without the reach trait takes [[/r 3d6 #poison]] poison damage.
