Frequency once per day

You secrete a cloak of concentrated poison that harms any who dare attack you. For 1 minute, any creature that touches you or damages you with an unarmed attack or melee weapon without the reach trait takes [[/r {3d6}[poison]]]{3d6 poison damage}.
