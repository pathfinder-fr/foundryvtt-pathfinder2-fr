Name: Ceremony of the Evened Hand (Branch) Nom: PrereqEN: PrereqFR: État: aucune ------ Description (en) ------

You have cultivated your exoskeleton’s form for close combat, using knots, whorls, and reinforced branches. When you select this feat, you gain a branch unarmed attack that deals 1d6 bludgeoning damage and has the backswing trait. This unarmed attacks is in the brawling weapon group and uses one of your hands.

Special You can select this feat twice, choosing the claws attack the second time you take it.
