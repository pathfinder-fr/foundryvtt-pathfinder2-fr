You can push larger foes around with your attack. You can use @Compendium[pf2e.feats-srd.19OLlTrxvEtDAOHc]{Aggressive Block} or @Compendium[pf2e.feats-srd.gt9pvIicabSOe6pB]{Brutish Shove} against a creature up to two sizes larger than you.

When a creature you @Compendium[pf2e.actionspf2e.7blmbDrQFNfdT731]{Shove} has to stop moving because it would hit an object, it takes damage equal to your Strength modifier (minimum 1). This happens regardless of how you Shoved the creature.
