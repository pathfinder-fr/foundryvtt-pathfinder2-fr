Whether you are climbing a ship's rigging, a jungle tree, or a clock tower, you have an uncanny knack for finding footholds and handholds where larger creatures can't. You gain a climb Speed of 10 feet. You can take the Legendary Climber feat even if you don't have the @Compendium[pf2e.feats-srd.P04Hw8E6WAWARKHP]{Quick Climb} feat, provided you meet its other prerequisites.
