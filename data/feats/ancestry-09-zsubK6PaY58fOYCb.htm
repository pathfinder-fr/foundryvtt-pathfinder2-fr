You have a @Compendium[pf2e.equipment-srd.nza9skYTNtJe2Wd3]{Keepsake} that grants you luck. You gain a +1 circumstance bonus to saves against spells and magical effects at all times, not just when you use @Compendium[pf2e.feats-srd.fYVFBnv9aVHv1UNg]{Leshy Superstition}. If you lose the keepsake, you lose the bonus until you designate a new keepsake, typically over the course of a week.
