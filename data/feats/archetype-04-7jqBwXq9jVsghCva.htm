Name: Gossip Lore Nom: Connaissance des ragots PrereqEN: Dandy Dedication PrereqFR: Dévouement de Dandy État: libre ------ Description (en) ------

Your time gossiping in fashionable salons keeps you informed on every topic.

You are trained in Gossip Lore, a special Lore skill that can be used only to @Compendium[pf2e.actionspf2e.1OagaWtBpVXExToo]{Recall Knowledge}, but on any topic. If you fail a check to Recall Knowledge with Gossip Lore, you get the effects of the @Compendium[pf2e.feats-srd.1Bt7uCW2WI4sM84P]{Dubious Knowledge} skill feat.

If you have legendary proficiency in Society, you gain expert proficiency in Gossip Lore, but you can't increase your proficiency rank in Gossip Lore by any other means.
