Name: Dragon Shape Nom: Morphologie draconique PrereqEN: Soaring Shape PrereqFR: État: changé État d'origine: officielle ------ Description (en) ------

You can take on the form of some of the world's most fearsome creatures.

Add the forms listed in @Compendium[pf2e.spells-srd.5c692cCcTDXjSEzk]{Dragon Form} to your wild shape list.
