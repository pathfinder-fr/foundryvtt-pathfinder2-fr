Name: Nocturnal Grippli Nom: Grippli nocturne PrereqEN: PrereqFR: État: libre ------ Benefits ---- Benefits: Avantage: Vous obtenez Vision dans le noir ------ Description (en) ------

You tend to do most of your hunting and work at night and have adapted to the requirements of nocturnal life. You gain @Compendium[pf2e.ancestryfeatures.HHVQDp61ehcpdiU8]{Darkvision}, allowing you to see in darkness and dim light just as well as you can in bright light. However, in darkness, you see in black and white only.

Special You can take this feat only at 1st level, and you can't retrain out of this feat or into this feat.
