Name: Cautious Curiosity Nom: Curiosité précautionneuse État: libre ------ Description (en) ------

Prerequisites at least one arcane or occult innate spell gained from a gnome heritage or gnome ancestry feat

You've learned a few magical techniques for getting yourself both into and out of trouble unnoticed. You gain @Compendium[pf2e.spells-srd.c2bTWBNO1BYX4Zfg]{Misdirection} and @Compendium[pf2e.spells-srd.gIdDLrbswTV3OBJy]{Silence} as 2nd-level arcane or occult innate spells.

The tradition of these spells must match the tradition you use for your gnome ancestry options. You can cast each spell once per day and can target only yourself.
