Name: Rough Rider Nom: Cavalier farouche PrereqEN: PrereqFR: État: officielle ------ Benefits ---- Benefits: You are especially good at riding traditional goblin mounts. Avantage: Vous êtes particulièrement doué pour monter les montures traditionnelles des gobelins. ------ Description (en) ------

You are especially good at riding traditional goblin mounts. You gain the @Compendium[pf2e.feats-srd.Rq5wkA8DtsmbzoGV]{Ride} feat, even if you don't meet the prerequisites. You gain a +1 circumstance bonus to Nature checks to use @Compendium[pf2e.actionspf2e.q9nbyIF0PEBqMtYe]{Command an Animal} on a goblin dog or wolf mount. You can always select a wolf as your animal companion, even if you would usually select an animal companion with the mount special ability, such as for a champion's steed ally.
