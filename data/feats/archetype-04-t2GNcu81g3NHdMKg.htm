You gain your mystery's initial revelation spell (@Compendium[pf2e.spells-srd.k2QrUk7jWMAWozMh]{Brain Drain}). Casting this spell progresses your @Compendium[pf2e.classfeatures.ibX2EhKkyUtbOHLj]{Oracular Curse}. Use the minor curse from your mystery, but when you progress it again, instead of your mystery's moderate curse effect, you become @Compendium[pf2e.conditionitems.AJh5ex99aV6VTggg]{Flat-Footed} in addition to the effects of the minor curse.

If you don't have one, you gain a focus pool of 1 Focus Point. You can @Compendium[pf2e.actionspf2e.OSefkMgojBLqmRDh]{Refocus} by reconciling the conflicting nature of your mystery, which also reduces your curse to minor.

Minor Curse of Lore

You take a -4 penalty to initiative, as trying to process a surge of information about what's happening around you slows your ability to respond to it.
