Avantage: Vous prenez feu pour mieux infliger des dégâts à vos adversaires et à vous-même. ------ Description (en) ------

You've spent enough time on fire that you know how to use it to your advantage. You can light yourself thoroughly on fire with a held torch, a bottle of alchemist's fire, or a similar incendiary, dealing yourself [[/r {1d6}[persistent,fire]]] @Compendium[pf2e.conditionitems.lDVqvLKA6eF3Df60]{Persistent Fire Damage}.

As long as you are suffering persistent fire damage, all your melee attacks against adjacent creatures deal an additional 1 fire damage per weapon damage die. Any creature that successfully Grapples, Shoves, or Trips you takes [[/r {1d6}[fire]]]{1d6 Fire Damage}; if it uses a weapon for that action, the weapon takes the damage instead.

You must still attempt the flat check to remove the Persistent Fire Damage each round, as normal.
