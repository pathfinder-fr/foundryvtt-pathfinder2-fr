Prerequisites Daring Act

You make quick use of an opening from your daring stunts. At the end of a successful Daring Act, you can make a melee Strike against the target or attempt to @Compendium[pf2e.actionspf2e.Dt6B1slsBy8ipJu9]{Disarm} the target. The target is @Compendium[pf2e.conditionspf2e.AJh5ex99aV6VTggg]{Flat-Footed} against the Strike you make during Daring Act as well as the next melee attack you attempt against it before the end of your turn.
