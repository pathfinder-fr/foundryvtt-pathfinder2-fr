Name: Friendly Toss Nom: Lancer amical État: changé État d'origine: libre ------ Description (en) ------

Requirements You are adjacent to an ally and have one or
more hands free.

You toss your friends around the battlefield. Pick up an adjacent ally of your size or smaller and throw them to an unoccupied space you can see within 30 feet. Their movement doesn't trigger reactions.

Your ally ends this movement on their feet and doesn't take damage from the fall. If your ally ends this movement within melee reach of at least one enemy, they can make a melee @Compendium[pf2e.actionspf2e.VjxZFuUXrCU94MWR]{Strike} against an enemy within their reach as a reaction.

Votre allié termine ce déplacement sur ses pieds et ne subit pas de dégâts de la chute. Si votre allié termine son mouvement à portée d'au moins un ennemi, il peut faire une @Compendium[pf2e.actionspf2e.VjxZFuUXrCU94MWR]{Frappe} de mêlée contre un ennemi dans cette portée en réaction.
