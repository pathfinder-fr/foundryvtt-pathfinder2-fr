You unleash your breath weapon without spending focus. This has the effects of your Dragon Breath sorcerer bloodline spell, though it deals [[/r 9d6]] damage instead of its usual damage, and you don't have to cast it or spend a Focus Point. You can't use Disciple's Breath again for [[/r 1d4]] rounds.
