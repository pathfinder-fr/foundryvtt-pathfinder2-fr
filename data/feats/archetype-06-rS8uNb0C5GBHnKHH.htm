You are even more skilled in the weapons of your people. You gain the critical specialization effects of the @Compendium[pf2e.equipment-srd.War0uyLBx1jA0Ge7]{Battle Axe}, @Compendium[pf2e.equipment-srd.LGgvev6AV0So8tP9]{Hatchet}, @Compendium[pf2e.equipment-srd.LJdbVTOZog39EEbi]{Longsword}, and @Compendium[pf2e.equipment-srd.7tKkkF8eZ4iCLJtp]{Shortsword}.

Whenever you gain a class feature that grants you expert or greater proficiency in certain weapons, you also gain that proficiency rank in the above four weapons.
