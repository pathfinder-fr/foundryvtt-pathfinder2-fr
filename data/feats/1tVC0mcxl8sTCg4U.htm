Name: Orc Weapon Familiarity Nom: Familiarité avec les armes orques État: officielle ------ Description (en) ------

In combat, you favor the brutal weapons that are traditional for your orc ancestors. You are trained with the falchion and greataxe. In addition, you gain access to all uncommon orc weapons. For you, martial orc weapons are simple weapons, and advanced orc weapons are martial weapons
