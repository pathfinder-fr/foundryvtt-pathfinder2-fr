Name: Cobra Stance Nom: Posture du Cobra État: libre ------ Description (en) ------

You enter a tight stance, coiled up like a lashing cobra with your hands poised as venomous fangs.

While in this stance, the only @Compendium[pf2e.actionspf2e.VjxZFuUXrCU94MWR]{Strikes} you can make are cobra fang unarmed attacks. These deal [[/r 1d4 #poison]] poison damage; are in the brawling group; and have the agile, deadly d10, finesse, nonlethal, poison, and unarmed traits.

While in Cobra Stance, you gain a +1 circumstance bonus to Fortitude saves and your Fortitude DC, and you gain poison resistance equal to half your level.
