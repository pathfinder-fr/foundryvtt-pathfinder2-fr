Name: Second Shield Nom: Second bouclier PrereqEN: Viking Dedication PrereqFR: Dévouement de Viking État: libre ------ Description (en) ------

Trigger Your @Compendium[pf2e.feats-srd.jM72TjJ965jocBV8]{Shield Block} causes your shield to break or be destroyed.

You're used to your shield breaking in the middle of battle, and you're prepared to use a backup or any convenient nearby object to defend yourself.

You can @Compendium[pf2e.actionspf2e.pvQ5rY2zrtPI614F]{Interact} to draw a shield on your person or an unattended shield within your reach. If there is an object within your reach that could serve as an improvised shield-for example, a table or chair-you can Interact to draw it with this feat. The GM determines if something can be used as an improvised shield.

Your new shield isn't raised until you use the @Compendium[pf2e.actionspf2e.xjGwis0uaC2305pm]{Raise a Shield} action, as normal.
