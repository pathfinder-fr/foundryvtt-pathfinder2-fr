Name: Quick Identification Nom: Identification rapide PrereqEN: trained in Arcana, Nature, Occultism, or Religion PrereqFR: qualifié en Arcanes, Nature, Occultisme ou Religion État: officielle ------ Benefits and spoiler ---- Benefits: Identify Magic in 1 minute or less. Avantage: Vous identifiez la magie en 1 minute ou moins ------ Description (en) ------

You can Identify Magic swiftly. You take only 1 minute when using Identify Magic to determine the properties of an item, ongoing effect, or location, rather than 10 minutes. If you're a master, it takes a 3-action activity, and if you're legendary, it takes 1 action.
