Name: Slag May Nom: Vierge crasseuse PrereqEN: PrereqFR: État: libre ------ Benefits ---- Benefits: Your mother was an annis hag , known for physical prowess, and you have one violet or steel-gray eye. Avantage: Vous possédez une attaque de griffes en fer froid. ------ Description (en) ------

Your mother was an annis hag, known for physical prowess, and you have one violet or steel-gray eye. You have thick, sturdy claws made of cold iron that grow naturally from your body. You gain a claw unarmed attack that deals [[/r {1d6}[slashing]]]{1d6 slashing damage}. Your claws are in the brawling group, have the unarmed and grapple traits, and are cold iron.

------ Description (fr) ------

Vous obtenez d'épaisses et robustes griffes faites de fer froid qui grandissent naturellement à partir de votre corps.
