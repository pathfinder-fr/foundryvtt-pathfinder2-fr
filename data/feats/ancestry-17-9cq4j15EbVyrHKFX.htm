Name: Forge-Blessed Shot Nom: Tir béni par la forge PrereqEN: PrereqFR: État: libre ------ Description (en) ------

Access You are from Dongun Hold or Alkenstar.

Frequency once per day

Trigger You attempt a ranged Strike.

You intone a prayer to the dwarven deities Torag or Angradd, willing a higher power to guide your aim in the heat of battle. On your next Strike with a ranged weapon before the end of your turn, you gain a +2 circumstance bonus to your attack roll and ignore your target's @Compendium[pf2e.conditionitems.DmAIPqOBomZ7H95W]{Concealed} condition, as well as your target's lesser, standard, and greater cover.
