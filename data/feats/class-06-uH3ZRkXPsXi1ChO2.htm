Your shield ally is more than just a spirit of protection-it's a conduit for deadly divine magic.

When you use your shield ally to @Compendium[pf2e.feats-srd.jM72TjJ965jocBV8]{Shield Block} a melee unarmed attack or a melee weapon @Compendium[pf2e.actionspf2e.VjxZFuUXrCU94MWR]{Strike} from an adjacent creature, the attacker takes 1d6 evil or negative damage (your choice).
