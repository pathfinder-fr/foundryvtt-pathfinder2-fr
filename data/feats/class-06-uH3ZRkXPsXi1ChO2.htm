When you use your shield ally to @Compendium[pf2e.feats-srd.jM72TjJ965jocBV8]{Shield Block} a melee unarmed attack or a melee weapon Strike from an adjacent creature, the attacker takes [[/r 1d6 #evil]] evil or negative damage (your choice).
