Whenever you succeed at an Athletics check to Climb a rope or an Acrobatics check to @Compendium[pf2e.actionspf2e.M76ycLAqHoAgbcej]{Balance} on a rope, you get a critical success instead. You aren’t @Compendium[pf2e.conditionspf2e.AJh5ex99aV6VTggg]{Flat-Footed} while Climbing or Balancing on a rope.
