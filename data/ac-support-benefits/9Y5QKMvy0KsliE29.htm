Name: Moth Support Benefit Nom: Avantage de soutien du papillon de nuit État: libre ------ Description (en) ------

Your moth releases a shower of dust to reveal your foes. Until the start of your next turn, if you damage a @Compendium[pf2e.conditionitems.Concealed]{Concealed} or @Compendium[pf2e.conditionitems.Hidden]{Hidden} creature in your moth's reach with a Strike, that creature requires only a Flat Check to target it if it's concealed, or Flat Check if it's hidden. The reduced DC lasts until the end of your next turn.
