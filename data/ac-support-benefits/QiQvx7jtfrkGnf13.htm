Name: Pangolin Support Benefit Nom: Avantage de soutien du pangolin État: libre ------ Description (en) ------

Your pangolin tears at your enemies with its serrated plates. Until the start of your next turn, your Strikes that damage a creature in your pangolin's reach also deal [[/r {1d6}[persistent,bleed]]]{1d6} @Compendium[pf2e.conditionitems.Persistent Damage]{Persistent bleed Damage}. If your pangolin is nimble or savage, the persistent bleed damage increases to [[/r {2d6}[persistent,bleed]]]{2d6}.
