Name: Hyena Support Benefit Nom: Avantage de soutien de la hyène État: libre ------ Description (en) ------

The hyena mockingly yowls at your enemies and bites at them when you create an opening. Until the start of your next turn, each time you hit a creature in the hyena's reach with a Strike, the creature becomes @Compendium[pf2e.conditionitems.Frightened]{Frightened 1}.
