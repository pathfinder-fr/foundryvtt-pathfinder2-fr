Name: Groetus Nom: Groetus État: changé État d'origine: libre ------ Description (en) ------

Ancient beyond mortal reckoning, Groetus is an entity who cannot be easily understood. He hangs in the sky above the Boneyard, a skull-faced moon constantly observing the passage of the souls below. Events can cause him to draw ominously closer to @Compendium[pf2e.deities.QZD0u1jxwz0kj8uI]{Pharasma}'s Spire, or to retreat back to a safer distance, with little obvious rhyme or reason for these actions. He evinces little regard for anything but his singular aim: the dissolution of the universe.

Edicts preach of the upcoming end times, destroy that which has outlived its usefulness, put the suffering out of their misery

Anathema artificially extend something's existence or lifespan, spread hope

Areas of Concern apocalypse, empty places, oblivion, ruins
