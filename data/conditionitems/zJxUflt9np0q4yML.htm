While invisible, you can't be seen. You're @Compendium[pf2e.conditionitems.VRSef5y1LmL2Hkjf]{Undetected} to everyone. Creatures can @Compendium[pf2e.actionspf2e.BlAOM2X92SI6HMtJ]{Seek} to attempt to detect you; if a creature succeeds at its Perception check against your Stealth DC, you become @Compendium[pf2e.conditionitems.iU0fEDdBp3rXpTMC]{Hidden} to that creature until you @Compendium[pf2e.actionspf2e.VMozDqMMuK5kpoX4]{Sneak} to become undetected again.
