Name: Grabbed Nom: Agrippé/empoigné État: libre ------ Description (en) ------

You’re held in place by another creature, giving you the @Compendium[pf2e.conditionitems.Flat-Footed] and @Compendium[pf2e.conditionitems.Immobilized] conditions. If you attempt a manipulate action while grabbed, you must succeed at a DC 5 flat check or it is lost; roll the check after spending the action, but before any effects are applied.
