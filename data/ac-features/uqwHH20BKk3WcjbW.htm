Your eidolon expands your primal magic with enchantment and illusion magic, allowing both of you to wield the power of fey charm and glamour. Your eidolon gains the @Compendium[pf2e.feats-srd.WfbmFsRxbVyzMmCz]{Magical Understudy} summoner feat, despite not meeting the prerequisite level, and it can choose fey gift cantrips in addition to primal cantrips.
