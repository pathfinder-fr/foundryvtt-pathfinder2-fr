Name: Blasphemous Decree Nom: Décret blasphématoire État: libre ------ Description (en) ------

Your demon utters indescribable words writhing with its associated sin. It can cast an evil @Compendium[pf2e.spells-srd.sX2o0HH4RjJDAZ8C]{Divine Decree} once per day as a 9th-level spell. You are unaffected by this decree, even if you aren't evil. At 19th level, this becomes a 10th-level divine decree instead.
