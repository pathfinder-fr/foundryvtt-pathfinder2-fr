Name: (Skeleton) Explosive Death Nom: (Squelette) Mort explosive État: officielle ------ Description (en) ------

When the skeleton is destroyed, its bones shatter and explode as the necromantic energy holding it together is released.

Adjacent creatures take [[/r {1d6}[slashing]]]{1d6 slashing damage} per 2 levels (minimum 1d6) with a basic Reflex save.
