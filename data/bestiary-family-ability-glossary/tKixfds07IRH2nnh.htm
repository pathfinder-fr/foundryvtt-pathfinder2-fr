Name: (Vampire, Vrykolakas Master) Children of the Night Nom: (Vampire, maître vrykolakas) Enfants de la nuit État: officielle ------ Description (en) ------

The presence of a vrykolakas master inspires savage creatures to crawl forth to do its bidding, including @Compendium[pf2e.pathfinder-bestiary.sPClc6y3dT3XZupv]{Rat Swarms}, @Compendium[pf2e.pathfinder-bestiary.Sft7n3LMmnTxhhYn]{Wargs}, @Compendium[pf2e.pathfinder-bestiary.bLMoqt9xqTZKnjxr]{Werewolves}, and similar creatures.
