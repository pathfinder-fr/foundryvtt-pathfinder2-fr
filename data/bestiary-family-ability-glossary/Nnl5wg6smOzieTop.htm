Name: (Vampire, True) Turn to Mist Nom: (Vampire, véritable) Se changer en brume État: officielle ------ Description (en) ------

The vampire turns into a cloud of vapor, as the @Compendium[pf2e.spells-srd.V8wXOsoejQhe6CyG]{Gaseous Form} spell, or back to its normal form. The vampire loses @Compendium[pf2e.bestiary-ability-glossary-srd.fJSNOw4zHGbIm4bZ]{Fast Healing} while in gaseous form.

The vampire can remain in this form indefinitely.
