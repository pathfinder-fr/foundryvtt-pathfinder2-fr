Name: (Lich) Void Shroud Nom: (Liche) Linceul de néant État: officielle ------ Description (en) ------

@Template[type:emanation|distance:30]{30 feet} @Compendium[pf2e.bestiary-ability-glossary-srd.v61oEQaDdcRpaZ9X]{Aura}

The lich is surrounded by an aura of death, drawing forth souls to be consumed by the lich's constant hunger.

Living creatures in the emanation take a -2 status penalty to saves against fear and death effects.
