Name: (Skeleton) Bone Powder Nom: (Squelette) Poudre d'os État: libre ------ Description (en) ------

When the skeleton takes physical damage from a critical hit, one of its bones is pulverized into a fine powder.

All creatures in a 5-foot emanation that breathe take [[/r {1d6}[persistent,poison]]] @Compendium[pf2e.conditionitems.lDVqvLKA6eF3Df60]{Persistent Poison Damage} (plus an additional 1d6 for every 6 levels the skeleton has).
