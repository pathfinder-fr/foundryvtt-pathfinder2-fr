Effect The vetalarana seizes a creature and consumes its memories. A victim that becomes stupefied 5 in this way is rendered comatose, becoming permanently @Compendium[pf2e.conditionitems.fBnFDH2MTzgFijKf]{Unconscious}. As long as the vetalarana lives, a comatose victim can't regain consciousness, and its stupefied condition doesn't decrease.
