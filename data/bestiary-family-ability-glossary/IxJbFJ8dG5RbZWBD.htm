Name: (Graveknight) Devastating Blast Nom: (Chevalier sépulcre) Déflagration dévastatrice État: officielle ------ Description (en) ------

The graveknight unleashes a @Template[type:cone|distance:30] of energy. Creatures in the area take [[/r {1d12}[damageType]]]{1d12 damageType damage}, plus an additional 1d12 damage for every two levels the graveknight has (@Check[type:reflex|dc:|basic:true] save).

The graveknight can use this ability once every [[/br 1d4 #Recharge Devastating Blast]]{1d4 rounds}.

This energy damage is of the same type as that of its ruinous weapons; Devastating Blast gains the associated energy trait.

------ Description (fr) ------

Le chevalier sépulcre projette un @Template[type:cone|distance:30]{cône d’énergie de 9 mètres}.
