Success The ravener tears off a small chunk of the creature's soul. Failure As success, but the creature's soul is ravaged. The creature is @Compendium[pf2e.conditionitems.4D2KBtexWXa6oUMR]{Drained 3} and the ravener adds a number of Hit Points to their soul ward equal to the creature's level.

The victim can't be restored to life as long as the ravener exists except via a 10th-level effect such as @Compendium[pf2e.spells-srd.YfJTXyVGzLhM6V8U]{Miracle} or @Compendium[pf2e.spells-srd.6dDtGIUerazSHIOu]{Wish}, and the ravener adds a number of Hit Points to their soul ward equal to twice the creature's level.
