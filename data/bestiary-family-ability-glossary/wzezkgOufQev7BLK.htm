The ghost possesses and transforms an adjacent corpse (this requires 2 actions if the ghost is possessing its own corpse; otherwise, it takes an hour).

The possessed corpse uses the statistics of a non-incorporeal undead 2 levels lower than the ghost, and the ghost possesses it as if using the @Compendium[pf2e.spells-srd.wU6hNzK8Yfqdmc8m]{Possession} spell. The specific undead is determined by the nature of the ghost and the corpse-@Compendium[pf2e.pathfinder-bestiary.LHHgGSs0ELCR4CYK]{Ghouls}, @Compendium[pf2e.pathfinder-bestiary.ZPjQkKVMi3xoPcU0]{Wights}, and @Compendium[pf2e.pathfinder-bestiary.0SJqmk4ItwL31Rg9]{Dullahans} are all common, but almost any undead is possible.

If the ghost ceases to possess the corpse, the corporeal undead is immediately destroyed.
