Name: Doomed Nom: État: aucune ------ Description (en) ------

A powerful force has gripped your soul, calling you closer to death. Doomed always includes a value. The @Compendium[pf2e.conditionspf2e.yZRUzMqrMmfLu0V1]{Dying} value at which you die is reduced by your doomed value. If your maximum @Compendium[pf2e.conditionspf2e.yZRUzMqrMmfLu0V1]{Dying} value is reduced to 0, you instantly die. When you die, you’re no longer doomed.

Your doomed value decreases by 1 each time you get a full night’s rest.
