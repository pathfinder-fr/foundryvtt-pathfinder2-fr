Name: Effect: Blade Ally Axiomatic Rune Nom: Effet : Allié lame rune Axiomatique État: libre ------ Description (en) ------

Complex and symmetrical, an axiomatic rune imbues a weapon with lawful energy. A weapon with this rune deals an additional 1d6 lawful damage against chaotic targets. If you are chaotic, you are @Compendium[pf2e.conditionitems.MIRkyAjyBeXivMa7]{Enfeebled 2} while carrying or wielding this weapon.

When you critically succeed at an attack roll with this weapon against a chaotic creature, instead of rolling, count each weapon damage die as average damage rounded up (3 for d4, 4 for d6, 5 for d8, 6 for d10, 7 for d12).

Craft Requirements You are lawful.
