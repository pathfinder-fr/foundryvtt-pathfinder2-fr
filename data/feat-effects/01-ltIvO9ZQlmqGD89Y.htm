Name: Effect: Hunter's Edge, Outwit Nom: Effet : Spécialité du chasseur, Ruse État: libre ------ Description (en) ------

Granted when a ranger shares their @Compendium[pf2e.classfeatures.NBHyoTrI8q62uDsU]{Outwit} edge with you.

You gain a +2 circumstance bonus to Deception checks, Intimidation checks, Stealth checks, and any checks to Recall Knowledge about the prey, and a +1 circumstance bonus to AC against your prey's attacks.
