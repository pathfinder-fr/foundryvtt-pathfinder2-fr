Name: Stance: Everstand Stance Nom: Posture : Posture toujours en position État: libre ------ Description (en) ------

When in this stance, you wield the shield with both hands. When wielding a shield this way, increase the weapon damage die of the shield's boss or spikes by one step, and increase the shield's Hardness by 2 when using the @Compendium[pf2e.feats-srd.jM72TjJ965jocBV8]{Shield Block} reaction.
