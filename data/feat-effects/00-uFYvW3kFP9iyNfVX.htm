You adopt the stance of clinging shadows. You can make shadow grasp Strikes. These deal 1d4 negative damage; are in the brawling group; and have the agile, grapple, reach, and unarmed traits.

While in the clinging shadows stance, you gain a +2 circumstance bonus to Athletics checks to @Compendium[pf2e.actionspf2e.PMbdMWc2QroouFGD]{Grapple}, and to the DC for creatures to @Compendium[pf2e.actionspf2e.SkZAQRkLLkmBQNB9]{Escape} from you.
